MMI Daily Iron Ore Index Report July 9 2026

Hellenic Shipping News  |  09 July 2026  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/mmi-daily-iron-ore-index-report-july-9-2026

Today, the DCE iron ore futures trended weak today, with contract 12609 closing at 745.5 yuan/mt, up 0.27% from the previous trading day.Port spot prices ranged from -2 to +3 yuan/mt compared with the previous trading day. Trader activity was moderate, steel mills made few inquiries, and of now, spot transaction volumes were low.

According to the SMM survey, total inventory at the 10 main ports stood at 108.14 million mt, down 1.32 million mt MoM. Among them, fines destocking slowed, lump ore destocking was good, and pellet inventory was at an extremely low level. In the short term, pellet and lump premiums are expected to remain firm, while fines prices will find it difficult to break higher as inventory accumulates. Demand side, steel mills’ purchase willingness showed a weakening trend MoM. In the short term, iron ore prices are expected to continue fluctuating with limited upside and resistance to downward pressure on prices.
