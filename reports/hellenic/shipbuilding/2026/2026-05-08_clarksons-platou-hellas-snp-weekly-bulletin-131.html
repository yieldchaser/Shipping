CLARKSONS PLATOU HELLAS – SnP WEEKLY BULLETIN

Hellenic Shipping News  |  08 May 2026  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/clarksons-platou-hellas-snp-weekly-bulletin-131
This week, the Dry Bulk sector has been at the forefront of market attention. The already firm sentiment strengthened further, supported by improving freight fundamentals,as reflected in the overall week-on-week increase of the BDI. Numerous enquiries were recorded across all vessel types, with particular focus on 15–20-year-old Panamax andKamsarmax tonnage built in Japan, while demand for eco bulkers remains firm amid limited availability of prompt candidates. Activity in the tanker segment has maintained a more subdued tone, although we are aware of a fair number of ongoing negotiations.Dry CargoIn the Post-Panamax segment, after inviting offers earlier this week, theCORONA BRAVE(ABT 88K DWT, 2006, IMABARI) was acquired by Chinese interests for USD 12.9m.In the Kamsarmax sector, theAVALON (ABT 81K DWT, 2011, SUNGDONG) is understood committed at region USD 18m,whilst the older MANDY MORN (ABT 82K DWT, 2008, TSUNEISHI ZOUSHAN) was reported sold to Chinese interests at levels circa USD 14m.On the Panamax segment, thePRABHU YUVIKA (ABT 76K DWT, 2004, SUMITOMO) was sold at mid-high USD 9mMarket rumors indicate that a 2017 Chinese Ultramax is being negotiated in region USD 26/27m.Moving to the Supramax size, theVALIANT WAVE (ABT 53K DWT, 2005, IMABARI) was committed at USD 10.5m.After inviting offers this week, we understand theSUMAQ QUEEN (ABT 51K DWT, 2017, IMABARI)has seen interest from more than 5 buyers at midUSD 25m levels and is currently under negotiations. We are hearing as well of a 2008 Mitsui sold for USD 14m basis very prompt delivery.
Lastly, the HandysizeINTERLINK FORTUITY (ABT 38K DWT, 2017, TAIZHOU KOUAN, ICE CLASS 1C) was acquired by German interests for USD 21.5m.

Tanker

On the Tanker side, although we are noticing various negotiationsdeveloping mainly across 15 to 20 year-old tonnage, there are no new tanker sales that we can report.(All details given in good faith and for information only, without guarantee/prejudice)
