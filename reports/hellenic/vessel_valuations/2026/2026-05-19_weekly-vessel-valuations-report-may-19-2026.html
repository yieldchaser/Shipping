Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, May 19 2026

Hellenic Shipping News  |  19 May 2026  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-may-19-2026

Bulkers:S&P activity remained active across the major segments this week, with Ultramax tonnage drawing particular interest from Greek and Chinese buyers.Buyers appeared willing to absorb near-term survey costs, with several vessels carrying upcoming SS/DD dates still achieving firm pricing, pointing to continued confidence in the segment’s earnings outlook. Values firmed across the Bulker segment in response

Kamsarmax Mandy Morn (82,600 DWT, 2008, Tsuneishi Zhoushan) sold by Ningbo Baoneng Shipping DD Due for USD 14 mil, VV Value USD 14.8 mil.

Supramax Dominator (63,700 DWT, 2021, Shin Kasado Dock) sold to Greek buyers SS/DD Due for USD 38 mil, VV Value USD 37.6 mil

Supramax Planet Team (53,400 DWT, 2009, Zhejiang Shipbuilding) sold by Transbulk Denizcilik AS for USD 10.5 mil, VV Value USD 10.2 mil.

Handysize Interlink Folly (40,100 DWT, 2017, Taizhou Kouan) sold to Conti Group KG for USD 21.7 mil, VV Value USD 21.8 mil.

Tankers:Secondhand values are softening in line with the earnings trend across most sectors, with newbuild and resale price seeing the largest decline. S&P activity this week was largely concentrated in the MR segment

VLCC Hull 20243009 (306,000 DWT, Jul 2026, Hengli Shipbuilding) sold to Trafigura Beheer BV for USD 163 mil, VV Value USD 151.1 mil

MR2 (Chemical/Product) Royal Jasmine (53,200 DWT, Jul 2008, Guangzhou Shipyard International Company Ltd.) sold DD Due to Unknown Turkish buyers for USD 20.8 mil, VV Value USD 20.29 mil

MR2 (Chemical/Product) High Tide (51,800 DWT, Apr 2012, Hyundai Mipo) sold to Precious Shipping for USD 28.45 mil, VV Value USD 29.52 mil

MR2 (Chemical/Product) Hull 5013 & Hull 5014 (50,000 DWT, 2026/2027, Zhoushan Changhong International) sold to Unknown Danish buyers for USD 100 mil in an en bloc deal, VV Value USD 98.15 mil

Containers:Stability reigns across the container values this week

Sub Panamax Container AS Patria (2,526 TEU, Jun 2006, STX Offshore) and Handy Container AS Sicilia (1,794 TEU, Apr 2008, Taizhou Kouan) sold en bloc to Chinese buyers for USD 26.5 mil and USD 18.5 mil respectively, VV Value USD 26.2 mil and USD 18.1 mil.

Handy Container Olivia (1,715 TEU, Jan 2013, Guangzhou Wenchong) sold to Global Feeder Shipping for USD 25 mil, VV Value USD 25.86 mil.

Handy Container Eramus Hope (1,347 TEU, Sep 2008, Jiangsu Yangzijiang) sold to an undisclosed buyer for USD 17.9 mil, VV Value USD 17.16 mil.

Feedermax KR Celebes (728 TEU, Nov 2002, Dae Sun) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 6.25 mil, VV Value USD 6.5 mil.
