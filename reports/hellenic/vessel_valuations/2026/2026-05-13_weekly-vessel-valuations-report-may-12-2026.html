Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, May 12 2026

Hellenic Shipping News  |  13 May 2026  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-may-12-2026

Bulkers:The secondhand Bulker market remained active this week. Modest value increases occurred across Bulker segments,as sales revealed more evidence of strong pricing in the sector

Capesize Bulk Joyance (175,900 DWT, 2012, Jinhai) sold by HNA Technology Investments Holdings for USD 33 mil, VV Value USD 30.5 mil.

Panamaxes Seacon Nola (85,600 DWT, 2023, GSW) and Seacon Hamburg (85,500 DWT, 2023, GSW) sold to Asyad Shipping, the former DD passed and latter DD Due, for USD 72.7 mil, VV Value USD 71.6 mil.

Supramax Bulk Colombia (57,900 DWT, 2011, Tsuneishi Cebu) sold by Neptune Maritime Leasing for USD 16.7 mil, VV Value USD 17.1 mil.

Supramax Xing Ning He (53,200 DWT, 2009, Zhejiang Shipbuilding) sold by Fortune Ocean Shipping for USD 11 mil, VV Value USD 11.2 mil.

Handysize Brave Star (38,200 DWT, 2019, Kanda) sold by Sugahara Kisen for USD 28.5, VV Value USD 28.1 mil

Tankers:The secondhand market was active in the Handy Tanker sector this week, where lower sale prices and softening earnings have shifted values down for older tonnage. In contrast, older Suezmax values continued their upward trajectory

LR2 Torm Ganga (119,500 DWT, Nov 2010, Hyundai Samho HI) sold by Torm for USD 53.5 mil, VV Value USD 55.71 mil

MR2 (Chemical/Product) Cers (51,400 DWT, Jun 2006, SLS) sold SS/DD Due by China Development Bank Financial Leasing for USD 14.5 mil, VV Value USD 15.59 mil

MR2 (Chemical/Product) Cordoba (46,800 DWT, Nov 2007, HSG Sungdong Shipbuilding) sold DD Passed by Armina Shipping for USD 17 mil, VV Value USD 17.8 mil

MR1 (Chemical/Product) Wonder Mimosa (36,700 DWT, Mar 2006, Hyundai Mipo) sold to Unknown Greek buyers for USD 12.8 mil, VV Value USD 13.76 mil

Containers:Container values remain stable this week, the only exception being older Feedermaxes which continue to come down

Handy Container Hansa Augsburg (1,740 TEU, Apr 2008, Guangzhou Wenchong) bought by Global Feeder Shipping for USD 20 mil, VV Value USD 20 mil
