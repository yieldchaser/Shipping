Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, June 02 2026

Hellenic Shipping News  |  02 June 2026  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-june-02-2026

Bulkers:Bulker S&P activity was solid, with 16 vessels changing hands across all segments.Broader market sentiment remains underpinned by cautious optimism following the US-China tariff truce, which has improved near-term trade flow expectations for dry bulk commodities. Strong pricing was observed across vintage tonnage, leading to our values for older vessels firming.

Panamax Kamares (74,400 DWT, 2004, Hudong) sold to Chinese for USD 8.6 mil, VV Value USD 7.9 mil.

Supramax Elpida GR (52,600 DWT, 2003, Kanasashi) sold by Windforce Maritime Enterprises for USD 8.3 mil, VV Value USD 8.5 mil

Supramax West Bay (52,200 DWT, 2004, Tsuneishi Cebu) sold to Chinese for USD 9.8 mil, VV Value USD 9.2 mil.

Handysize Captain Ravi (37,900 DWT, 2009, Jiangsu Eastern) sold by MPP Carriers USD 10 mil, VV Value USD 10.1 mil.

Tankers:The secondhand Tanker market remained subdued with downward pressure on value movements. VLCC transactions were particularly subdued, totalling just USD 664.3 mil across nine vessels over the past three months. Torm was the standout buyer, acquiring 10 vessels for approximately USD 420.0 mil, primarily from Singaporean sellers. Scorpio Tankers remained the dominant seller, disposing of 12 vessels totalling USD 543.6 mil.

VLCC Abie (303,000 DWT, Nov 2002, Samsung) sold DD due for USD 41 mil, VV value USD 45.41 mil

MR1 VS Spirit (34,700 DWR, Mar 2007, Dalian Shipbuilding) sold by Valleoby Shipping for USD 14 mil, VV value USD 14.28 mil

Containers:Sales in the Handysize sector have supported firming across all ages.

Handy Container Log in Pantanal (1,674 TEU, Jan 2007, Aker Ostsee) sold in an internal deal for USD 17.50 mil between Log In Logistica and MSC, VV value USD 18.51 mil

Handy Container Nordtiger (1,730 TEU, Jul 2014, Zhejiang Ouhua) sold for USD 29 mil, VV value USD 25.65 mil
