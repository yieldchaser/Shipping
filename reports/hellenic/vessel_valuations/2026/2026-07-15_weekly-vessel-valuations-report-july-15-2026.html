Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, July 15 2026

Hellenic Shipping News  |  15 July 2026  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-july-15-2026

Bulkers:Value movements were subdued this week, as achieved prices held broadly steady,with the exception of a slight correction in mid-age Supramax vessels.

Kamsarmax Polynesia Queen (82,200 DWT, 2012, Tsuneishi Zhoushan) sold by Laus Ocean Shipping for USD 21 mil, VV Value USD 21.1 mil.

Ultramax Livita (63,500 DWT, 2017, Shin Kasado Dock) sold by Ugland Bulk Shipping AS for USD 30.5 mil, VV Value USD 30.9 mil.

Supramax Sea Abigail (53,300 DWT, 2003, Kanasashi) sold by Sea Castle Ship Management to UAE buyers for USD 8.8 mil, VV Value USD 8.7 mil.

Handy HTK Lucky (28,500 DWT, 2003, Imabari) sold by HTK Shipping & Import Export to Chinese buyers for USD 6 mil, VV Value USD 5.6 mil.

Tankers:The Tanker S&P market was quiet this week, with mid-age MR values continuing to correct as a result of previous sales.

No notable sales this week.

Containers:Feedermax values continued to move this week with two notable sales, seemingly the only segment where charter-free tonnage is still available.

Feedermax Alexander B (1,221 TEU, 2006, Detlef Hegemann Rolandwerft) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 11.5 mil, VV Value USD 11 mil.

Feedermax A Goryu (1,096 TEU, 2023, Kyokuyo) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 26.5 mil, VV Value USD 27 mil.
