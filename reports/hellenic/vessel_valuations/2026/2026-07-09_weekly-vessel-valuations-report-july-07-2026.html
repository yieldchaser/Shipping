Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, July 07 2026

Hellenic Shipping News  |  09 July 2026  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-july-07-2026

Bulkers:Midage Supramax values dipped in response to soft pricing for the Livita,while older tonnage firmed in response to the sale of the VW Trust to Chinese buyers.

Kamsarmax AC Youth (82,600 DWT, 2007, Tadotsu Tsuneishi) sold by Glorious Youth Shipping for USD 15 mil, VV Value USD 14.3 mil.

Ultramax Livita (63,500 DWT, 2017, Shin Kasado Dock) sold to Greek buyers for USD 30.5 mil, VV Value USD 30.6 mil.

Supramax VW Trust (52,500 DWT, 2002, Tadotsu Tsuneishi) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 8 mil, VV Value USD 7.8 mil.

Handy Astro Propus (38,300 DWT, 2014, Imabari) sold to Devbulk Shipping for USD 19 mil, VV Value USD 19.1 mil.

Tankers:VV S&P values were broadly stable on a one-week basis, with MR values ticking up modestly across the board. Sentiment continues to be supported by easing Middle East tensions and continued growth in U.S. crude exports

VLCC Eclat (299,000 DWT, 2004, Universal) sold to All Win Ship Management for USD 50 mil, VV Value USD 51.78 mil.

LR2 Jag Lokesh (105,900 DWT, 2009, Hyundai Heavy Ind Ulsan Shipyard) sold by Great Eastern Shipping to Y-Knot Ship Management for USD 44.10 mil, VV Value USD 48.60 mil.

MR2 Dylan (50,300 DWT, 2009, Guangzhou Shipyard International) sold by Kurdran Shipping to undisclosed buyers for USD 20.90 mil, VV Value USD 19.12 mil.

MR2 Lanikai (46,300 DWT, 2002, STX Offshore) sold by Lanikai Shipping to undisclosed Chinese buyers for USD 9.50 mil, VV Value USD 10.14 mil.

MR2 Caroline (45,000 DWT, 2002, STX Offshore) sold by Caroline Shipping to undisclosed Chinese buyers for USD 9.50 mil, VV Value USD 9.91 mil.

Containers:A stable story across container values this week with little movement across most sectors, older Feedermaxes continue their upward trend. Port congestion and no notable changes in the Strait of Hormuz keeping vessel utilisation high.

Sub Panamax AS Angelina (2,127 TEU, 2007, Wadan Wards MTW) sold with forward delivery by MPC Container Ships for USD 17 mil, VV Value USD 22.5 mil.

Handy Container AS Selina (1,740 TEU, 2012, Guangzhou Wenchong) sold with forward delivery by MPC Container Ships for USD 24 mil, VV Value USD 24 mil.
