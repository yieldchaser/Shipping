Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, May 26 2026

Hellenic Shipping News  |  28 May 2026  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-may-26-2026

Bulkers:Bulker S&P activity was strong this week, with 18 sales reported across all major size segments.The standout deal was an en bloc transaction involving four 2024-built Handysize vessels; Clacton, Margate, Eastbourne and Portsmouth sold by Bank of Communications to Norden at USD 30.5 mil each. Values firmed in response to strong pricing, observed among both new and vintage tonnage

Capesize Chin Shan (175,600 DWT, 2004, CBC Kaohsiung) sold by Sincere Navigation Corp for USD 20.3 mil, VV Value USD 19.0 mil

Panamax Moana (82,000 DWT, 2026, Yizheng Yangzi) sold for USD 36.5 mil, VV Value USD 35.1 mil

Supramax Crimon Knight (58,700 DWT, 2013, Nantong COSCO KHI) sold by Marubeni for USD 19.1 mil, VV Value USD 19.3 mil

Handysizes Clacton, Margate, Portsmouth (40,600 DWT, 2024, Jiangmen Nanyang), and Eastbourne (40,500 DWT, 2024, Jiangmen Nanyang) sold to Norden in an enbloc deal for USD 122 mil, VV Value USD 126.9 mil

Tankers:The secondhand Tanker market was quiet this week against a backdrop of broader sector-wide slowdown

LR1 Voula (73,800 DWT, Apr 2009, New Times Shipbuilding) sold by Trafigura Beheer BV for USD 25 mil, VV Value USD 23.49 mil

MR2 (Chemical/Product) Hellas Fighter (50,000 DWT, Oct 2015, Hyundai Mipo) sold internally by Latsco Shipping to Euroholdings for USD 39.25 mil, VV Value USD 39.17 mil

MR2 (Chemical/Product) CS Fujairah & CS Rotterdam (50,000-50,600 DWT, 2026, Chengxi Shipyard) sold including TC until Aug 2027 by China Construction Bank FL for USD 99 mil in an en bloc deal, VV Value USD 95.11 mil

Containers:With no new second-hand sales coming to light this week, values remain stable across most sectors and ages
