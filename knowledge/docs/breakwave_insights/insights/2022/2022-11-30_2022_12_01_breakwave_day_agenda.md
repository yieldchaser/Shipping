## 10 AM - NOON (EST)
Breakwave Advisors is hosting its 2nd annual shipping market event on Dec 1st from 10 a.m. to noon (EST). This two-hour event will feature well-respected industry participants and speakers who will be streaming live from around the world.
There will be two panel discussions, as well as two market presentations for content, covering both tankers and dry bulk. The goal is to educate investors in an engaging way and provide insights from market experts discussing the global shipping markets, as well as their forecasts and outlook for 2023.
No registration required for the event. To access the stream on the day of the event simply visit our homepage.
We hope to see you on Dec 1st.
