## Summary
Main
There seems to be slightly more interest in the crude sector this week and sentiment is growing that we are at the beginning of an upwards period. A handful of short-term VLCC period fixtures were reported and rates moved higher with the one-year average for VLCCs up $1,000 to an estimated $22,500/pdpr. On the clean side, there has been little to report in the way of fixtures although rates for MRs increased slightly across all periods with one-year periods now approximately $12,250/pdpr.

## Main

Oil prices have risen to a two-week high as the US eases travel restrictions and the American Petroleum Institute (API) reported its first crude oil inventory draw in six weeks.
