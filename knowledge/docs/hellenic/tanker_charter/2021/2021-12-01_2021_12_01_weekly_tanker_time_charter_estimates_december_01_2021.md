## Summary
Main
Despite excitement over the last few weeks that the tanker market was finally recovering, the crude market remained subdued this week as concerns over the impact of the Omicron Covid variant halted any progression in the market. Some period activity reported in the clean market this week as owners and charterers look to put together some fixtures before the holiday season begins.
