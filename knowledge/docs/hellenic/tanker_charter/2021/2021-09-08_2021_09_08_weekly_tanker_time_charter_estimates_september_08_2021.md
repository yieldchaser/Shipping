## Summary
Main
In the crude spot market, rates firmed slightly as more cargo entered the market but this has yet to have an impacton the period market where rates remained flat. Limited fresh period business to report on the clean side as the market remained at low levels. Oil prices fell mid-week on concerns over weak demand in the US and Asia and pressure from a strong dollar.

## Main
