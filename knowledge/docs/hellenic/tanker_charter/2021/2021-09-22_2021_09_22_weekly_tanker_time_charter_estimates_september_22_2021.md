## Summary
Main
Some VLCC fixtures have been reported in the crude sector this week but this has had little impact on rates as sentiment remains low. Limited activity in the clean sector this week and very little to report in terms of period fixtures. Oil prices climbed this week as data showed US crude stocks falling more than expected following the two hurricanes highlighting tight supply as demand improves.
