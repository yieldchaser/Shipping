Highlights of the Week
The aggressive interest-rate hikes by Central Banks around the world is making a soft landing harder to achieve and economists believe that a
slowdown in economic growth is necessary to restore global stability. Fears of recession, waning consumer confidence and a drop in demand are
bringing steel prices down, which in turn are lowering prices offered by recyclers to secure tonnage. While all the major recycling destinations are
currently facing massive currency devaluation and inflationary pressure, yet the offer prices have remained relatively stable due to a paucity of
