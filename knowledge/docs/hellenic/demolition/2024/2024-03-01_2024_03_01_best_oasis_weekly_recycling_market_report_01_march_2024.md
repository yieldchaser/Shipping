The ship recycling markets across India, Bangladesh, Pakistan, and Turkiye are currently navigating through varying degrees of stagnation and cautious
trading. In India, the market is experiencing a downturn with a significant drop in demand for vessels and scrap materials, signaling a market stagnation.
