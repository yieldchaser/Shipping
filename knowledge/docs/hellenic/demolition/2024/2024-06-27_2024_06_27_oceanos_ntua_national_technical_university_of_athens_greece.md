## Main
Oceanos - NTUA was established in 2016 and comprises 70 undergraduate students from various institutions.Located at the NTUA campus, the team is organized into five sub-teams. They actively participated in the HydroContest for two years (2017-18) and have been involved in the MEBC since then. Their notable achievement includes securing 2nd place overall in 2023, with three 1st place wins. Beyond competition, their objectives extend to promoting the broader impact of green energy and contributing to a sustainable future.
