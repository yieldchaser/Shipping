port infrastructure, particularly in developing nations. Furthermore, a portion of the proceeds should be directed to the least developed countries to aid in their
transition away from the usage of fossil fuels. The World Bank joins a rising chorus of governments and organizations, including the United States, France, and
Denmark, all eager for IMO member states to agree on and implement a carbon levy as soon as possible.
