policies to regulate crude steel production, and the escalating demand emanating from burgeoning sectors such as new energy vehicles and solar power.

period.

developments mostly due to robust demand for services and significant advancements in combating inflation. This phenomenon enhances the likelihood of
a smooth transition for the global economy. However, it is important to be watchful as the inherent uncertainty of global events continues to persist.
