revival. In India, the market mirrored last week's underwhelming performance, with optimism hinging on potential post-budget activity, though this remains

awaited opportunity.

decline more sharply, from 2.8% to 1.9%, due to labor market weaknesses and fiscal constraints. India, on the other hand, is expected to maintain robust
