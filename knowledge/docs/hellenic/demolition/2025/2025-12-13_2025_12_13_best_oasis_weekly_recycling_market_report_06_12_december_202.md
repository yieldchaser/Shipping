MARKET SUMMARY
INDIA
The market softened during the week, with values continuing to face downward
pressure amid weak overall sentiment.
Ongoing currency weakness continued to weigh on buyer confidence, limiting
participation in the local market.
Buyer participation remained limited throughout the week, as most buyers adopted a
cautious and selective approach.
Overall activity levels stayed subdued, with little evidence of near-term improvement.
BANGLADESH
The local market remained weak during the week, with overall activity levels staying
very low.
Buyer participation continued to be extremely limited, with only one notable Bulker
sale concluded.
The Bulker transaction was achieved at a surprisingly strong level, outperforming
regional alternatives.
Despite weak sentiment and limited buyers, this sale highlights that Bangladesh can
still deliver stronger outcomes for owners seeking higher levels.
PAKISTAN
Market conditions remained unchanged during the week, closely mirroring the
previous period.
Sentiment stayed steady, with no visible shift in buyer behavior.
Overall conditions reflected a market in a wait-and-see mode.

TURKIYE
The market remained steady throughout the week, maintaining its usual consistency.
Pricing and sentiment showed no material movement during the period.
Activity levels continued without any notable change. The Turkish market once again
reflected its low-volatility nature.
