## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 24/05/2024
DCE iron ore futures continued to fluctuate in a day the main contract closed 908ďĽŚan decrease of 0.44% throughout the day;Traders' quoting intentions are generally average, while steel mills are more cautious and wait-and-see, but overall purchasing intentions are still acceptable. The overall market transaction atmosphere is still good today. In Shandong, the transaction price of PB powder is in the range of 885-895 yuan/ton, which is basically stable compared to yesterday's prices; as of May 24th, SMM tracked a total of 144.40 million tons of inventory in 35 ports, an increase of 430,000 tons from last week and an increase of 20.02 million tons year-on-year; the average daily port clearance volume of imported ore is 2.893 million tons, a decrease of 84,000 tons compared to the previous month, an increase of 74,000 tons year-on-year, and port inventories are still relatively high, which puts pressure on ore prices to rise; Considering that the current profit of steel mills is in a loss-making state, there is a strong willingness to pressure ore prices, and it is expected that the price of iron ore will continue to fluctuate in the near future.
