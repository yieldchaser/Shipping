## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 13/06/2024
DCE iron ore futures fell first and then rose today， the main contract closed 817，an increase of 0.93% throughout the day;Traders ship according to market conditions; Some steel procurement more cautious, the continuation of a small number of multi-frequency procurement strategy. Today the overall market transaction atmosphere is acceptable. PBF at Shandong port dealt 805-815 yuan/mt; the same as yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 830-840 yuan/mt; increased 0-5 yuan/mt over yesterday. the Federal Reserve's interest rate meeting released hawkish signals, reducing the likelihood of a rate cut. Aﬀected by this, coupled with poor macroeconomic data performance, market sentiment has become even more pessimistic, and the market has continuously fallen to new lows in the past two months. However, from the perspective of industry data, rainfall and high temperatures have led to a continued weakening of terminal demand, and the total inventory of steel has begun to accumulate. However, the magnitude of the accumulation is relatively small, which is in line with market expectations. The pessimistic sentiment has eased, driving a rebound in the market. Considering that the fundamentals of iron ore are still relatively weak, it is expected that it will be difficult to reverse in the short term and will continue to show a weak and volatile trend.
