## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 19/04/2024
DCE iron ore futures tight, The main contract I2409 closed at 871.an decrease of 0.34% throughout the day;Traders ship according to market conditions; Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchased on demand. ## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 19/04/2024
DCE iron ore futures tight, The main contract I2409 closed at 871.an decrease of 0.34% throughout the day;Traders ship according to market conditions; Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchased on demand. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 900-905 yuan/mt; decreased 0-5 yuan/mt over yesterday. As of April 19, the total inventory of 35 ports tracked by SMM reached 141.54 million tons, an increase of 0.86 million tons compared to the previous period, and an increase of 14.59 million tons compared to the same period last year.
