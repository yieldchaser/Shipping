in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 08/01/2024

## Main
DCE iron ore futures open higher and go lower, futures market falling trends today; the main contract I2405 closed992.5. some traders were negative to sell; Some steel procurement more cautious, the continuation of a small number of multi-frequency procurement strategy. PBF at Shandong port dealt 1040-1043 yuan/mt; decreased 2-10 yuan/mt from last Friday. SMM's global iron ore shipments totaled 29.08 million tons, a significant decrease of 18.1% compared to the previous month. Both Australia and Brazil experienced a significant decline in shipping volume. Affected by domestic pressure on ports, the total amount of arrivals was 25.2047 million tons, a significant decrease of 10.27% compared to the previous month. The surge of overseas mines has basically ended and will return to normal levels in the later stage. But the arrival volume may continue to be high. The overall supply remains loose. On the one hand, the demand is due to the approaching Spring Festival, and on the other hand, terminal demand continues to weaken. Some steel mills have added blast furnace maintenance plans, resulting in low iron production. However, considering that there is still support for replenishing inventory before the Spring Festival, short-term mining prices may continue to fluctuate.
