## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 11/03/2024
DCE iron ore futures market falling sharply trends by 5.41%. the main contract I2405 closed 831.Some traders adopt separate quotations; Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchasing enthusiasm is not high. total transactions is poor PBF at Shandong port dealt 835 yuan/mt; decreased 40-43 yuan/mt from last Friday. Last week, SMM's global iron ore shipments totaled 30.43 million tons, a decrease of 0.8% compared to the previous week. Among them, 18.53 million tons were shipped from Australia, an increase of 22.5% compared to the previous period; Australia shipped 14.52 million tons to China, accounting for 78.3% of the total shipment to Australia, an increase of 24.4% month on month. Brazil shipped 5.42 million tons, a month on month decrease of 33.9%; Brazil.
