## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 19/01/2024
DCE iron ore futures rose largely today by 2.63%. the main contract I2405 closed 957. some traderswere negative to sell；Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchased on demand.

## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 19/01/2024
DCE iron ore futures rose largely today by 2.63%. the main contract I2405 closed 957. some traderswere negative to sell；Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchased on demand. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 1000-1012 yuan/mt; increased 10 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 1010 yuan/mt; the same as yesterday. As of January 19, 2024, SMM has tracked a total of 123.27 million tons of inventory in 35 ports, with a month on month accumulation of 430000 tons and a year-on-year accumulation of 5.16 million tons; The daily average port clearance of imported ore in this period increased by 59000 tons month on month to 3.06 million tons, a decrease of 53000 tons year-on-year. Recently, there has been a significant decline in iron ore prices, coupled with the recovery of steel mill profits. Some steel mills have accelerated their replenishment pace, which has led to an increase in port dredging. Considering that the replenishment is coming to an end, although there is still an expectation of an increase in molten iron production, it is difficult to have a significant increase in overall demand, and it is expected that mining prices will continue to fluctuate within a narrow range.
