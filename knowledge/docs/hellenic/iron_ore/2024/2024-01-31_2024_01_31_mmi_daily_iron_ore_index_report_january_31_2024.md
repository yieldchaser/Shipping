## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 31/01/2024
DCE iron ore futures market falling sharply trends by 3.08%. the main contract I2405 closed 960.5.Some traders adopt separate quotations. and Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchasing enthusiasm is not high.

## Main
DCE iron ore futures market falling sharply trends by 3.08%. the main contract I2405 closed 960.5.Some traders adopt separate quotations. and Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchasing enthusiasm is not high. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 1007-1014 yuan/mt; decreased 10-15 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 1040-1030 yuan/mt; decreased 0-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. The operating rate of blast furnaces tracked by SMM remained stable this week, with a month on month increase of 3800 tons/day in molten iron production. But as the end of the year approaches, most steel mills have already completed restocking, and only some steel mills near the port still have a small demand for restocking; Weakening support for iron ore prices. The short-term macro benefits have been exhausted, and market sentiment has turned negative, causing iron ore prices to fluctuate and decline. Considering that there will still be a certain increase in molten iron in the future, and overseas shipments are entering the off-season, which will provide some support for ore prices, it is expected that iron ore prices will face a dilemma of upward and downward fluctuatiƟons, with continued volatility as the main trend.
