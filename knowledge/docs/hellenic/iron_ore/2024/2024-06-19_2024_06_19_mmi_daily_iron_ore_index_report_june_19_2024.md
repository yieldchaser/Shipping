## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 19/06/2024
DCE iron ore futures continued to ﬂuctuate in a day，Significant drop at the end. the main contract closed 824，an decrease of 0.36%throughout the day; some traders were negative to sell；Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchased on demand. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general . PBF at Shandong port dealt 820-830 yuan/mt; the same as yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 840-850 yuan/mt; the same as yesterday. According to the SMM survey, as of June 19, the operating rate of blast furnaces surveyed by SMM was 93.91%, ﬂat WoW. The capacity utilization rate was 95.78%, down 0.07% WoW. The average daily pig iron output of the sample steel mills was 2.2905 million mt, down 1,800 mt.according to SMM tracking, local steel mills' profits are recovering. Next week, there will still be blast furnaces resuming production as planned, and there will be a significant increase in molten iron; There is still an increase in demand for iron ore. Although there have been continuous reports of crude steel production restrictions recently, in the short term, the actual impact is limited, and it is expected that the iron ore price range will ﬂuctuate.
