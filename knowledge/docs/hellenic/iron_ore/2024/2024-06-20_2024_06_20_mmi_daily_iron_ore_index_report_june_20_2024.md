## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 20/06/2024
DCE iron ore ﬂuctuated repeatedly， the main contract closed 824.5，an decrease of0.36% throughout the day; Traders followed the market trend to sell; steel mills were mostly cautious and not very active in purchasing. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 825-830 yuan/mt; increased 5 yuan/mt over yesterday.. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 835-850 yuan/mt; increased 5 yuan/mt over yesterday. Today, the apparent demand for steel slightly rebounded, indicating that end-user demand, although in the oﬀ-season, still shows resilience. However, the overall market sentiment remains pessimistic. Additionally, there have been many recent market rumors, making it difficult to distinguish between true and false information. With no clear direction in the fundamentals, prices are easily swayed by news. Iron ore prices face pressure from above and support from below, and in the short term, they may continue to ﬂuctuate within a range.
