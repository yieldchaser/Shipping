## Main
DCE iron ore futures today, and experienced a significant increase in the afternoon.The main contract I2409 closed at 870, with a daily increase of 4.25%. Traders are actively selling, while steel mills are cautious and have reduced their inquiries. Overall, the market has a good trading atmosphere today. PBF at Shandong port dealt 875-888 yuan/mt; increased 30-40 yuan/mt over yesterday. According to SMM research, on April 17th, the blast furnace operating rate calculated by SMM was 90.94%, up 0.59% from the previous period. The blast furnace capacity utilization rate was 91.95%, up 0.61% from the previous period. The daily average pig iron production of sample steel mills was 2.1979 million tons, an increase of 14.6 thousand tons from the previous period. It is estimated that there will be more.
