## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 22/02/2024
DCE iron ore futures fall today，but the decline has narrowed somewhat. an decrease of 1.49% throughout the day; Traders ship according to market conditions; Inquiries from steel mills have increased, with a focus on on-demand procurement. Today the overall market transaction atmosphere is acceptable.

## Main
DCE iron ore futures fall today，but the decline has narrowed somewhat. the main contract I2405 closed893.5. an decrease of 1.49% throughout the day; Traders ship according to market conditions; Inquiries from steel mills have increased, with a focus on on-demand procurement. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 940- 945 yuan/mt; decreased 5 yuan/mt over yesterday. Based on the inventory data of the top ten main port iron ore varieties tracked by SMM, the port inventory showed an accumulated state this week.
