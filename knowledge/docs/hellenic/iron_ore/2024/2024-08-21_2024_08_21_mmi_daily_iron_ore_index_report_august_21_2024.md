## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 21/08/2024
Iron ore futures opened high and moved higher, with the most-traded contract I2501 surging significantly to close at 742, up 4.58% for the day.Traders' enthusiasm for selling increased noticeably. Steel mills were more cautious, with average purchasing intentions. The overall market transaction atmosphere was acceptable today. In Shandong, PB fines traded at 725-730 yuan/mt, up 15-20 yuan/mt from yesterday; in Tangshan, PB fines traded at 745-750 yuan/mt, up 15-20 yuan/mt from yesterday. Finished steel prices stabilized and rebounded, steel mill profits recovered somewhat, and expectations for improved demand in the peak season strengthened, boosting ferrous metals significantly. However, this week, SMM reported a blast furnace operating rate of 91.42%, down 0.95% WoW. Sample steel mills' daily pig iron production was 2.2421 million mt, down 25,100 mt WoW. Iron ore demand continues to decline, and pressure on iron ore remains, with limited upward momentum. Attention should be paid to the future improvement in apparent steel demand.
