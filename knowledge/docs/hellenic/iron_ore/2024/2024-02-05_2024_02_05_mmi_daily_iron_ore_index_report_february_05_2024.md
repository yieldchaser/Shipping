## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 05/02/2024
DCE iron ore futures fall today; the main contract I2405 closed 943. As the Spring Fesτιval approaches,some traders have already closed down. And the pre holiday restocking of steel mills has basically ended, with fewer inquiries and almost stagnant market transacτιons.. PBF at Shandong port dealt 985 yuan/mt; the same as last Friday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 985-995 yuan/mt; the same as last Friday. According to SMM shipping data, the total global iron ore shipments last week were 28.58 million tons, a decrease of 10.2% compared to the previous week. Among them, 15.69 million tons were shipped from Australia, a decrease of 10% compared to the previous period; Australia sends 12.65 million tons to China, and the proporτιon of shipments to China has increased. The arrival volume in this period was 26.31 million tons, an increase of 3.28% compared to the previous period. On the demand side, there may be a decline with the end of pre holiday storage in steel mills. Short term loose supply suppresses mining prices. It is expected that short-term mining prices may conτιnue to fluctuate weakly.
