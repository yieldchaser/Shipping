## Main
DCE iron ore futures open higher and go lower， falling trends in the day， the main contract closed 813，an decreaseof 1.63% throughout the day; Some traders are closed and unable to ship, while others have average enthusiasm for shipping；Some steel procurement more cautious, the continuation of a small number of multi-frequency procurement strategy . total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 810 yuan/mt; decreased 7-20 yuan/mt from last Friday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 825-830 yuan/mt; decreased 10-15 yuan/mt from last Friday. Today, iron ore prices experienced another pullback, mainly following Friday's bearish M1 data; In addition, the one-year MLF bid interest rate announced by the central bank is 2.50%, which is unchanged from before and lower than market expectations. Affected by this, the market has declined slightly. From the fundamental perspective of iron ore itself, last week, SMM's global iron ore shipments totaled 27.85 million tons, a decrease of 8.6% compared to the previous week. Shipping volumes from Australia, Brazil, and non mainstream mines have all declined. The arrival volume was 20.54 million tons, an increase of 6.65% compared to the previous month. But it is still lower than the same period last year. The pressure on iron ore supply has eased to some extent, and iron ore prices still have support, with limited room for short-term exploration.
