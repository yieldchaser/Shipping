## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 23/01/2024
DCE iron ore futures open lower and go higher, the main contract I2405 closed 965.5. an increase of6.48% throughout the day. Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchasing enthusiasm is not high.

## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 23/01/2024
DCE iron ore futures open lower and go higher, the main contract I2405 closed 965.5. an increase of6.48% throughout the day. Some traders adopt separate quotations. Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchasing enthusiasm is not high. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 1017-1018 yuan/mt; increased 6-7 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 1035 yuan/mt; increased 10 yuan/mt over yesterday. According to SMM statistics, this week (January 20th to January 26th), the impact of blast furnace maintenance on molten iron was 1.9889 million tons, a decrease of 35800 tons compared to last week's maintenance. The impact of blast furnace maintenance on molten iron next week (January 27th to February 2nd) is 1.9525 million tons, a decrease of 36400 tons compared to this week. The production of molten iron continues to slightly recover. The global shipping volume of iron ore has declined, and the fundamental support for iron ore has strengthened. Considering the current weak industrial chain, macro news has a significant impact on prices, and short-term iron ore prices may continue to fluctuate.
