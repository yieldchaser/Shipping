## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 21/03/2024
DCE iron ore futures rose largely today，the main contract closed 849.5，an increase of 2.72% throughout the day.The market trading sentiment is warming up, with decent shipping sentiment among traders. On the steel mill side, there is an increased willingness to purchase, leading to an overall improvement in market transactions compared to previous periods. In Shandong, the transaction price for PB powder is in the range of 830-835 yuan/ton, while in Tangshan, the transaction price is 840-845 yuan/ton, representing an increase of 15-20 yuan/ton compared to the previous day.
