## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 23/03/2022
DCE iron ore futures open lower and go higher, the main contract closed 822.5, an increase only 0.43% throughout the day; but as the diskcontinues to decline. some traders were active to sell, Some steel mills purchasing enthusiasm are not high. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 930-950 yuan/mt; prices are mixed today over yesterday. SSF at Shandong port dealt 644-650 yuan/mt; increased 5 yuan/mt over yesterday. SSF at Tianjin port dealt 670 yuan/mt; decreased 3 yuan/mt over yesterday. Affected by the COVID-19, Tangshan implemented the whole area closed control, traffic and transportation standstill. The epidemic situation in Shandong Binzhou has not been alleviated. Two places of raw materials and timber transport stagnation, a number of steel mills stuffy furnace, iron ore demand rapid decline, in the short term, the price of weak shock operation.
