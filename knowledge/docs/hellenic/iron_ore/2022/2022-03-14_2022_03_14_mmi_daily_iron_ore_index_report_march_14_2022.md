## Main
DCE iron ore futures market falling sharply trends by 6.98%.the main contract closed 759.5. the quotation in the morning was decreased by10 yuan/mt from last Friday. some traders were active to sell, Steel mills buy in small quantities on demand. total transactions were few. PBF at Shandong port dealt 935-970 yuan/mt; decreased 10-40 yuan/mt from last Friday. SSF at Shandong port dealt 608-610 yuan/ mt; decreased 25-30 yuan/mt from last Friday. JMBF at Shandong port dealt 835-845 yuan/mt; With the closing of the Winter Paralympic Games, the northern environmental protection limit production came to an end. Many places of steel enterprises have resumed production of blast furnace, but because of the continued contraction of steel profits, steel procurement of low - and medium-quality products increased. As steel mills inventory consumption, iron ore demand will increase this week, give some support to the mine price.
