DCE iron ore was was shocked and keep upward all the day. The main contract closed at 782,increased of 1.82%. Traders are activelymotivated to ship, Speculative demand increases. SSF at Shandong port dealt 640 yuan/mt .PBF at Tangshan port dealt782-794 yuan/mt, decrease 10 yuan/mt. SSF at Tangshan port dealt 680 yuan/mt. In the macro aspect, the fourth meeting of the Sixth General Assembly of China Iron and steel industry association was held in Beijing. The meeting mentioned that the steel industry should continue to reduce crude steel production and increaseiron ore development in the second half of the year.
