## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 29/07/2022
DCE iron ore was was shocked and keep upward all the day. The main contract closed at 782,increased of 1.82%. Traders are activelymotivated to ship, Speculative demand increases. Steel mills are mainly on the sidelines, and market turnover continues to pick up. PBF at Shandong port dealt 775-792yuan/mt; keep stable compare with yesterday. SSF at Shandong port dealt 640 yuan/mt .PBF at Tangshan port dealt782-794 yuan/mt, decrease 10 yuan/mt. SSF at Tangshan port dealt 680 yuan/mt. In the macro aspect, the fourth meeting of the Sixth General Assembly of China Iron and steel industry association was held in Beijing. The meeting mentioned that the steel industry should continue to reduce crude steel production and increaseiron ore development in the second half of the year. In terms of fundamentals, as of July 29, the inventory of 35 ports tracked by SMM totaled 131.39 million tons, 3.58 million tons more than last week and 5.16 million tons more than the same period last year. The port inventory has accumulated for five consecutive periods. The average daily port dredging volume of imported mines in this period increased by 6000 tons to 2.499 million tons on a weekly basis, mainly due to the increased demand for replenishment of raw materials under.
