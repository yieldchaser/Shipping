## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 08/03/2022
DCE iron ore futures open higher and go lower, the main contract closed 844.5, an increase only 0.36% throughout the day; The spotquotation in the morning quoted varied. some traders were active to sell, steel mills continue purchase on demand. Transaction price difference is larger, the overall market transaction atmosphere is general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 992-1015 yuan/mt, decreased 5-25 yuan/mt over yesterday. According to data tracked by SMM, 74 ships arrived at domestic main ports in February 28-March 6. Arrivals of cargoes are estimated to stand at 11.26 million mt, up 1.95 million mt from the previous session and down 1.66 million mt year on year. Shipments that departed Australian ports were estimated to fall 1.54 million mt week on week to 15.57 million mt, up 0.13 million mt on the year. And that from Brazilian ports added 0.64 million mt to 5.86 million mt on a weekly basis, down 1.03 million mt on the year. The total arrivals of imported ore rose significantly from the prior session, while the combined shipments from Australia and Brazil dropped slightly. The arrivals rose palpable in the latest session after falling for two weeks in a row. The blast furnaces in north China are expected to resume the production after the environmental protection- related production restriction ended, creating more demand for iron ore. Iron ore prices are expected to carry some upside momentum.
