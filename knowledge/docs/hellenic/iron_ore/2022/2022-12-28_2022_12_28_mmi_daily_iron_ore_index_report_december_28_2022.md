## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 28/12/2022
DCE iron ore futures rose by 0.72% today, the main contract closed 835.5. The traders' willingness to ship is general.The steel mills are wait and see.The overall trading sentiment of the market is general.PBF at Shandong port dealt 822-830 yuan/mt. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 835-838 yuan/mt,decrease 2-5yuan/mt. According to the seasonal ﬂuctuations in previous years, the steel plant will have a plan to resume production of some repaired blast furnaces in January, and it is expected that there will be a certain amount of hot metal increment in January. The pace of replenishment of the steel plant on New Year's Day and before the New Year has accelerated, and the demand for iron ore has a certain support. At present, the macro benefit is gradually consumed, and the iron ore price is still expected to operate in a wide range of shocks.
