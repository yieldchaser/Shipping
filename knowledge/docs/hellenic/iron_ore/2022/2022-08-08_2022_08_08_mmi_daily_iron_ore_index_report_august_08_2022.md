## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 08/08/2022
DCE iron ore was was shocked and keep downward all the day.The main contract closed at 737.5,,increased of 4.31%. Traders are generallymotivated to ship, Speculative demand increases. Steel mills are mainly on the sidelines, and market turnover continues to pick up.PBF at Shandong port dealt 785-795 yuan/mt;,increase 10-20 yuan/mt than last week. PBF at Tangshan dealt 800 yuan/mt,increase 30 yuan/mt than last week. According to SMM statistics, SMM's global iron ore shipments totaled 29.95 million tons, a decrease of 12.1% from the previous month. Among them, Australia sent 13.9 million tons to China, decrease of 10.7% from the previous month; Brazil sent 2.61 million tons to China, decrease of 13.6% from the previous month. In terms of demand, this week will continue to fulfill the expectation of resumption of production, the average daily production of molten iron will increase slowly, and the replenishment of raw materials in steel mills may increase, which may lead to a decline in port inventories. At present, the supply side has narrowed, and the average daily port dredging volume has increased. In the short term, the iron ore price tends to fluctuate strongly.
