## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 10/10/2022
DCE iron ore futures rose by 2.21% today, the main contract closed 739.5.More transactions in the market.PBF at Shandong port dealt 763-768 yuan/mt, increase 3-13yuan/mt;PBF at Tangshan port dealt 775 yuan/mt ;increase 5 yuan/mt. According to SMM statistics, the global iron ore shipments this week totaled 30.47 million tons, down 4.6% month on month. Among them, 13.27 million tons were sent from Australia to China, a decrease of 17% month on month; Brazil sent 3.42 million tons to China, down 8.3% month on month. Overseas shipments have declined for two consecutive periods. In terms of demand, the steel plant's inventory was at a low level in the same period after the consump− tion of raw materials during the holiday, and under the guidance of the opening of futures, the steel plant began to replenish its inventory one after another. And the downstream demand margin improved after the festival. The current average daily molten iron output of the steel plant is high, driving the demand for iron ore. Considering the recent overhaul of a few steel plants due to production restriction, loss and other reasons, and the important meeting in October is approaching or affecting the production of steel plants, it is expected that the ore price will be.
