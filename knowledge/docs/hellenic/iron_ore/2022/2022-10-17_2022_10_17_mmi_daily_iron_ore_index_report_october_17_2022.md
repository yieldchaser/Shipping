## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 17/10/2022
DCE iron ore futures declined by 2 % today, the main contract closed 686.5.General transactions in the market.PBF atShandong port dealt 720- 723 yuan/mt, decrease 12-18yuan/mt. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 740 yuan/mt. According to SMM, during the period from October 10 to October 16, the global iron ore shipments totaled 29.57 million tons, a decrease of 3% month on month; Among them, the volume from Australia to China increased slightly, the volume from Brazil to China decreased, and the volume of domestic port arrivals decreased slightly.

## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 17/10/2022
DCE iron ore futures declined by 2 % today, the main contract closed 686.5.General transactions in the market.PBF atShandong port dealt 720- 723 yuan/mt, decrease 12-18yuan/mt. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 740 yuan/mt. According to SMM, during the period from October 10 to October 16, the global iron ore shipments totaled 29.57 million tons, a decrease of 3% month on month; Among them, the volume from Australia to China increased slightly, the volume from Brazil to China decreased, and the volume of domestic port arrivals decreased slightly. On the demand side, the restrictions on sintering in Tangshan have been lifted recently, but several steel plants in Shanxi have reported the overhaul of blast furnaces. In the short term, due to the inﬂuence of the epidemic on the sales of finished products and the poor profits of steel mills, the price of iron ore may still be volatile.
