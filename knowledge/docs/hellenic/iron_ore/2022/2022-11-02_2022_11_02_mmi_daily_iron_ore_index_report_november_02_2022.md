## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 02/11/2022
DCE iron ore futures rose by 2.28 % today, the main contract closed 629. The traders' willingness to ship is more. The steel mills are purchaseon demand. The overall trading sentiment of the market is general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 650 yuan/mt,increase 2-10 yuan/mt.PBF at Tangshan port dealt 650 yuan/mt, decrease 5-7 yuan/mt. Recently, news about the maintenance of the blast furnace has been reported frequently. According to the survey, the operating rate of the blast furnace calculated by SMM on November 2 was 92.27%, down 0.3% month on month; The productivity utilization rate of blast furnace is 92.67%; It is estimated that the average daily hot metal output of steel plants in China is 2.6395 million tons, a decrease of 19400 tons compared with the previous month. This week, the centralized mainte- nance of the blast furnace was concentrated in the northwest and north China. The main reason was that the profits of the steel plant continued to decline due to poor terminal demand, which led to the independent maintenance of the steel plant. Considering the current steel market oﬀ-season, it is difficult for demand to recover significantly, and the sustainability of the iron ore price rebound is questionable.
