## Main
Jan 4th, 2022 CFR Qingdao, USD/dry tonne DCE iron ore open lower and go higher, the main contract closed 689, an increase of 2.23%throughout the day. the quotation in the morning was increased by 0-10 yuan/mt from last Friday.
