Basically, according to SMM statistics, from August 15 to August 21, the total volume of global iron ore shipments was 29.62 million tons, an increase of 0.8% month on month. Among them, the volume sent to China by Australia due to berth maintenance decreased by 19.8% month on month, and the volume sent to China by Brazil increased by 31.3% month on month.
