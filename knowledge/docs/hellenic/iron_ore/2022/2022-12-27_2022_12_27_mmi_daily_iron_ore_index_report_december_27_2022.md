## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 27/12/2022
DCE iron ore futures rose by 1.83% today, the main contract closed 836. The traders' willingness to ship is more.The steel mills are wait and see.The overall trading sentiment of the market is general.PBF at Shandong port dealt 825-829 yuan/mt,increase 2-14 yuan/mt. From a macro perspective, the market is no longer optimistic about the news of the implementation of full nucleic acid testing for inbound personnel. On the supply side, the Bureau of Statistics announced that in November, China's iron ore raw mineral volume was 79.536 million tons, up 0.6% year on year; From January to November, the accumulated iron ore supply was 889.046 million tons, down 0.5%. Currently, iron ore supply is mainly stable. On the demand side, according to SMM statistics, the hot metal impact brought by the overhaul of the blast furnace this week was 182.27 tons, an increase of 26,400 tons compared with last week. This week, some blast furnaces decided to stop production due to the completion of the production plan and poor profits, aﬀecting the demand for a small amount of iron ore. Considering that the recent epidemic news may continue to stimulate the market, it is expected that the iron ore price will still ﬂuctuate widely.
