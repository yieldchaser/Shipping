## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 30/11/2022
DCE iron ore futures declined by 0.32% today, the main contract closed 769. The traders' willingness to ship is more.The steel mills are purchase on demand. The overall trading sentiment of the market is general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 759-761 yuan/mt,decrease 10 yuan/mt; PBF at Tangshan port dealt 773-778 yuan/mt. The recent macro news is driven by good real estate news, and the market sentiment is optimistic. However, the purchasing managers' index of China's manufacturing industry was released in November today, showing a continuous decline, indicating that the pressure on economic operation has increased due to the frequent outbreaks of the epidemic. And from the current terminal data, the downstream demand is relatively light. Considering the fundamentals, according to the SMM survey, the operating rate of the blast furnace on November 30 increased by 0.44% to 91.97% month on month. It is estimated that the daily average hot metal output of the steel plant increased slightly, and the demand for iron ore was supported. To sum up, it is expected that iron ore prices will ﬂuctuate in the short term.
