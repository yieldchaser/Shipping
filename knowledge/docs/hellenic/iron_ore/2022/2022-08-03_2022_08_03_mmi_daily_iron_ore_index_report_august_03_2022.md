## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 03/08/2022
DCE iron ore was was shocked and keep downward all the day.The main contract closed at 786.5,decreased of 0.82%. Traders are generallymotivated to ship, Speculative demand increases. Steel mills are mainly on the sidelines, and market turnover continues to pick up.PBF at Shandong port dealt 780-795 yuan/mt;,decrease15 yuan/mt than yesterday;SSF at Shandong port dealt 660 yuan/mt;PBF at Tangshan dealt 777 -790 yuan/mt,decrease 10-23yuan/mt than yesterday. Affected by geopolitical news, the market sentiment panicked, and the futures disk fell slightly. From a fundamental point of view, the supply and demand pattern of the steel market is slowly improving, but the terminal demand has not yet improved significantly. According to SMM research, on August 3, the blast furnace operating rate according to SMM statistics was 89.45%, an increase of 0.74 percentage points from the previous week. The blast furnace capacity utilization rate was 90.01%, up 0.19 percentage points from the previous week. At present, the news of steel plant maintenance and production resumption is mutual, resulting in a slow recovery of the average daily hot metal production. The confidence in the stable development of the macro is still there, and it is expected.
