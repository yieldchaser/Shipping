## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 14/02/2022
DCE iron ore futures market falling sharply trends by 6.82%. the main contract closed 776.5. Some traders adopt separate quotations.Market wait-and-see sentiment increased. total transactions is poor. PBF at Shandong port dealt 930 yuan/mt, decreased 40 yuan/mt from last Friday; SSF at Shandong port dealt 580-585 yuan/mt, decreased 40-50 yuan/mt from last Friday. SMM analysis, since January, due to the resumption of production expectations and macro positive news, iron ore has been in a fast rising trend, especially before and after the holiday, although iron ore demand is poor, but iron ore prices are rising, divorced from the fundamentals. Development and Reform Commission issued three consecutive statements, iron ore prices began to fall. Superimposed on the recent port inventory back to the peak, tomorrow will continue to be weak shocks.
