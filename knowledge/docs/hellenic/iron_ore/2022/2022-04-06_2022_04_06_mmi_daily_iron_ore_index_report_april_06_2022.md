in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 06/04/2022

## Main
DCE iron ore futures rose largely，an increase of 2.15% throughout the day. the main contract 2209 closed 926.5. The spot quotation in themorning almost kept steady. Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchasing enthusiasm is not high. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 1020-1030 yuan/mt; increased 5-15 yuan/mt from last week. SSF at Shandong port dealt 740 yuan/mt; increased 5 yuan/mt from last week. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 1050 yuan/mt; increased 15 yuan/mt from last week. SSF at Tangshan port dealt 757 yuan/mt. According to SMM research, due to the COVID-19 outbreak, cross-regional transportation control in many parts of the country, steel mills inventory is low. With the improvement of the epidemic situation in Tangshan, the main area of Tangshan city will be unsealed recently, steel production will accelerate, the demand for iron ore is strong, give the mine price support, in the short term is expected to continue to strong shock.
