## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 29/08/2022
DCE iron ore was was shocked and keep downaward all the day.The main contract closed at 714,decreased of 1.38%. Traders are generallymotivated to ship. From a macro perspective, the Fed's further interest rate hike is bad for commodity prices, affecting market confidence, and the iron ore futures market is down slightly. Basically speaking, SMM's global iron ore shipments totaled 28.03 million tons, a month on month decrease of 5.4%. Among them, the volume sent by Australia to China increased by 7.8% month on month, and the volume sent by Brazil to China decreased by 39.6% month on month. The supply side was narrowed, and there was a certain support from the ore price. This week, there are still some steel mills expecting to resume production, and the average daily molten iron output may rise slightly.
