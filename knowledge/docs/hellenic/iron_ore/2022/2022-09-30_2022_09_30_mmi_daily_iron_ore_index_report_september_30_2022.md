## Main
DCE iron ore futures rose by 0.07% today, the main contract closed 721.5.Few transactions in the market.PBF at Shandong port dealt 753yuan/ mt,PBF at Tangshan port dealt 768 yuan/mt ; keep stable compare with yesterday. This week, the steel mill just needs to replenish the warehouse, which drives the port dredging volume to increase again. Considering the impact of the major meeting in October and the advance of environmental protection production restriction, the demand for iron ore may decline slightly after the festival. It is expected that the price of iron ore will fluctuate widely in the later period.
