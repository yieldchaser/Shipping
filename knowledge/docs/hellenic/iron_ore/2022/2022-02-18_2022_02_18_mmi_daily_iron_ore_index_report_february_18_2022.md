## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 18/02/2022
DCE iron ore futures continued to fluctuate in a day. the main contract low hit 661.5, an decrease of 1.37% throughout the day. The spotquotation in the morning quoted varied. some traders were active to sell, and steel mills purchased on demand. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 840-850 yuan/mt, increased 0-10 yuan/mt over yesterday; SSF at Shandong port dealt 505-510 yuan/mt, increased 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 845 yuan/mt, increased 5 yuan/mt over yesterday; Stocks at 35 ports tracked by SMM totaled 155.39 million tonnes as of Feb. 18, accumulating another 1.2 million tonnes from the previous week. Meanwhile, the average daily port clearance volume of imported ore continued to decrease by 183,000 tons to 2.183 million tons on a weekly basis. Iron ore prices fell sharply this week, steel initially more cautious wait-and-see attitude, import ore spot transaction is rare, daily port activity has also declined. Considering the next week to limit production efforts to ease, daily port is expected to begin to improve, port inventory is expected to begin to decline. But iron ore demand is difficult to pick up sharply. Next week iron ore prices are expected to continue weak shock.
