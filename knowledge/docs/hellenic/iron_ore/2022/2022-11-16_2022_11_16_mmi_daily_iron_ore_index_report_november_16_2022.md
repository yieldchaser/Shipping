## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 16/11/2022
DCE iron ore futures rose by 2.16% today, the main contract closed 734. The traders' willingness to ship is general .The steel mills are purchase on demand. The overall trading sentiment of the market is general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 725-735 yuan/mt,increase 5-15 yuan/mt. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 735-740 yuan/mt,increase 5-10 yuan/mt. Overseas, Svitzer, an Australian tugboat service giant, announced that it planned to suspend work indefinitely from November 18. According to SMM, the ports to be shut down will not involve Hedland Port and Dampier Port with the largest export volume of iron ore, but the iron ore shipment in Geraldton and Esperance will be disturbed by the shutdown. SMM estimates that the average weekly iron ore shipment volume of Geraldon Port and Esperance Port is about 330000 tons, accounting for no more than 3% of the total shipment volume of Australia. The strike had little impact on the iron ore shipping business in Western Australia. On the domestic side, recent macro news has spread frequently, and the National Development and Reform Commission has announced to further accelerate infrastructure construction, saying that the economic province will speed up stable development.
