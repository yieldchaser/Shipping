## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 06/06/2022
Jun 6th, 2022 CFR Qingdao, USD/dry tonne DCE iron ore futures operated with shock all day. The main contract closed 925,increased of0.65%. Traders are generally enthusiastic about selling, steel mills are mainly keep take and see attitude, and the market transaction atmosphere is positive. PBF at Shandong port dealt 985- 999 yuan/mt， increased 5-10 yuan/mt over last week.PBF at Tangshan port dealt 992-995 yuan/mt; increased 7-10 yuan/mt over last week. The transaction price of SSF in Tangshan was concentrated at 817 yuan, 10-15 yuan / ton higher than that of last week. Before the Dragon Boat Festival, the demand of East and North China recovered, and the market mentality of starting work after the festival was strong, driving the rise of futures. On the supply side, due to the heavy rain in Brazil, the port shipment has been significantly reduced. The overall shipping volume of Australia and Brazil is difficult to increase significantly in the short term. In terms of demand, the daily average hot metal output is at a high level, the demand for iron ore is relatively strong under favorable policies, and the port inventory is declining. Taking into account of the steel mill profits and policy repression, iron ore has limited to rise. It is expected that iron ore will run at a high level in the short term.
