## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 28/03/2022
DCE iron ore futures market rose sharply trends by 4.64%. the main contract closed 879. the quotation in the morning was increased by 20yuan/mt from last Friday. The rise in prices has stimulated speculative demand, steel mills purchased on demand. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 990-1000 yuan/mt; increased 20-30 yuan/mt from last Friday. SSF at Shandong port dealt 695-700 yuan/mt; increased 25-30 yuan/mt from last Friday; SSF at Caofeidian port dealt 710 yuan/mt; increased 20 yuan/mt from last Friday. PBF at Tianjin port dealt 1000-1010yuan/ mt; increased 25-35 yuan/mt from last Friday Recently, although affected by the epidemic, Shandong and Hebei, blast furnace operation rate has decreased, iron ore demand has been suppressed, but the overall market for the post-epidemic steel demand is expected to be optimistic. Superposition of recent steel data shows that the terminal also remains optimistic, in the national stable economic environment, favorable policy is still expected. Considering the impact of the epidemic will continue for a period of time, it is expected that the short-term iron ore prices will fluctuate.
