## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 13/04/2022

The spot quotation in the morning almost keptsteady. some traders quotation is still stronger, steel mills purchased on demand. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 980- 1000 yuan/mt; decreased 10-30 yuan/mt over yesterday. SSF at Shandong port dealt 710-730 yuan/mt; prices are mixed today over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 990-1000 yuan/mt; SSF at Tangshan port dealt 730-735 yuan/mt. the same as yesterday. In the morning, policy supervision again out of the news, iron ore futures fell.
