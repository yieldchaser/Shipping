## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 12/12/2022
DCE iron ore futures declined by 0.8 % today, the main contract closed 802.5. The traders' willingness to ship isgeneral. The steel mills are less active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is worse.

## Main
DCE iron ore futures declined by 0.8 % today, the main contract closed 802.5. The traders' willingness to ship isgeneral. The steel mills are less active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is worse. PBF at Shandong port dealt 815 yuan/mt,decrease 5yuan/mt; SSF at Shandong port dealt 677 yuan/mt .PBF at Tangshan port dealt 826-827 yuan/mt,decrease 1-9 yuan/mt. On the supply side, according to SMM statistics, the global iron ore shipments last week totaled 30.48 million tons, down 3.3% month on month. Among them, 15.75 million tons were shipped from Australia to China, an increase of 2.8% month on month; Brazil sent 2.05 million tons to China, a year-on-year decrease of 46.9%. The total amount of iron ore in China reached 27.31 million tons, an increase of 33.6% month on month. The decrease in arrivals in the previous period was mainly due to the impact of the epidemic on the efficiency of port operations, while the arrivals in the current period have recovered. On the current demand side, the iron ore inventory of the steel mill has increased slightly, and the winter storage plan is still slowly carried out. From the macro news, the market had some optimistic expectations for the economic meeting and the Fed's interest rate
