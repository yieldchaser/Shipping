## Main
DCE iron ore futures declined by 1.92% today, the main contract closed 765. The traders' willingness toship is less.The steel mills are not active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is worse. PBF at Shandong port dealt 786-792 yuan/mt,decrease 3-9 yuan/mt; PBF at Tangshan port dealt 793-801 yuan/mt,increase 2-6 yuan/mt. Macroscopically, the joint prevention and control mechanism of the State Council issued specific measures on epidemic prevention and control, and the market saw the consumption of emotions. In terms of fundamen- tals, as of December 7, SMM statistics showed that the blast furnace operating rate was 92.12%, up 0.15% month on month. The estimated daily average hot metal output of steel mills across the country was 2.6221 million tons, up 3400 tons month on month. The hot metal recovered slightly, and the iron ore consumption was stable and bullish. The steel mills are still in a loss state even though they have been stored in winter. Their acceptance of the rise of the raw material end is relatively limited. It is expected that the short-term price of iron ore will ﬂuctuate mainly.
