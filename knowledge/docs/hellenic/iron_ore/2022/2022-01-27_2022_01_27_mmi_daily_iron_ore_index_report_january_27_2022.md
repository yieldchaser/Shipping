## Main
DCE iron ore futures continued to fluctuate in a day, an increase of 0.46% throughout the day. the main contract closed 769. the quotation inthe morning was increased by 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. Today there are still sporadic a small amount of just need to purchase, but the overall market transaction is relatively quiet. SSF at Shandong port dealt 575 yuan/mt, increased 5 yuan/mt over yesterday; PBF at Tangshan port dealt 900 yuan/mt,increased 10 yuan/ mt over yesterday; This week, SMM counted blast furnace operating rates at 80.13%, up 0.58 percentage points on a weekly basis and 1 percentage point on a monthly basis. East China, southwest part of the short-term maintenance of the resumption of production led to a small increase in operating rates. At the same time give ore prices certain support.
