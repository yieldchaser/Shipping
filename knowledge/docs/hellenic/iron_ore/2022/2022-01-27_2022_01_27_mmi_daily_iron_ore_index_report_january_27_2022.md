## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 27/01/2022
DCE iron ore futures continued to fluctuate in a day, an increase of 0.46% throughout the day. the main contract closed 769. the quotation inthe morning was increased by 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. Today there are still sporadic a small amount of just need to purchase, but the overall market transaction is relatively quiet. SSF at Shandong port dealt 575 yuan/mt, increased 5 yuan/mt over yesterday; PBF at Tangshan port dealt 900 yuan/mt,increased 10 yuan/ mt over yesterday; This week, SMM counted blast furnace operating rates at 80.13%, up 0.58 percentage points on a weekly basis and 1 percentage point on a monthly basis. East China, southwest part of the short-term maintenance of the resumption of production led to a small increase in operating rates. At the same time give ore prices certain support. Considering the replenishment of warehouses before the Spring Festival has ended, the Winter Olympic Games will be officially opened, and steel enterprises in the north will reduce and stop production according to the production limit requirements. Iron ore demand has fallen in the short term, and it is expected that the price of ore will continue to shock tomorrow.
