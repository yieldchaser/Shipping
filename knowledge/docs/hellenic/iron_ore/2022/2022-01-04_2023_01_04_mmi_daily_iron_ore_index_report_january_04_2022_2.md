## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 04/01/2023
DCE iron ore futures declined by 0.35% today, the main contract closed 846.5.The traders' willingnessto ship is general.The steel mills are wait and see.The overall trading sentiment of the market is general.PBF at Shandong port deal 838-850 yuan/mt,decrease 1-10 yuan/mt; PBF at Tangshan port dealt 846-852 yuan/mt,decrease 3-14 yuan/mt. Today, it was rumored that the relevant parts of the country were conducting research on iron ore prices. This news touched the market sentiment, and iron ore futures fell back again. Fundamentally, according to the blast furnace operation rate data surveyed by SMM, as of January 4, the number of repaired blast furnaces in the sample steel plants had increased due to losses, and the blast furnace operation rate and the utilization rate of blast furnace capacity had decreased significantly this week. And the terminal demand is weak after entering the slack season, and the demand for iron ore just drops. However, considering that the replenishment is in progress before the Spring Festival, and the market may have certain expectations for the recovery of economic develop- ment in 2023, it is expected that the iron ore price will remain volatile in the short term.
