## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 03/03/2022
DCE iron ore futures market roseing sharply trends , They rose as much as 9.58%. the main contract closed 797.5, an increase of 6.83%throughout the day. the quotation in the morning was increased by 30-50 yuan/mt over yesterday. Some traders adopt separate quotations, steel mills purchased on demand, Today the overall market transaction atmosphere is acceptable. PBF at Shandong port dealt 960-980 yuan/mt; increased 30-50 yuan/mt over yesterday. SSF at Shandong port dealt 615-620 yuan/mt, increased 35-40 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 975-980 yuan/mt, increased 40-45 yuan/mt over yesterday. The relevant units in Tangshan issued the specific measures of environmental protection control in Tangshan during The period of 3.3-3.5. For iron and steel enterprises, only a few sintering machines stopped production during the environmental control period, which has a small impact on hot metal production. The expected growth of iron ore demand remains unchanged, giving strong support to the ore price. In addition, russia-Ukraine conflict and other factors to boost market sentiment, promote the ore rose sharply, but considering the policy of supervision, is expected to run high volatility of mine prices.
