## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 26/04/2022
CFR Qingdao, USD/dry tonne DCE iron ore futures stop falling and rebounds，the main contract closed 809，an decrease of 2.53%throughout the day; the quotation in the morning was increased by 5 yuan/mt over yesterday. some traders were active to sell, steel mills purchased on demand. today's overall market transaction significantly improved. PBF at Shandong port dealt 923-930 yuan/mt; increased 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. SSF at Shandong port dealt 693-710 yuan/mt; increased 20-30 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 925-930 yuan/mt; increased 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. SSF at Tangshan port dealt 700-705 yuan/mt; increased 20-25 yuan/mt over yesterday. SMM tracking data shows that the current period is expected to be 12.246 million tons of cargo, the arrival of 250,000 tons less than the previous period, 36,000 tons more than the same period last year. During this period, The outbound shipments from Australia decreased by 450,000 tons to 18.01 million tons, an increase of 4.3 million tons over the same period last year. Brazil's outbound shipments increased by 580,000 tons to 6.1 million tons, up 590,000 tons from the same period last year. The amount of imported ore to Hong Kong in this period decreased slightly month-on-month, while the overall shipment from Australia and Brazil increased slightly. Recently, the shipments from Australia and Brazil have reached the year-on-year level, and the arrivals to Hong Kong are relatively stable. However, due to the impact of the epidemic in China, the blast furnace operation of steel enterprises is insufficient. Taking into account the approaching of the May Day holiday, the pre-holiday replenishment demand increased slightly, supporting the mine price.
