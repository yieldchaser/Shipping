## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 26/07/2022
DCE iron ore was was shocked and keep upward all the day.The main contract closed at 748.5,increased of 5.57%. Traders are moremotivated to ship, Speculative demand increases. Steel mills are mainly on the sidelines, and market turnover continues to pick up.PBF at Shandong port dealt 745 yuan/mt;increase 23-33 yuan/mt than yesterday. Recently, iron ore futures continued to rise, driving spot prices to rise rapidly. According to SMM statistics, as of July 25, there were 81 blast furnaces under inspection in China, with a cumulative volume of 95,420 m³, affecting a total of about 295,400 tons of molten iron production per day. It is estimated that the impact of molten iron caused by blast furnace maintenance this week is 2.044 million tons, an increase of 60,800 tons from last week. At present, the profit recovery speed of steel mills is not as fast as that of raw materials, and the willingness to purchase is low. It is difficult to reverse the supply and demand pattern in a short period of time, which will continue to support the ore price. It is expected that the iron ore price may continue to fluctuate in the short term.
