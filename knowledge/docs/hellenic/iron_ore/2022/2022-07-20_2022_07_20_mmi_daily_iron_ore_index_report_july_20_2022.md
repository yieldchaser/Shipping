## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 20/07/2022
DCE iron ore was was shocked all the day.The main contract closed at 665.5,decreased of 0.37%. Traders are general motivated to ship, steel mills are mainly on the sidelines, and there are few transactions in the market.PBF at Shandong port dealt 690-700 yuan/mt;increase10-15 yuan/mt than yesterday;SSF at Shandong port dealt 580-585 yuan/mt. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 695-708 yuan/mt On the supply side, iron ore prices were boosted by Vale's release of second-quarter iron ore delivery data and a lower annual output target. In terms of demand, the loss and production reduction of steel mills are still expanding, and the willingness of steel mills to replenish inventories is low. In addition, according to the national real estate investment data in the first half of 2022, only seven provinces experienced slow growth in real estate investment in the first half of the year, while other regions experienced investment regressions to varying degrees. At present, weak terminal demand is still the main point of contradiction, and it is expected that in the short term, the mining price will mainly run in a weak and volatile manner.
