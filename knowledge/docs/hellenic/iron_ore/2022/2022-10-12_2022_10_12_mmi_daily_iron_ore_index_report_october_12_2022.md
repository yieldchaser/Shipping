## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 12/10/2022
DCE iron ore futures declined by 2.12% today, the main contract closed 714.5.General transactions in the market.PBF at Shandong port dealt738-755 yuan/mt, decrease 10-17 yuan/mt;PBF at Tangshan port dealt 748-755 yuan/mt ;decrease 10-20 yuan/mt. On the macro level, based on the current domestic and international economic environment, the market has certain expectations for important national conferences. In terms of supply, Australia and Brazil are about to enter the rainy season, which may lead to a reduction in shipment. After the festival, the replenishment of steel mills was gradually ended, and the epidemic situation in some areas affected the downstream construction and raw material transportation. The market lacked confidence in the growth of iron ore demand. The price range of iron ore will fluctuate in the short term.
