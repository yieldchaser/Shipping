## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 10/06/2022
DCE iron ore fluctuated in a wide range. The main contract closed 914.5,decreased of 1.72%. Traders are generally enthusiastic about selling,steel mills are mainly keep take and see attitude, and the market transaction atmosphere is positive. PBF at Shandong port dealt 975-980yuan/ mt,decreased 15-20yuan/mt compare with yesterday.PBF at Tangshan port dealt 980-985yuan/mt, decreased 5-10 yuan/mt than yesterday. As of June 10, the inventory of 35 ports tracked by SMM totaled 123.71 million tons, a continuous decrease of 3.8 million tons compared with the previous week, an increase of 3.46 million tons over the same period of last year, and the port inventory has fallen in the 11th period. According to SMM data, overseas mainstream iron ore shipments are expected to rebound next week. In addition, there is no significant increase in demand in the recent policy boost. The off-season of the steel market is approaching, the steel mills' profits are poor, the operating rate is average, and the steel mills' demand for iron ore is expected to increase significantly, which is expected to reduce the enthusiasm for port dredging. On the whole, it is expected that the iron ore price will fluctuate at a high level next week.
