## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 18/11/2022
DCE iron ore futures rose by 3.29% today, the main contract closed 753.5. The traders' willingness to ship is general.The steel mills are purchase on demand. The overall trading sentiment of the market is less. PBF at Shandong port dealt 738-750 yuan/mt,increase 8-25 yuan/mt; PBF at Tangshan port dealt 760 yuan/mt,inrease 25 yuan/mt. According to the inventory data of the top ten ports tracked by SMM, the total inventory of the top ten ports increased by 1.2% month on month to 89.67 million tons this week. As of November 18, the inventory of 35 ports tracked by SMM totaled 130.2 million tons, 580000 tons more than last week, 18.55 million tons less than the same period last year. The port inventory has been accumulated for four consecutive periods. The average daily port dredging volume of imported ore in this period decreased by 35000 tons to 2.792 million tons on a weekly basis. However, back to fundamentals, overseas shipment in November was still on the high side. Recently, overseas mines are relatively stable, and it is expected that overseas supply will remain relatively loose in the later period. The demand side is disturbed by the maintenance of the blast furnace and sintering machine, and the hot metal output may decline slightly next.
