## Main
DCE iron ore was was shocked and keep upward all the day.The main contract closed at 695,increased of 3.98%. Traders are generallymotivated to ship. Steel mills are more mainly on the sidelines, and market turnover continues to pick up.PBF at Shandong port dealt 735-745 yuan/mt,increase 15-25 yuan/mt.SSF at Shandong port dealt 635-640 yuan/mt.PBF at Tangshan dealt 750 yuan/mt,increase 30 yuan/mt than yesterday. Although there are many stocks, the market is worried that it may affect the supply of some domestic mines, and the futures market is going up.
