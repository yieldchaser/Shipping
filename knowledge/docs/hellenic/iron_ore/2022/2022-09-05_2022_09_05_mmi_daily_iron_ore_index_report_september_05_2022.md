## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 05/09/2022
DCE iron ore was was shocked and keep upward all the day.The main contract closed at 695,increased of 3.98%. Traders are generallymotivated to ship. Steel mills are more mainly on the sidelines, and market turnover continues to pick up.PBF at Shandong port dealt 735-745 yuan/mt,increase 15-25 yuan/mt.SSF at Shandong port dealt 635-640 yuan/mt.PBF at Tangshan dealt 750 yuan/mt,increase 30 yuan/mt than yesterday. According to SMM statistics, a total of 180 ships from around the world arrived at China's main ports this week, and the amount of imported minerals increased by 7.23 million tons compared with last week. During this period, the shipment volume of Australia and Pakistan increased. Recently, there have been frequent accidents in domestic mines. Although there are many stocks, the market is worried that it may affect the supply of some domestic mines, and the futures market is going up. In addition, due to the low inventory of steel mills, some steel mills began to stock up for the National Day holiday, which may delay the logistics. In a word, the ore price may fluctuate strongly in the short term.
