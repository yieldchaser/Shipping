## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 08/09/2022
DCE iron ore was was shocked all the day.The main contract closed at 706,increased of 3.14%. Traders are generally motivated to ship. Steelmills are mainly on the sidelines, and market turnover continues to pick up.PBF at Shandong port dealt 740-755 yuan/mt,increase 10-15 yuan/ mt than yesterday. PBF at Tangshan dealt 758 yuan/mt,increase 23 yuan/mt than yesterday. As the Mid-Autumn Festival is approaching, some steel mills are looking for replenishment resources for the holiday. The low profit of imported mines and the high cost of traders support mine prices. At present, the demand for finished products of steel mills has improved, and the average daily production of molten iron has increased slightly, which has driven the enthusiasm of steel mills to produce. Moreover, the current steel mill inventory is low, and there is a certain demand for raw material replenishment. It is expected that the demand for iron ore will improve in the short term following the demand for finished products, and the price of iron ore will fluctuate strongly.
