## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 05/08/2022
DCE iron ore was was shocked and keep downward all the day.The main contract closed at 780.5,increased of 1.30%. Traders are generallymotivated to ship, Speculative demand increases. Steel mills are mainly on the sidelines, and market turnover continues to pick up.PBF at Shandong port dealt 766-786 yuan/mt;,increase 10 yuan/mt than yesterday;SSF at Shandong port dealt 630-645 yuan/mt,increase 15 yuan/ mt.PBF at Tangshan dealt 770 yuan/mt,increase 10 yuan/mt than yesterday. As of August 5, the 35 port stocks tracked by SMM totaled 133.13 million tons, accumulating 1.74 million tons from last week and an increase of 5.22 million tons from the same period last year. Port stocks have accumulated for six consecutive periods. The average daily port dredging volume of imported mines in this period increased by 82,000 tons to 2.581 million tons on a week-on-week basis. The number of blast furnaces resumed this week has increased, which has increased the demand for restocking of raw materials. The enthusiasm for port dredging has increased, and port inventory has accumulated. The volume has narrowed from the previous period. It is expected that future shipments will drop slightly after the peak period ends.
