## Main
DCE iron ore was was shocked and keep downward all the day.The main contract closed at 780.5,increased of 1.30%. Traders are generallymotivated to ship, Speculative demand increases. Port stocks have accumulated for six consecutive periods. The average daily port dredging volume of imported mines in this period increased by 82,000 tons to 2.581 million tons on a week-on-week basis. The number of blast furnaces resumed this week has increased, which has increased the demand for restocking of raw materials. The enthusiasm for port dredging has increased, and port inventory has accumulated. The volume has narrowed from the previous period. It is expected that future shipments will drop slightly after the peak period ends.
