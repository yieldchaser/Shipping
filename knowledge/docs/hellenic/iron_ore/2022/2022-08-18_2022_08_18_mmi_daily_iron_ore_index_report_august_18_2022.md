in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 18/08/2022

## Main
DCE iron ore was shocked and keep downward all the day. The main contract closed at 678.5,decreased of 3.96 %. Traders are no more motivated to ship. Steel mills are mainly on the sidelines, and market turnover continues to pick up. PBF at Shandong port dealt 730-740yuan/ mt, decrease 10-15yuan/ mt than yesterday. PBF at Tangshan dealt 745 yuan/mt, keep stable compare with yesterday. According to the inventory data of the top ten ports tracked by SMM, the total inventory of the top ten ports this week increased slightly by 0.6% month on month to 90.1 million tons. Since the beginning of July, the current top ten ports have been accumulated in the seventh phase. At present, the downstream demand is affected by high temperature and limited production, which slows down the resumption of production of the steel plant. The optimistic expectation of the market for the approaching peak season has weakened, resulting in the steel plant being cautious in purchasing. However, at present, the production of molten iron has increased month on month this week, and the demand side is still supported.
