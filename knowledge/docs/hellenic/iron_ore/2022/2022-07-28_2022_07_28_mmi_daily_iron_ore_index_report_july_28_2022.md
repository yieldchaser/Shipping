## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 28/07/2022
DCE iron ore was was shocked and keep upward all the day.The main contract closed at 793.5,increased of 7.16%. Traders are activelymotivated to ship, Speculative demand increases. Steel mills are mainly on the sidelines, and market turnover continues to pick up.PBF at Shandong port dealt 772-780yuan/mt;increase 25-50 yuan/mt than yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt780-805yuan/mt,increase 40-55yuan/ mt. From a macro perspective, the recent fall in the US dollar index, coupled with the meeting held by the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, proposed to improve the stability of the industrial chain, supporting the continued rebound in iron ore prices. From the fundamen− tal point of view, the apparent demand of steel mills has improved and the profits have increased, but the sustainability of profits remains to be observed. The low inventory of raw materials in steel mills has boosted the demand for raw materials. In terms of overseas supply, the output of FMG iron ore in the second quarter reached 46.8 million tons, an increase of 6% month on month, and the Simandou large iron ore project made a new breakthrough.
