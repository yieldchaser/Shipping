## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 31/01/2022
DCE iron ore futures strong pull up again by 7.59% throughout the day. the main contract closed 829. With the continuous strengthening ofthe disk, traders speculation operation increased, the port spot transaction atmosphere has improved. PBF at Shandong port dealt 910-945 yuan/mt, increased 10-45 yuan/mt over yesterday; SSF at Shandong port dealt 600 yuan/mt, increased 25 yuan/mt over yesterday; PBF at Tangshan port dealt 920-940 yuan/mt, increased 20-40 yuan/mt over yesterday; As of Jan. 28, stocks at 35 ports tracked by SMM totaled 149.02 million tons, down 1.07 million tons month-on-week, but less than last week. Mainly from the recent import of ore to the port cargo volume decreased obviously caused by. In addition, the average daily port clearance volume of imported ore decreased by 88,000 tons to 3.087 million tons. During the Spring Festival holiday next week, the average daily port clearance volume is expected to continue to decline. Although overseas mine shipments have decreased recently, port operation efficiency is expected to decline significantly during the Spring Festival holiday, and the decline in port inventory is expected to slow down.
