## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 22/07/2022
DCE iron ore was was shocked and keep upward all the day.The main contract closed at 681,increased of 3.57%. Traders are general motivated to ship, steel mills are mainly on the sidelines, and there are few transactions in the market.PBF at Shandong port dealt 680-690 yuan/ mt;increase15-30 yuan/mt than yesterday;SSF at Shandong port dealt 565-585 yuan/mt. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 700 yuan/mt. The early backlog risk of iron ore futures was released, and the disk rebounded slightly. However, from the perspective of fundamentals, the inventories of 35 ports tracked by SMM totaled 127.81 million tons, slightly accumulating 1.73 million tons over last week, an increase of 1.43 million tons over the same period last year. And the average daily port dredging volume of imported mines fell by 63000 tons to 2.493 million tons on a weekly basis, reaching the low point of port dredging volume in recent two months. At present, the domestic steel market is in a severe situation, and the weak demand situation is difficult to change in the short term. Superimposed on the frequent disturbance of external news, it is expected that the iron ore price will fluctuate in the short term.
