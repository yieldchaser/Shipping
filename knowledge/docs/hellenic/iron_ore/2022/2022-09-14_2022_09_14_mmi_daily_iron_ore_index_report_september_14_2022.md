## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 14/09/2022
DCE iron ore futures declined by 0.96% today, the main contract closed 720.5. Some traders were active to sell. More steel mills tended topurchase, and purchasing enthusiasm is high. Total transactions returned actively.PBF at Shandong port dealt 755-759 yuan/mt; decreased 5-15 yuan/mt than last week.SSF at Shandong port dealt 650 yuan/mt, decreased 5 yuan/mt than last week. The U.S. unseasonably adjusted CPI in August increased by 8.3% year-on-year, which was higher than expected, and the possibility of the Federal Reserve raising interest rates again increased. Affected by this, the iron ore futures disk began to fall. From a fundamental point of view, according to SMM statistics, the blast furnace operating rate is 94.35%, down 0.15% week-on-week; the blast furnace capacity utilization rate has dropped by 1.13%; and it is estimated that the average daily production of molten iron in steel mills across the country decreased by 22,000 tons week-on-week, mainly due to the The weekly blast furnace maintenance has increased, but due to the normal stocking in the traditional peak season, the raw material inventory of steel mills is low, and the demand for iron ore will not decrease. Supply has narrowed this week due to overseas shipments.
