## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 03/11/2022
DCE iron ore futures rose by 1.12% today, the main contract closed 634.5. The traders' willingness to ship is more. The steel mills are purchase ondemand. The overall trading sentiment of the market is general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 645 yuan/mt,increase 0-5 yuan/mt. SSF at Shandong port dealt 542 yuan/mt .PBF at Tangshan port dealt 655-660 yuan/mt, increase 0-5 yuan/mt. Although the market rose today, the spot price was relatively stable. Today, the maintenance of steel plants' blast furnaces is still increasing. According to SMM research, up to now, there are still 3 blast furnaces with new maintenance plans in November, and 6 blast furnaces may resume production. Considering that the heating season in November is approaching, there will be a policy of production restriction that will aﬀect the production of steel plants again; In addition, in the oﬀ-season, the profits of steel mills are unlikely to recover, and the daily average hot metal output may decline again in November. In the short term, there is no strong support for iron ore prices, and the subsequent trend will be mainly shock.
