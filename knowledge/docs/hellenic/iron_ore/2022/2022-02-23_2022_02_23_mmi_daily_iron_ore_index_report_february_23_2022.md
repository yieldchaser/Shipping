## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 23/02/2022
DCE iron ore fluctuated to the upside in the morning and retreated in the afternoon. the main contract closed 700. an decrease of 0.78%throughout the day; The quotation in the morning steady decline over yesterday. Market wait-and-see sentiment increased, and port spot turnover continues low. PBF at Shandong port dealt 890 yuan/mt; SSF at Shandong port dealt 520-545 yuan/mt, increased 0-5 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 890 yuan/mt, increased 10 yuan/mt over yesterday; Since Last October, port inventories have accumulated continuously, reaching a high of 150 million tons, the highest level since 2019. Today, the National Development and Reform Commission organized some port enterprises to hold a "study on excessive iron ore hoarding work" special meeting. In the future, it will focus on solving the problem of high inventory; In addition, the iron ore demand has not recovered to the normal level, and in the short term, it is expected that the price of ore will continue to bear pressure and shock.
