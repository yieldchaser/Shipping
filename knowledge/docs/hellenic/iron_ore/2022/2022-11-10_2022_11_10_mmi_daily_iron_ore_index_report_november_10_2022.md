## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 10/11/2022
DCE iron ore futures declined by 1.39% today, the main contract closed 675.5. The traders' willingness to ship is general.The steel mills are purchase on demand. The overall trading senTment of the market is less. PBF at Shandong port dealt 677 yuan/mt,decrease 11 yuan/mt.
PBF at Tangshan port dealt 683 yuan/mt,decrease 16-17 yuan/mt. Recently, the market sentiment has cooled down, and there is insufficient momentum for the market to rise. According to the current shipping plan of overseas mines, the subsequent supply is sTll relatively loose, and the port inventory will accumulate later. According to the inventory data of the top ten ports tracked by SMM, the total inventory of the top ten ports this week accumulated 0.3% month on month to 88.58 million tons. In terms of varieties, the stock of coarse powder has decreased slightly, and the stock of fine powder, lump ore and pellets has accumulated to varying degrees. The main reason is that many provinces have limited the production of sintering machines in response to heavy pollution weather this week, resulting in poor demand for coarse powder.
