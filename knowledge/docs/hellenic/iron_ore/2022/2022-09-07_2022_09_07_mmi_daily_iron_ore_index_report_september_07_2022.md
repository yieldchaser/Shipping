## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 07/09/2022
DCE iron ore was was shocked all the day.The main contract closed at 680,decreased of 1.73%. Traders are generally motivated to ship. Steelmills are mainly on the sidelines, and market turnover continues to pick up.PBF at Shandong port dealt 730 yuan/mt,decrease 13 yuan/mt than yesterday. PBF at Tangshan dealt 735 yuan/mt,decrease 10-15 yuan/mt than yesterday. The market optimism is gradually consumed, and there is no strong support on the disk. Today, according to the statistics of the General Administration of Customs of China, in August 2022, China imported 96.208 million tons of iron ore and its concentrate, an increase of 4.964 million tons compared with the previous month. The supply data showed that the supply was loose in August. In September, the epidemic struck back again, sealing and suppressing steel demand in many places. However, considering that the epidemic may be gradually controlled, the increased enthusiasm of steel mills in the later stage may drive the replenishment of raw materials and support the price of ore. Iron ore prices are expected to fluctuate widely in the short term.
