in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 13/07/2022

## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 13/07/2022
DCE iron ore was was shocked and keep upward all the day.The main contract closed at 732,increased of 1.53%.Traders are generally motivated to ship, steel mills are mainly on the sidelines, and there are few transactions in the market. PBF at Shandong port dealt 755-775 yuan/mt; PBF at Tangshan port dealt 773 yuan/mt.Recently, the Industry Department of the National Development and Reform Commission held a coordination and promotion meeting for key domestic iron ore projects, aiming to speed up the development and construction of projects. The top real estate companies announced a month-on-month improvement in sales data, boosting market confidence, and iron ore futures rose late in the session. From a fundamental point of view, according to the General Administration of Customs, the import volume of iron ore in June fell slightly by 0.5%. The spokesman said that iron ore imports will be promoted, and the supply side is expected to continue to be loose. While the steel mills are implementing their production resumption plans one after another, a few steel mills still decide to suspend production for maintenance, the average daily hot metal production has declined, and the terminal demand has not improved.
