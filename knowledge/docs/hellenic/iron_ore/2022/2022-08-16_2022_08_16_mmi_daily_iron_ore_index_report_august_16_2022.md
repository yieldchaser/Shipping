## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 16/08/2022
DCE iron ore was was shocked and keep upward all the day.The main contract closed at 720.5,decreased of 0.28 %. Traders are moremotivated to ship. Steel mills are mainly on the sidelines, and market turnover continues to pick up.PBF at Shandong port dealt 765- 70yuan/mt.SSF at Shandong port dealt 640 yuan/mt.PBF at Tangshan dealt770-785 yuan/mt,in crease 10 yuan/mt than yesterday. The central bank's adoption of an unexpected interest rate cut may indicate that there will be more policies to stabilize the economy in the later period, giving the market some confidence and driving the iron ore futures market to continue to rise. However, from the basic point of view, there is a certain increase in the supply side, while the demand side still continues the slow recovery trend in the previous period. The port inventory continues to be high, and the support for the continuous rise of ore prices is not strong. It is expected that the iron ore price will mainly fluctuate in the short term.
