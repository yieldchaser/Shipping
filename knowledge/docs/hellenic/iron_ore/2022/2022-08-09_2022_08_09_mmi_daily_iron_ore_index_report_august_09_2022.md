## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 09/08/2022
DCE iron ore was was shocked sharply all the day.The main contract closed at 723,decreased of 1.36%. Traders are generally motivated toship, Speculative demand increases. Steel mills are mainly on the sidelines, and market turnover continues to pick up.PBF at Shandong port dealt 780 yuan/mt;,decrease 5-15 yuan/mt than yesterday. SSF at Shandong port dealt 645yuan/mt,decrease 10 yuan/mt.PBF at Tangshan dealt 790yuan/mt,decrease 10 yuan/mt than yesterday. The demand for raw materials in Shandong Province has improved, while the demand for raw materials in Hebei Province is generally affected by environmental protection and production constraints. At present, the attitude of steel mills is cautious. On the one hand, due to doubts about the sustainability of finished product sales, the balance between production and sales is the main factor in the short term; On the other hand, the profit of the steel plant has rebounded, and considering the rise of coke price and ore price, the sustainability of the profit of the steel plant is uncertain. On the whole, driven by the resumption of production of steel plants, the demand grows slowly, while the overseas shipment has dropped. It is expected that the iron ore price will still be subject to strong fluctuations.
