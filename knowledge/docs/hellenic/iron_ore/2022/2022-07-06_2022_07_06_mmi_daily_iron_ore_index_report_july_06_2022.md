## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 06/07/2022
DCE iron ore was was shocked all the day.The main contract closed at 747,increased of 1.84%. Traders are generally motivated to ship, steelmills are mainly on the sidelines, and there are few transactions in the market. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 805yuan/mt,keep stable compare with yesterday. Today, iron ore futures were consolidating and fluctuating. After a slight rebound at the beginning of the week, there was no strong upward momentum. Recently, there have been frequent reports of joint production cuts by steel mills around the world, and the market sentiment is more pessimistic. The output of finished products has declined slightly. In the short term, the output of molten iron may continue to decline. In the future, it is necessary to observe the implementation of production reduction in steel mills. Under the pattern of strong supply and weak demand, the slump in international oil prices is further negative for ore prices. It is expected that the ore prices will fluctuate weakly in the short term.
