## Main
DCE iron ore futures continued to fluctuate in a day，an increase of 2.23% throughout the day. the main contract closed 755.5. Thequotation in the morning was less, and chosed single discussion. Today the overall market transaction atmosphere is acceptable。Some businesses are optimistic about the future market,shipping enthusiasm turned weak. PBF at Shandong port dealt 860-870 yuan/mt, increased 10-20yuan/ mt over yesterday; SSF at Shandong port dealt 550-560 yuan/mt, increased 15-20 yuan/mt over yesterday.. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 865-870 yuan/mt. increased 15-20 yuan/mt over yesterday. Stocks at 35 ports tracked by THE SMM totaled 150.08 million tons as of Jan. 21, down 2,560,000 tons month-on-week. At the same time, the average daily port clearance volume of imported ore increased by 160,000 tons to 3.175 million tons. With the pre-spring Festival replenishment procurement activities of steel mills, the average daily port clearance volume continued to increase recently, leading to a significant drop in port inventory. Considering the last week before the Spring Festival next week, steel replenishment will come to an end, superimposed on the Winter Olympics production restriction policy is about to start, iron ore demand may fall, it is expected that the mine price next week may have a correction risk.
