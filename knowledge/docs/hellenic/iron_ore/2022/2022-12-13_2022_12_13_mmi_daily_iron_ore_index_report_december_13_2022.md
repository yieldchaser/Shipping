## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 13/12/2022
DCE iron ore futures declined by 0.19% today, the main contract closed 808.5. The traders' willingness toship is general. The steel mills are less active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is worse. PBF at Shandong port dealt 805-815 yuan/mt,decrease 7-10 yuan/mt; PBF at Tangshan port dealt 813-820 yuan/mt,decrease 7-10 yuan/mt. According to SMM statistics, the impact of blast furnace maintenance on molten iron this week was 183.74 tons, 78700 tons less than that of last week. It is expected that the impact of blast furnace maintenance on molten iron next week will shrink again, supporting a certain demand for iron ore. Considering the macro impact and the expectation of future replenishment, the iron ore price is expected to remain volatile in the short term.
