## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 31/10/2022
DCE iron ore futures declined by 4.11% today, the main contract closed 606.5. The traders' willingness to ship is less. The steel mills are purchase on demand. Theoverall trading sentiment of the market is less. PBF at Shandong port dealt 639 yuan/mt,decrease 21-31 yuan/mt. SSF at Shandong port dealt 540 yuan/mt,decrease 30 yuan/mt. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 655-658 yuan/mt, decrease 10-15 yuan/mt. According to SMM tracking, from October 24 to October 30, global iron ore shipments totaled 31.64 million tons, an increase of 39.6% month on month; Among them, 16.42 million tons were shipped from Australia to China, and 7.65 million tons from Brazil to China, both of which had significant increases. This week, due to terminal weakness, it is hard for iron fearing aquatic products to recover, and the port inventory may accumulate again. Under the pattern of strong supply and weak demand, it is expected that the short-term iron ore price will continue to ﬂuctuate weakly.
