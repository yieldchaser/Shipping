## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 08/04/2022
DCE iron ore futures open higher and go lower, an increase of 0.66% throughout the day. The spot quotationin the morning almost kept steady. some traders quotation is still stronger, and steel mills purchased on demand.

## Main
DCE iron ore futures open higher and go lower, an increase of 0.66% throughout the day. the main contract closed 918.5. The spot quotationin the morning almost kept steady. some traders quotation is still stronger, and steel mills purchased on demand. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 990-1015 yuan/mt; the same as yesterday. SSF at Shandong port dealt 720-725 yuan/mt; decreased 10 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 1025-1030 yuan/ mt. Iron ore inventories across 35 ports tracked by SMM totaled 147.40 million mt as of April 8, a decrease of 1.95 million mt from last week, but up 14.93 million mt year-on-year. The daily average shipment from the 35 ports increased 343,000 mt weekly to 2.911 million mt last week. Last week, pandemic related traffic control in Tangshan and other northern areas was eased, which led to an increase in daily average shipment, especially at Jingtang Port. In addition, the iron ore imports arriving at ports did not increase significantly recently. However, considering that the downstream of the terminal is gradually picking up, steel mills still need to purchase iron ore, which is conducive to the decline of the port inventory. The shipments from overseas mines have not increased significantly recently. It is expected that the port inventory will continue to decline.
