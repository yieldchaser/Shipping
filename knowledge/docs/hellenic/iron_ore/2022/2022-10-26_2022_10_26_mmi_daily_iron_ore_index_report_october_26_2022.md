## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 26/10/2022
DCE iron ore futures declined by 1.27% today, the main contract closed 662.5. The traders' willingness to ship is general . The steel mills are purchase on demand.The overall trading sentiment of the market is general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 705-715 yuan/mt. According to SMM survey, as of October 26, SMM statistics showed that the blast furnace operating rate was 92.57%, up 0.89% month on month. It is estimated that the daily average hot metal output of steel plants in China increased by 26100 tons month on month. In addition, in the near future, the West Iron and Steel Plant will jointly reduce the production, and in the later period, the production of molten iron may stop increasing and turn down. At present, the downstream demand has not improved, and the demand for finished products is difficult to improve significantly. The overall transaction of iron ore is relatively light due to this drag. In the short term, iron ore prices will continue to operate in a weak and volatile manner.
