## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 24/02/2022
DCE iron ore futures tight , the main contract closed 703, an increase of 0.43% throughout the day. The spot quotation in the morningalmost kept steady. some traders were active to sell, Some steel procurement more cautious. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 860-865 yuan/mt; decreased 10 yuan/mt over yesterday. SSF at Shandong port dealt 530-540 yuan/mt, decreased 5 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 870 yuan/mt, decreased 20 yuan/mt over yesterday; This week, SMM's blast furnace operating rate stood at 78.8%,up 1.9 percentage points week on week and down 1.3 percentage points month on month. Mainly due to the Beijing Winter Olympic Games, 10 blast furnaces in Tangshan have resumed production, but considering the winter Paralympic Games will be held next week, the northern region is still facing environmental protection production control, iron ore demand is still stable in the short term. In the short term, the mine price is still weak shock.
