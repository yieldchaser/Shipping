## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 03/01/2023
DCE iron ore futures declined by 0.41% today, the main contract closed 851.5.The traders' willingness to shipis general.The steel mills are wait and see.The overall trading sentiment of the market is general.PBF at Shandong port deal 825-846 yuan/mt,decrease 1-20 yuan/mt; PBF at Tangshan port dealt 847-855 yuan/mt,decrease 2-13 yuan/mt. Last week, SMM's global iron ore shipments totaled 33.15 million tons, down 2.1% month on month. Among them, 14.68 million tons were shipped from Australia to China, down 6.4% month on month; Brazil sent 4.08 million tons to China, up 4.1% month on month. Aﬀected by foreign Christmas holidays, the supply side narrowed slightly but changed little overall. At the beginning of the Spring Festival, steel mills still have the demand to replenish iron ore stocks. However, considering the suppression of steel plant profits, it is expected that iron ore prices will still ﬂuctuate in the short term.
