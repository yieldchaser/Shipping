## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 22/11/2021
DCE iron ore futures market rose by 6.18%,the main contract closed 558. the quotation in the morning was increased from last Friday. Sometraders were active to sell and buy, steel mills purchased on demand. Total transactions are better today. PBF at Shandong port dealt 590-595 yuan/mt,increased 20-25 yuan/mt from last Friday; PBF at Tangshan port dealt 580-595 yuan/mt, increased 10-35 yuan/mt from last Friday. SMM analysis,affected by market news, all black lines rose today, driving the price of iron ore to rise, but from the fundamental point of view, there is no change in loose supply, downstream demand is affected by profit and environmental regulation policies to reduce the trend, in the short term, the price of ore continues to shock, easy to fall but difficult to rise. However, in the medium and long term, some steel mills have completed the horizontal control task of crude steel, and the blast furnace is about to resume production, offering some support to ore prices.
