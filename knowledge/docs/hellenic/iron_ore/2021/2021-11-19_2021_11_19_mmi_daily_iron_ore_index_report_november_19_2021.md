## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 19/11/2021
DCE iron ore recovered in the afternoon.an increase of 2.49% throughout the day. The spot quotation in the morning almost kept steady.Some traders were active to sell, steel mills purchased on demand.The trading volumes has increase.PBF at Shandong port dealt 565-575 yuan/mt, increased -5-5 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 560-570 yuan/mt, the same as yesterday. As of November 19, the iron ore inventories across the 35 Chinese ports tracked by SMM totalled 147.33 million mt, an increase of 1.21 million mt from the previous week and 23.85 million mt year-on-year. The daily average shipments from the 35 ports increased 155,000 mt on a weekly basis to 2.79 million mt last week. The shipments from some north-eastern ports increased significantly as some steel mills in north China are restocking raw materials for winter. According to some steel mills in south China, their demand of iron ore declined under the production restrictions, and they were considering selling the ores that they purchased in long-term orders. The shipments from southern ports declined slightly. The iron ore purchases are hard to increase as the profits of many steel mills are shrinking, and Tangshan and Handan still implement production restrictions for environmental protection. The shipments at ports may not continue to increase.
