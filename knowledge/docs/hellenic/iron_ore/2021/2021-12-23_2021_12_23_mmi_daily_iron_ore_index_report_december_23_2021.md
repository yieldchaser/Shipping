## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 23/12/2021
DCE iron ore futures continued to drop today, and the main contract closed 692.5. an decrease only 0.65% throughout the day; thequotation in the morning was decreased by 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. some traders quotation is still stronger. steel mills purchased on demand. Today the overall market transaction atmosphere is acceptable. PBF at Shandong port dealt 790-800 yuan/mt, decreased 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday；SSF at Shandong port dealt 490-495 yuan/mt, the same as yesterday；PBF at Tangshan port dealt 792-805 yuan/mt, decreased 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday；PBF at Jiangnei port dealt 818 yuan/mt; This week, SMM counted blast furnace operating rates at 77.35%, up 1.04% week-on-week and 0.44% month-on-month. Up to now, in addition to individual areas, the overall crude steel output does not exceed last year's target, Tangshan, Shandong, part of the blast furnace has resumed production, the recent blast furnace operating rate ushered in a trend to rise. Give mine price certain support, iron ore prices are still based on shock operation.
