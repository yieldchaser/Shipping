## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 13/12/2021
DCE iron ore futures rose largely today, and the main contract closed 668.5, increased more than 5% for the day. the quotation in the morningwas increased by 10-20 yuan/mt from last Friday. Most traders tended to negotiate on the price separately, and some steel mills purchased on demand.

## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 13/12/2021
DCE iron ore futures rose largely today, and the main contract closed 668.5, increased more than 5% for the day. the quotation in the morningwas increased by 10-20 yuan/mt from last Friday. Most traders tended to negotiate on the price separately, and some steel mills purchased on demand. Today the overall market transaction atmosphere is acceptable. PBF at Shandong port dealt 710-725yuan/mt,increased 15-25 yuan/ mt from last friday;SSF at Shandong port dealt 465-470, increased 10-30 yuan/mt from last friday; PBF at Tangshan port dealt 720-730 yuan/mt; increased 25-35 yuan/mt from last Friday. PBF at Jiangnei port dealt 760 yuan/mt; SMM analysis, the recently macro expected warming; Iron ore demand will improve under the expectation of steel mill resumption, and the stacking port exhaustion speed will slow down; And tangshan 13 D grade steel mills to grade C, the market for production limit expectations to relax, have formed a strong support for iron ore prices, short- term iron ore prices will continue to be strong shock.
