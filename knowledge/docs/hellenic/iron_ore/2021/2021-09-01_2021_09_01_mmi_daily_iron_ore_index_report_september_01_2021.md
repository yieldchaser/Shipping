## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 01/09/2021
DCE iron ore futures fell sharply today, the main contract decreased by around 7.8% in a day. The spot quotation in the morning has increasedby 10yuan/mt over yesterday, while traders gradually lowered the quotations and accepted the bargain space, although they had bad selling mood today. PBF at Shandong port dealt 1020yuan/mt, fell by 70yuan/mt; presell PBF at Shandong port dealt 1010-1015yuan/mt, fell by 50- 55yuan/mt over yesterday. Recently, Guangxi has issued the policy on the energy control so as to require steel mills to cut production in the following days this year. Also, one large-scaled steel group are required to control their crude steel production that less than last years' output before the end of November, which all caused the extremely bear news on iron ore price. It expects that the iron ore price will be likely to be a low level in the short-term, but the recovering steel demand still need to be focus, that could support iron ore price after releasing the pessimistic mood, to some extent.
