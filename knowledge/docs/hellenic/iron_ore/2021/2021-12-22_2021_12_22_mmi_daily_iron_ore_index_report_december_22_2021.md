## Main
DCE iron ore futures market falling trends by 0.43%,the main contract closed 693.5. The spot quotation in the morning almostkept steady. Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 805-810 yuan/mt, the same as yesterday; SSF at Shandong port dealt 490-495 yuan/mt, decreased 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday；SMM analysis, the recent macro policy boost and blast furnace production expectations, iron ore prices rose shock. However, with the approaching of the Winter Olympics, Handan issued the air pollution management method to clear the steel production limit ratio, there are regional differences in the demand for iron ore increase or decrease, and the price of ore continues to operate under pressure and shock.
