## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 04/11/2021
DCE iron ore futures continued to fluctuate in a day,the main contract closed 582.5,an increase of 0.34% throughout the day. The spot quotationin the morning quoted varied, some traders give more bargain space on some brands and their negotiation pattern is more flexible.and steel mills purchased on demand. PBF at Shandong port dealt 715 yuan/mt, increased 5-20 yuan/mt over yesterday; SSF at Tangshan port dealt 390 yuan/mt, decreased 15-20 yuan/mt over yesterday ; This week, the blast furnace operating rate calculated by SMM was 77.11%, with a decrease of 0.74% on a weekly basis and 2.19% on a monthly basis. According to SMM survey data, several BF in Shandong will resume production in the near future. At the same time, after the relaxation of power restriction in Jiangsu Province, the blast furnace operating rate of the second and third tier steel plants has increased significantly. It is expected that the space for the decline of molten iron production in China is limited, and the demand for iron ore is expected to increase, which has a certain support for the ore price.
