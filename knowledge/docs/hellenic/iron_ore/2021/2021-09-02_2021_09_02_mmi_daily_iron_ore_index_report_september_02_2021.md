## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 02/09/2021
DCE iron ore futures market remained vulnerable tendency, and the physical port stock market inched down continuously. Prices for domesticconcentrates dropped sharply as well. Majority steel mills held their purchasing on demands as some of them see bearish of iron ore market. More and more steel mills started conducting production cuts and some of their iron ore in-plant inventory accumulated. However, some steel mills increased their purchasing for iron ore in the past few weeks when iron ore prices plummeted sharply, they told SMM, they have increased their in-plant inventory in consideration of the upcoming National Day holiday. The transactions in the physical port stock market warmed up although the quotations in the morning declined by 30yuan/mt. PBF in Shandong market was closed at 1020-1025yuan/mt while that in Tangshan market was 1085yuan/mt. The supply shortage of PBF in Tangshan market caused the expanding price gap between that in Shandong and Tangshan markets.
