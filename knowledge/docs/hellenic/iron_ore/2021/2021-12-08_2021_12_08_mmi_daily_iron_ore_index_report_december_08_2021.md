## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 08/12/2021
DCE iron ore futures tight by 1.46%. the main contract closed 658.5. The spot quotation in the morning almost kept steady. some traders wereactive to sell, Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see.today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 715-720 yuan/mt, the same as yesterday; SSF at Shandong port dealt 465-470, increased 10-15 yuan/mt over yesterday;.PBF at Tangshan port dealt 705-710 yuan/mt; increased 0-5 yuan/mt over yesterday;SMM analysis, recently macro environment is expected to improve, steel demand in the case of marginal warming of real estate policy is expected to increase, driving iron ore demand, give strong support to the mine price; However, iron ore fundamentals remained weak, with arrivals up 1.66 million tons and shipments to Australia and Brazil up 2.22 million tons. In addition, port inventory pressure is still large at this stage, there are constraints for the rebound; In the short term iron ore prices are still mainly shock.
