## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 10/12/2021
DCE iron ore futures continued to drop today. the main contract closed 639.5. The spot quotation in the morning almost kept steady. some tradersquotation is still stronger, and Some steel mills purchased on demand. Today's port spot trading volume reached this week's peak. PBF at Shandong port dealt 695-700 yuan/mt, decreased 0-5 yuan/mt over yesterday;SSF at Shandong port dealt 440-456, decreased 2-15 yuan/mt over yesterday;PBF at Tangshan port dealt 695 yuan/mt; decreased 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday; This week,the iron ore inventories across the 35 Chinese ports tracked by SMM totalled 150.57 million mt, up 160,000 mt from the previous week. Although the port inventory has accumulated for 11 consecutive weeks, the growth has slowed down significantly. The daily average shipments from the 35 ports increased slightly by 27,000 mt on a weekly basis to 2.71 million mt last week. The daily average shipments from the two major ports in Tangshan city declined 19% from the previous week to 337,000 mt. But as the arriving shipments in Tangshan have dropped noticeably over the past three weeks, the inventory at the two ports remained on the decline. In addition, some steel mills in north China have plans to cut output or suspend the production of blast furnaces due to the Winter Olympic Games. This will limit the increase in the operating rates at local blast furnaces. Iron ore supply strong demand weak pattern makes the ore price continue to pressure shock operation.
