## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 02/12/2021
DCE iron ore futures market falling trends，an decrease of 3.10% throughout the day; the main contract closed 609；some traders quotationis still stronger;steel mills purchased on demand. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. ## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 02/12/2021
DCE iron ore futures market falling trends，an decrease of 3.10% throughout the day; the main contract closed 609；some traders quotationis still stronger;steel mills purchased on demand. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 670-680 yuan/ mt,decreased 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday; IOCJ at Shandong port dealt 790 yuan/mt;PBF at Tangshan port dealt 675-680 yuan/mt, decreased 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday; According to SMM research, this week SMM's blast furnace operating rate was 79.05% on down 0.15% month-on-week. This week the domestic large steel enterprises into the resumption of production cycle, but some small and medium-sized steel enterprises due to energy consumption and crude steel output standards shut down, blast furnace operating rate growth is small. Follow- up, in December, some large state- owned enterprises in northeast and North China will continue to resume and put into production, short-term blast furnace operation rate still has room to rise, good ore prices.
