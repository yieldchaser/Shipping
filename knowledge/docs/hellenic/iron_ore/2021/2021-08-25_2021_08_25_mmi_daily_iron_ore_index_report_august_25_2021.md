## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 25/08/2021
DCE iron ore fluctuated slightly today, the spot quotation in the morning increased by 20-25yuan/mt from yesterdays' morning quotation. Somesteel mills tend to inquiry and traders sell actively that resulted from the previously low market price. Traders tend to give quite lower bargain space today. PBF at Shandong port dealt 1025-1030yuan/mt, up 5-25yuan/mt over yesterday's deal price. One steel mill told that they tend to buy on a frequent basis with less amount under current market situation. Although some BFs overhaul currently, the remaining BFs still support the iron ore demand. In addition, steel price maintained a quite high level with resilient steel price; according to $138 iron ore price, the rebar profits of long-process steel mills has reached 855yuan/ mt while HRC even reached to more than 1200yuan/mt, good steel profit still support iron ore price, to some extent.
