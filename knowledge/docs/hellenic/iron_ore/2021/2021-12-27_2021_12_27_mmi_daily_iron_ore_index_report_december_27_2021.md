## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 27/12/2021
DCE iron ore futures market falling trends by 3.33%.the main contract closed 683. The spot quotation in the morning almost kept steady.but as the disk continues to decline, most traders was decreased by 5-10 yuan/mt. Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 800-810 yuan/mt, decreased 15-25 yuan/mt from last Friday; SSF at Shandong port dealt 495 yuan/mt, decreased 10-15 yuan/mt from last Friday; PBF at Tangshan port dealt 805 yuan/mt, decreased 5-20 yuan/mt from last Friday; SMM analysis, in the recent macro positive background; Superimposed on the completion of the national crude steel reduction task, in January, the blast furnace may be concentrated in the resumption of production, iron ore demand is expected to be strong; At the same time, into January, some steel before the Spring Festival replenishment or will start, good mine prices. But the port inventory is still on the high side, the timber is weak, dragging down iron ore, in the short term price shock operation.
