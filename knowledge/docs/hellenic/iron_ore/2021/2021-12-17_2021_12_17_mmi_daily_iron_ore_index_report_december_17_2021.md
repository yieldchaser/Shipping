## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 17/12/2021
DCE iron ore futures continued to fluctuate in a day, the main contract closed 676.5, an increase of 1.88% throughout the day. The quotationin the morning was increased by 20 yuan/mt over yesterday. some traders were active to sell, steel mills continued to purchas on demand. total transactions is poor. PBF at Shandong port dealt 748 yuan/mt, increased about 10 yuan/mt over yesterday; SSF at Shandong port dealt 479 yuan/mt, increased 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday；PBF at Tangshan port dealt 750 yuan/mt; increased 20 yuan/mt over yesterday; Iron ore inventories across 35 ports tracked by SMM totalled 152.72 million mt, an increase of 2.15 million mt from the previous week. This marked the twelfth consecutive weeks of growth. The daily average shipments from the 35 ports to domestic market continued to increase, up 63,000 mt on a weekly basis to 2.77 million mt last week. The arrivals of goods at portscontinued to increase due to rising shipments from overseas markets after the short-term decline in the arrivals of imported ore arrivals in the previous period, pushing higher the port inventory. Port transportation in Tangshan has recently been restricted again due to the bad weather and strict environmental protection requirement. The daily shipments from thetwo major ports in Tangshan maintained a low of 366,000 mt, while the daily shipments in Tianjin and Shandong continued to rise as some mills turned to restock from these two regions. Port inventory is expected to increase further in the short term due to the Winter Olympics and stricter environmental protection requirement. So the price of ore is still running in shock.
