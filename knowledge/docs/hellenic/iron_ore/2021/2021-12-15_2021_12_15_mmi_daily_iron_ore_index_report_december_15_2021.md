## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 15/12/2021
DCE iron ore futures continued to drop by 2.04% today, the main contract closed 649. The spot quotation in the morning almost keptsteady. Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchasing enthusiasm is not high. total transactions is poor. PBF at Shandong port dealt 715-725 yuan/mt, the same as yesterday; SSF at Shandong port dealt 460-465, the same as yesterday; SMM analysis, today November macro economic data release, real estate data exceeded expectations, boosted market sentiment; But the actual purchase demand of steel mills is still not optimistic, and many places in the country issued a new severe pollution weather warning, the price of ore is not enough power; Iron ore prices in the short term is still a range of shocks.
