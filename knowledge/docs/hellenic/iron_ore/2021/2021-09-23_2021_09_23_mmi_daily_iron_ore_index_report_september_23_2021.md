## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 23/09/2021
According to data tracked by SMM, 59 ships arrived at domestic main ports during September 13-19. Arrivals of cargoes are estimated to standat 8.93 million mt, down 3.63 million mt from the previous session, and down 3.27 million mt year on year. Shipments that departed Australian ports were estimated to increase 1.27 million mt week on week and 3.47 million mt on the year to 18.74 million mt, and that from Brazilian ports rose 230,000 mt on the week and down 490,000 mt on the year to 6.1 million mt.

## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 23/09/2021
According to data tracked by SMM, 59 ships arrived at domestic main ports during September 13-19. Arrivals of cargoes are estimated to standat 8.93 million mt, down 3.63 million mt from the previous session, and down 3.27 million mt year on year. Shipments that departed Australian ports were estimated to increase 1.27 million mt week on week and 3.47 million mt on the year to 18.74 million mt, and that from Brazilian ports rose 230,000 mt on the week and down 490,000 mt on the year to 6.1 million mt. Iron ore arrivals fell significantly from the previous session, but that from Australia and Brazil climbed on the week. While domestically speaking, the output of hot metal is expected to decline further amid production reduction policies. The overall supply of iron ore is likely to remain sufficient in the short term. DCE iron ore futures market today vibrated narrowly and the physical port stock transaction prices for PBF rose by 15-30yuan/mt over yesterday. Traders turned to be cautious in making deals, while mills showed no big interests in purchasing as well, total transactions inched down over yesterday.
