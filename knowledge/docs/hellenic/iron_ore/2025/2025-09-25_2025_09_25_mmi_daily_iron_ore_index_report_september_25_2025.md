in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 25/09/2025
DCE iron ore futures strengthened a er an ini al dip today, holding up well throughout the session. The most-traded contract I2601 finallyclosed at 805.5, up 0.25% from the previous working day. Traders showed moderate willingness to sell; steel mills adopted a cau ous wait-and-see approach, purchasing as needed. Market transac on senment was generally muted.
