## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 07/03/2025
Today, iron ore futures fluctuated repeatedly, with the most-traded I2505 contract finally closing at 774 yuan/mt, up 0.19%for the day. Traders remained less motivated to sell; steel mills conducted limited restocking for rigid demand; and market transaction sentiment was sluggish. ## Main
Today, iron ore futures fluctuated repeatedly, with the most-traded I2505 contract finally closing at 774 yuan/mt, up 0.19%for the day. Traders remained less motivated to sell; steel mills conducted limited restocking for rigid demand; and market transaction sentiment was sluggish. In Shandong, mainstream transaction prices for PB fines were around 773-777 yuan/mt, basically stable compared to yesterday; in Tangshan, According to SMM port inventory data, port arrivals slightly declined this week. However, as ore prices fell, steel mills' restocking enthusiasm increased, driving port pick-up volume to continue rising and leading to a significant drop in port inventory, which provided some support for ore prices. Looking ahead to next week, pig iron production is expected to shift from a decline to an increase, further boosting iron ore demand, and iron ore prices may bottom out.
