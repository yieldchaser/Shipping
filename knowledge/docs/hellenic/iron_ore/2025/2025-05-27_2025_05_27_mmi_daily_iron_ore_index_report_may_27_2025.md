## Main
Today, DCE iron ore futures were in the doldrums, with the most-traded contract I2509 closing at 698.5, down 1.76% for the day. Traders sold goodsin line with market conditions, while steel mills adopted a wait-and-see attitude and purchased as needed. The market transaction atmosphere was average. In the Shandong region, the mainstream transaction prices of PB fines were in the range of 733-740 yuan/mt, down 5-10 yuan/mt from yesterday's prices. In the Tangshan region, the transaction prices of PB fines were around 745-750 yuan/mt, also down 5-10 yuan/mt from yesterday's prices. Recently, iron ore demand has peaked and pulled back, and is expected to continue to decline in the medium and long term. On the supply side, there has been a slight increase. The overall supply-demand imbalance has widened. Meanwhile, demand across the industry is also weakening. The prices of finished steel products have fallen, dragging down ore prices. Market sentiment is relatively pessimistic.
