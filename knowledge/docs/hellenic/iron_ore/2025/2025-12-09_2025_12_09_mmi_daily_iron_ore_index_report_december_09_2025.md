## Main
Today, iron ore continued its weak and fluctuating trend. The main contract I2605 closed at 757.5, down 0.72% comparedto the previous trading day. Traders sold goods in line with market conditions, while steel mills made purchases based on demand, with a relatively positive procurement sentiment. The overall market trading atmosphere was moderate. In the Shandong region, the transaction price for PB fines ranged from 778 to 783 yuan per ton, down 0-3 yuan per ton compared to yesterday. In the Hebei region, the transaction price for PB fines ranged from 785 to 790 yuan per ton, also down 0-3 yuan per ton compared to yesterday. According to SMM statistics, during this week (December 6 to December 12), the impact of blast furnace maintenance on hot metal production was 1.666 million tons, an increase of 121,000 tons compared to last week's maintenance impact. Next week (December 13 to December 19), the impact of blast furnace maintenance on hot metal production is projected to be 1.7156 million tons, an increase of 49,600 tons compared to this week. Hot metal production is expected to decline to its lowest point this month, further weakening the fundamentals and putting pressure on iron ore prices. Tomorrow, the Federal Reserve's interest rate decision is set to be announced, which may cause some disruption to iron ore prices. However, in the short term, iron ore prices are likely to remain in a narrow range with limited fluctuations.
