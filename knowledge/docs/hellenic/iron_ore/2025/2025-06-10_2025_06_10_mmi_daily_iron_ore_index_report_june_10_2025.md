## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 10/06/2025
Today, Dalian iron ore futures jumped initially and then pulled back, operating in the doldrums throughout the day. The most-traded contract I2509 eventuallyclosed at 698.5, with a daily decline of 0.85%. Traders' enthusiasm for selling was moderate, and steel mills mainly purchased as needed. The market transaction atmosphere was average. In the Shandong region, the mainstream transaction price of PB fines was around 720 yuan/mt, slightly dropping by 5-8 yuan/mt from yesterday's price. In the Tangshan region, the transaction price of PB fines was around 735 yuan/mt, slightly dropping by 5-10 yuan/mt from yesterday's price. Optimistic expectations from the morning's China-US-UK talks once drove iron ore prices to rebound slightly. However, as the news flow dried up in the afternoon, market sentiment weakened somewhat, and prices fell under pressure.
