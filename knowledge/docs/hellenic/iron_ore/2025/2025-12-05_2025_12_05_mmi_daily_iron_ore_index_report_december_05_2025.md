## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 05/12/2025
The DCE iron ore futures contract on the DCE traded in the doldrums today. As the contract rollover shi ed to I2605, it closed at769 yuan, down 0.77% from the previous trading day. Traders showed a moderate willingness to sell. Approaching the weekend, steel mills saw a decline in purchasing enthusiasm, resul ng in a sluggish market transac on atmosphere. In Shandong, PB fines traded at 780-787 yuan/mt, down 5-10 yuan/mt from yesterday. In Hebei, PB fines traded at 790-800 yuan/mt, also down 5-10 yuan/mt from yesterday. As of December 5, SMM sta s cs showed that iron ore inventories at 35 ports na onwide totaled 139.82 million mt, an increase of 1.21 million mt WoW. The average daily port pick-up volume this week was 2.686 million mt, down 69,000 mt WoW. Recently, as annual maintenance at blast furnaces in steel mills entered a concentrated period, iron ore demand declined significantly, pu ng pressure on prices. Considering that an important mee ng is scheduled for next week, market senment remains posi ve, and downside room is expected to be limited.
