## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 08/12/2025
Iron ore experienced a weak and vola le trend today, with the main contract I2605 closing at 760.5, down by 1.43% compared to the previous trading day.Traders adjusted their shipments according to market condi ons, while steel mills remained cau ous and took a wait-and-see approach, showing limited purchasing interest. Market transac ons were sluggish. In the Shandong region, the transac on price for PB fines ranged between 780-785 yuan, down by 0-5 yuan per ton from last Friday. In the Hebei region, the transac on price for PB fines ranged between 790-795 yuan per ton, also down by 0-5 yuan per ton from last Friday. Last week, SMM reported that global iron ore shipments totaled 35.87 million tons, an increase of 2.57 million tons from the previous week, represen ng a growth of 7.7%. On the demand side, SMM esmated that the average daily hot metal produc on this week showed a larger decline compared to the previous week. Addi onally, market rumors today suggested that nego a ons between China Minmetals and BHP are nearing comple on. If the nego a ons conclude and restricted products are suddenly released into the market, concerns about poten al selling pressure could further weigh on iron ore prices. Given the ongoing macroeconomic support expected this week, the downside for iron ore prices appears limited, and a weak and vola le trend may persist in the short term.
