## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 03/07/2025
Today, DCE iron ore futures continued to rise, with a significantly expanded increase. The most-traded contract I2509 closedat 733, up 2.45% for the day. Traders actively sold their goods, and speculative trading volume increased. Steel mills purchased as needed, with some locking in profits and increasing purchases. The market transaction atmosphere was moderate. In the Shandong region, the mainstream transaction prices of PB fines were around 721-724 yuan/mt, up 8-10 yuan/mt from yesterday. In the Tangshan region, the transaction prices of PB fines were around 735-740 yuan/mt, up 10-12 yuan/mt from yesterday.
Today, the apparent demand for the five major steel products slightly increased. The total inventory increase was relatively small, and there was no significant rise. Coupled with the recent optimistic market sentiment, it drove iron ore futures to continue rising.
