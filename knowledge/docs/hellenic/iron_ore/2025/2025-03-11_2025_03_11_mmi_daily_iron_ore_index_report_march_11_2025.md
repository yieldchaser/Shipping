## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 11/03/2025
Today, DCE iron ore futures opened lower but closed higher, with the most-traded I2505 contract finally settling at 774.5 yuan/mt, down0.06% for the day. Traders showed moderate enthusiasm for selling; steel mills purchased as needed, with limited inquiries; and the market transaction atmosphere remained sluggish. In Shandong, the mainstream transaction prices for PB fines were around 775 yuan/mt, basically stable compared to yesterday's prices. In Tangshan, PB fines transaction prices were around 780-785 yuan/mt, also stable compared to yesterday. This week, the blast furnace maintenance impact tracked by SMM was 1.5882 million mt, down 25,600 mt WoW. Pig iron production is expected to increase slightly, with next week's estimated blast furnace maintenance impact at 1.3874 million mt, down 200,800 mt WoW. Pig iron production has been continuously increasing, with the growth rate expanding, providing support for ore prices. However, macro sentiment remains pessimistic, posing significant resistance to upward movement in ore prices.
