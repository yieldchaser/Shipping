## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 25/03/2025
Today, DCE Iron ore futures moved downwards after a higher opening, fluctuating upward throughout the day. The most-traded contractI2505 finally closed at 776, with a daily increase of 0.65%. Traders' willingness to sell was moderate; steel mills' inquiries decreased compared to yesterday; at the same time, traders' speculative enthusiasm weakened; the market transaction atmosphere was moderate. The mainstream transaction prices of PB fines in Shandong were around 775 yuan/mt, down 2-5 yuan/mt from yesterday; the transaction prices of PB fines in Tangshan were around 780 yuan/mt, down 5-9 yuan/mt from yesterday. According to SMM statistics, this week (March 22 to March 28), the impact from blast furnace maintenance on pig iron production was 1.3263 million mt, down 124,000 mt WoW. Next week (March 29 to April 4), the impact from blast furnace maintenance on pig iron production will be 1.2318 million mt, down 94,500 mt WoW. Pig iron production will continue to grow in the next two weeks, providing some support for ore prices. However, news of production cuts in Shandong, Jiangsu, and other provinces circulated again during the session, slightly weakening market sentiment.
