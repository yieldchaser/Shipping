## Main
Today, the DCE Iron Ore futures continued to operate in the doldrums, with the most-traded contract I2509 closing at 722.5, down 0.89% for the day.Traders' enthusiasm for selling was moderate; steel mills adopted a cautious wait-and-see attitude and purchased as needed. The market transaction atmosphere was average. In the Shandong region, the mainstream transaction prices of PB fines were around 760-765 yuan/mt, down 0-5 yuan/mt from last Friday. In the Tangshan region, the transaction prices of PB fines were approximately 770-775 yuan/mt, down 5-10 yuan/mt from last Friday. Last week, SMM shipping data showed that global iron ore shipments increased slightly by 2.42 million mt to 34.05 million mt. Among them, shipments from Australia and non-mainstream countries increased slightly. Brazil's shipments decreased slightly. This week, port arrivals decreased slightly by 540,000 mt to 26.27 million mt, with overall supply remaining at a moderate level and having some room for growth in the future. The impact on ore prices was limited. However, today's release of real estate and macro data showed a decline on a WoW basis.
