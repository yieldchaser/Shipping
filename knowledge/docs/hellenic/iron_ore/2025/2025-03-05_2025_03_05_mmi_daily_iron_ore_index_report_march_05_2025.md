## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 05/03/2025
Today, DCE iron ore futures continued to fluctuate downward. The most-traded I2505 contract finally closed at 771 yuan/mt, down 1.34%throughout the day. Traders remained cautious, with some halting offers; steel mills showed stronger wait-and-see sentiment, with fewer inquiries; and the market transaction atmosphere was sluggish. In Shandong, mainstream transaction prices for PB fines were around 775 yuan/mt, down 5 yuan/mt from yesterday; in Tangshan, transaction prices for PB fines were around 785 yuan/mt, also down 5 yuan/mt from yesterday. A key domestic meeting was held today, confirming that crude steel production will continue to be controlled through 2025, further validating previous rumors. Pessimistic sentiment intensified. Coupled with the US imposing additional tariffs on China again, ore prices pulled back to new lows under the dual pressure. Attention is on tomorrow's SMM steel inventory data.
