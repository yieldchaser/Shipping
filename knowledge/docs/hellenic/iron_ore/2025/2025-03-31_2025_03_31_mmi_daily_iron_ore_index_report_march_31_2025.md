## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 31/03/2025
Today, iron ore futures continued to decline weakly. The most-traded contract I2505 closed at 773, down 1.47% for the day.Traders'willingness to sell was poor, steel mills remained cautious and mainly observed, and purchase willingness was moderate. The market transaction atmosphere was relatively flat. In Shandong, the mainstream transaction prices for PB fines were around 773-775 yuan/mt, down 10 yuan/mt WoW. In Tangshan, the transaction prices for PB fines were around 780-785 yuan/mt, down 10 yuan/mt WoW. SMM's global iron ore shipments totaled 33.51 million mt, up 4.4% MoM. Among them, shipments from Australia were 19.24 million mt, up 2.4% MoM, while shipments from Brazil were 7.12 million mt, up 32.8% MoM. However, shipments to China declined in this period. SMM's iron ore arrivals in China totaled 26.98 million mt, down 7.5% MoM but up 15.8% YoY. Shipments have returned to seasonal normal levels, and supply remains loose. Additionally, news about steel exports today caused some disturbance in futures prices. With tariffs expected to be.
