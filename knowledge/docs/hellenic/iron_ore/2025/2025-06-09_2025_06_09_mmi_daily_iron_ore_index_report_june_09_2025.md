## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 09/06/2025
Today, the DCE Iron Ore futures market opened with a strong upward trend but pulled back in the afternoon, after a doldrums session the day before yesterday.The most-traded contract I2509 eventually closed at 703, down 0.71% for the day. Traders sold goods in line with market conditions, while steel mills remained cautious and adopted a wait-and-see aƫtude, focusing on purchasing as needed. The market trading atmosphere was average. In the Shandong region, the mainstream transaction prices of PB fines were in the range of 723-728 yuan/mt, dropping slightly by 5 yuan/mt from Friday's prices. In the Tangshan region, the transaction prices of PB fines were around 740-745 yuan/mt, also dropping slightly by 5 yuan/mt from Friday's prices. SMM shipping data showed that last week, SMM's total global iron ore shipments reached 36.06 million mt, an increase of 1.09 million mt WoW. Among them, shipments from Australia and non-mainstream countries increased, while Brazil's shipments dropped slightly.
