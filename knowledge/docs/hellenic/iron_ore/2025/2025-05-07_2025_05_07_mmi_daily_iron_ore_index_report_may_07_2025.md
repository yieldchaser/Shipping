## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 07/05/2025
Today, DCE Iron Ore futures opened higher and then fluctuated downward, with the most-traded contract I2509 eventually closingat 708, up 0.35% for the day. Traders' enthusiasm for selling was moderate; steel mills adopted a wait-and-see attitude cautiously, with some purchasing as planned, resulting in a generally moderate market transaction atmosphere. In the Shandong region, the mainstream transaction prices of PB fines were around 760-765 yuan/mt, up 0-5 yuan/mt from yesterday's prices; in the Tangshan region, the transaction prices of PB fines were approximately 770-780 yuan/mt, also up 0-5 yuan/mt from yesterday's prices.

## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 07/05/2025
Today, DCE Iron Ore futures opened higher and then fluctuated downward, with the most-traded contract I2509 eventually closingat 708, up 0.35% for the day. Traders' enthusiasm for selling was moderate; steel mills adopted a wait-and-see attitude cautiously, with some purchasing as planned, resulting in a generally moderate market transaction atmosphere. This morning, at a State Council press conference, a series of macro stimulus policy packages were announced, significantly boosting market risk appetite. Meanwhile, the market once again received key signals indicating that the policy to reduce crude steel production was about to be implemented.
