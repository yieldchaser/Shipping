## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 22/10/2025
On October 22, the closing price of the DCE I2601 contract was 774 yuan/mt, up 0.65% from the previous trading day. Mainstream spot cargoat ports rose by 2-3 yuan from the previous working day, with transac on prices for PB fines in the Shandong region at 777-783 yuan, up 5 yuan/mt from yesterday; transac on prices for PB fines in the Hebei region were 790-793 yuan, up 2-3 yuan from the previous day. Inquiries were made today, but spot transac ons were limited, with traders showing moderate enthusiasm in offering prices and mainly selling at suitable prices.

## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 22/10/2025
On October 22, the closing price of the DCE I2601 contract was 774 yuan/mt, up 0.65% from the previous trading day. Mainstream spot cargoat ports rose by 2-3 yuan from the previous working day, with transac on prices for PB fines in the Shandong region at 777-783 yuan, up 5 yuan/mt from yesterday; transac on prices for PB fines in the Hebei region were 790-793 yuan, up 2-3 yuan from the previous day. Inquiries were made today, but spot transac ons were limited, with traders showing moderate enthusiasm in offering prices and mainly selling at suitable prices. Fundamentally, steel mill profits con nued to narrow, while the blast furnace opera ng rate improved slightly, providing weak support for iron ore demand. In terms of supply, port unloading efficiency rebounded, and supply is expected to increase in the near term. Overall, fundamental support weakened again. On the news front, the US Fed Watch raised expecta ons for a US Fed interest rate cut to 98.9%, boos ng market senment. Combined with posi ve market expecta ons for subsequent mee ngs, iron ore prices are expected to hold up well.
