## Main
This morning, the iron ore futures continued to hit new lows, with a slight rebound in the afternoon. The most-traded I2505 contract closed at 762,down 0.46% for the day. Traders sold according to market conditions; steel mills were cautious and observed, with moderate purchase willingness; the overall trading atmosphere was average. In Shandong, the mainstream transaction prices of PB fines were around 760-765 yuan/mt, basically stable compared to yesterday's prices; in Tangshan, the transaction prices of PB fines were around 770-772 yuan/mt, also basically stable compared to yesterday. This week, the steel industry data was released, showing that the apparent demand for the five major steel products continued to grow. Inventory levels continued to decline. In particular, the apparent demand for rebar continued to increase, with a moderate destocking rate. This provided some support to the futures market. However, there were concerns about negative feedback in the market, and it was expected that ore prices would struggle to strengthen in the short term.
