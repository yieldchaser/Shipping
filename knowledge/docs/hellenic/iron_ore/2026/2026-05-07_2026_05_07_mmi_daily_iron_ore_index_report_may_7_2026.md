## Main
On May 7, 2026, the iron ore market showed a volatile but strengthening trend with intense trading activity.The main contract I2609 finally closed at 817 yuan/ton, up 0.62% from the previous trading session. Port spot prices remained largely unchanged from the previous day. Traders showed moderate quoting activity, while steel mills were cautious in their purchases; overall spot market transaction sentiment was average. At present, the supply and demand fundamentals of iron ore remain stable. However, oil prices have impacted the shipping market, leading to a sharp rise in iron ore freight costs today, further adding pressure on the cost side of iron ore prices. Meanwhile, according to reports, the United States and Iran are negotiating to resolve conflicts and open the Strait of Hormuz, resulting in oil prices falling for three consecutive days. This news may boost market sentiment in the short term, providing positive emotional support for iron ore prices.
