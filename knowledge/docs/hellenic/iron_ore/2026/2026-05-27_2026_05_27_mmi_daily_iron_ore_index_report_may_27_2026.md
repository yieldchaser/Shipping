## Main
Today, the iron ore futures market showed weak fluctuations. The main contract I2609 finally closed at 781.5RMB/ton, down slightly by 0.32% from the previous trading session. Port spot prices remained largely unchanged from the previous day. Traders showed moderate enthusiasm in quoting; steel mills made few inquiries, with a stronger wait-and-see sentiment and cautious procurement. As of now, spot market transactions are limited. PB fines at Shandong port traded at 753 RMB/ton, while Mac fines at Caofeidian port traded at 763 RMB/ton. According to an SMM survey on May 27, the blast furnace operating rate of 242 steel mills stood at 89.70%, up 0.15 percentage points from the previous period. The average daily hot metal output of sample steel mills was 2.4281 million tons, up 2,300 tons from the previous period. This week, one blast furnace entered maintenance while three resumed production, mainly in Hebei and Henan. Recently, blast furnace restarts have led to an overall upward trend in hot metal output. The increased demand for iron ore provides bottom support for ore prices. However, as the off-season for end-user demand deepens, demand is expected to weaken further. It is anticipated that iron ore prices will continue to fluctuate weakly in the short term.
