## Main
Iron ore futures showed strength today, with the main contract I2605 ulimately closing at 795.5 RMB per ton,up 1.34% from the previous trading session. Meanwhile, spot prices increased by 5-10 RMB compared to the previous trading day. Traders showed moderate enthusiasm in quoting prices, while steel mills made fewer inquiries. Overall spot market transactions remained limited. This week, total inventory at 10 key ports reached 118.99 million tons, a sequential increase of 1.08 million tons. Notably, there is a clear destocking trend for Carajas fines and PB lumps, reflecting growing market demand for high-grade iron ore. Looking ahead, news regarding the implementation of previously reported structural supply shortages has begun to emerge, intensifying market anxiety over shortages of certain iron ore varieties.
