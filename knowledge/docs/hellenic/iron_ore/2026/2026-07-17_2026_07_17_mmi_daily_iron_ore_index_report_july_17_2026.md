## Main
The iron ore market traded sideways today, with the DCE main contract I2609 eventually closing at 762 RMB/ton, up 0.53% from the previous day'sclosing price. During the same period, the average daily port oŏake fell slightly by 55,000 tonnes to 3.22 million tonnes.
