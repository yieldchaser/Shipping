## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 25/08/2023
DCE iron ore futures tight by 0.55%. the main contract I2401 closed 811, Traders ship according to market conditions,Some steel mills purchasing enthusiasm. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 890-895 yuan/mt; decreased 5 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 885-894 yuan/mt; decreased 1-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. As of August 25th, the total inventory of 35 ports tracked by SMM was 117.11 million tons, a decrease of 230000 tons compared to last week and 19.11 million tons compared to the same period last year. The daily average port clearance volume of imported ore in this period increased by 9000 tons to 3.183 million tons on a weekly basis, with the port clearance volume increasing for three consecutive periods, mainly supported by the high iron content of blast furnace steel plants. It also indicates that the demand for iron ore is at a high level, supporƟng the rise of ore prices. Next week, the demand for iron ore will remain high, but the implementation of the policy of reducing crude steel may have a certain impact on steel mill production. The demand for iron ore may decrease, and it is expected that ore prices will fluctuate.
