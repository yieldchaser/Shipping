## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 18/07/2023
DCE iron ore futures rose by 0.66% today, the main contract closed at 845.The traders' willingness to ship is general.The steel mills are active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is normal.PBF at Tangshan port deal 880 yuan/mt,increase 7-8 yuan/mt. Some futures companies have received notification from the Daishang Exchange that their supervision of iron ore holdings is becoming stricter. Today's speech at the regular meeting of the National Development market sentiment. Currently, both supply and demand are strong, and iron ore prices are supported. However, the profits of blast furnace steel mills continue to shrink, and attention needs to be paid to the enthusiasm for later production and procurement of raw materials. In the short term, attention should be paid to the risk of a pullback in ore prices.
