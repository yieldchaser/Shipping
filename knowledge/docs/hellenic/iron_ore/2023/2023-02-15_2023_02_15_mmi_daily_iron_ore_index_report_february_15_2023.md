## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 15/02/2023
DCE iron ore futures rose by 2.18 % today, the main contract closed 865.5.The traders' willingness to ship is more.The steel mills are active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is better.PBF at Shandong port deal 875-880 yuan/mt, increase 6-20 yuan/ mt.PBF at Tangshan port deal 884-888 yuan/mt,increase 4-8 yuan /mt. ## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 15/02/2023
DCE iron ore futures rose by 2.18 % today, the main contract closed 865.5.The traders' willingness to ship is more.The steel mills are active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is better.PBF at Shandong port deal 875-880 yuan/mt, increase 6-20 yuan/ mt.PBF at Tangshan port deal 884-888 yuan/mt,increase 4-8 yuan /mt. On the news side, the US CPI data exceeded the market expectations, but the recovery of domestic downstream data was still slow. As of today, according to SMM statistics, the operating rate of blast furnace is 92.12%, up 0.45% from the previous period, and the utilization rate of blast furnace capacity is up 0.6% to 93.05% from the previous period. As the blast furnace has resumed production, the daily average output of molten iron in the steel plant has risen slightly, and the iron ore inventory in the steel plant continues to be consumed. At present, the iron ore price is supported by the fundamentals, and the falling space is relatively limited. In the short term, the ore price is mainly volatile.
