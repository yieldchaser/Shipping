## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 13/12/2023
DCE iron ore futures fall today. the main contract I2405 closed 948. some traders were negative to sell,some traders do not sell temporarily due to low volume of goods.

## Main
DCE iron ore futures fall today. the main contract I2405 closed 948. some traders were negative to sell,some traders do not sell temporarily due to low volume of goods. Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchased on demand. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 995-998 yuan/mt; decreased 5-15 yuan/mt over yesterday. SSF at Shandong port dealt 883; decreased 7 yuan/mt over yesterday. The Central Economic Conference has ended, and the content of the meeting is slightly lower than market expectations. Macro drivers have weakened, and market sentiment has slightly declined. However, from a fundamental perspective, the operating rate of blast furnaces according to SMM statistics has slightly increased, with a month on month increase of 10500 tons in molten iron production. As New Year's Day approaches, some steel mills in certain regions have expectations of restocking, which still provides support for ore prices. Expected to continue oscillating in the short term.
