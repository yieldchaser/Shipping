## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 17/08/2023
DCE iron ore futures rose by 4.34 % today, the main contract closed at 768.5. The traders' willingness to ship is general. The steel mills are active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is better.PBF at Shandong port deal 860 yuan/mt,increase 10 yuan/mt. PBF at Tangshan port deal 855 yuan/mt. On the current fundamentals, there is a significant increase in the arrival of iron ore at ports, while the demand side is supported by the high iron content of steel mills and blast furnaces, making iron ore prices easy to rise but difficult to fall. However, considering the flat control policy for crude steel, steel mills have a cautious aƫtude towards raw material procurement, and they need to pay attention to the actual situation of steel mill profits and policy implementation in the future.
