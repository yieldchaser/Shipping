## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 22/03/2023
DCE iron ore futures declined by 2.15% today, the main contract closed at 865.5.The traders' willingness to ship is more. The steel mills aremore active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is general.PBF at Shandong port deal 880-892 yuan/mt, decrease 1-13 yuan/ mt.PBF at Tangshan port deal 895 yuan/mt,decrease 20 yuan/mt. From the perspective of the news, overseas economic events continue to affect domestic market sentiment, and the iron ore futures market conƟnues to weaken. Fundamentally, according to SMM statistics, the blast furnace operating rate was 93.46%, up 0.3% from the previous period; The utilization rate of blast furnace capacity increased by 0.35% to 94.23% on a month-on-month basis, and the current amount of molten iron in the blast furnace provides some support to the ore price. Pessimistic news such as pressure cuts, production restrictions, regulation, and external market declines have put pressure on ore prices, which are expected to be dominated by range fluctuations in the short term.
