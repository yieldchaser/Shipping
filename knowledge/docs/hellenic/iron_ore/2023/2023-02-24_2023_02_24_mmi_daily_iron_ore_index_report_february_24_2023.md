## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 24/02/2023
DCE iron ore futures declined by 0.22% today, the main contract closed 909.5.The traders' willingness to ship is less.The steel mills are wait and see .The overall trading sentiment of the market is worse.PBF at Shandong port deal 910-916 yuan/mt;PBF at Tangshan port deal 925 yuan/mt . As of today, the inventory of 35 ports tracked by SMM totaled 138.11 million tons, a cumulative of 1 million tons compared with last week and a decrease of 15.13 million tons compared with the same period last year. The average daily port dredging volume of imported mines in this period increased by 45000 tons to 3.078 million tons on a weekly basis, and the port dredging volume increased for four consecutive periods, and currently returned to a relatively high level, while the port inventory increased for five consecutive periods. The increase in inventory this week was mainly due to the unloading and warehousing of ships in port. Iron ore dredging is limited by downstream demand and steel mill profits, and the growth space is questionable. However, considering the optimistic expectations before the national meeting and the low supply data, iron ore prices are mainly subject to strong shocks in the short term.
