## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 14/03/2023
DCE iron ore futures declined by 0.22% today, the main contract closed at 920.The traders' willingness to ship is more.The steel mills aremore active to purchase.The overall trading sentement of the market is general. PBF at Shandong port deal 933-940 yuan/mt, increase 2-15yuan/mt. PBF at Tangshan port deal 945-950 yuan/mt,decrease 2-5 yuan/mt. According to today's news, there are many rumours of limited production in the market, but the authenticity is questionable, which only has a certain impact on the market mentality, resulting in large fluctuations in the market trend. According to SMM statistics, the impact of blast furnace maintenance on molten iron this week was 1.0962 million tons, 42400 tons less than that of last week. It is expected that the impact of blast furnace maintenance will still drop slightly next week. At present, the supply and demand pattern of fundamentals has not changed much, and there is no obvious directional policy. It is expected that iron ore prices will still fluctuate in the short term.
