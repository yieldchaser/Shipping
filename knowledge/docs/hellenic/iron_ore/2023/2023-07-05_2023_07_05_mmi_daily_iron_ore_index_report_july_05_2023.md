## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 05/07/2023
DCE iron ore futures rose by 0.073 % today, the main contract closed at 828.The traders' willingness to ship is less.The steel mills are active to purchase. The overall trading sentiment of the market is general. PBF at Shandong port deal 865- 867 yuan/mt. PBF at Tangshan port deal 892 yuan/mt. The global iron ore transportation volume on the supply side is relatively high, while steel mills still have a high enthusiasm for increasing profits and production, and the demand for iron ore is supported. However, Tangshan Steel Plant's grading restrictions on sintering machine production are expected to last for up to one month, so as sintering ore consumption increases or the demand for lump ore pellets increases. Currently, the contradiction between supply and demand has not fully accumulated, and ore prices are still mainly fluctuating at high levels.
