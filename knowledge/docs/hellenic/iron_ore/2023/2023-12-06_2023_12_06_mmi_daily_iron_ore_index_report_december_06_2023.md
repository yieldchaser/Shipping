## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 06/12/2023
DCE iron ore futures rose by 1.87 % today, the main contract closed at 980. The steel mills are not active to purchase.The overalltrading sentiment of the market is general. PBF at Shandong port deal 970 yuan/mt. According to SMM research, as of today, the operating rate of blast furnace steel plants and the utilization rate of blast furnace capacity have both decreased month on month compared to the previous period; The daily average iron production of the sample steel plant was 2.1578 million tons, a decrease of 30300 tons compared to the previous period, and the demand side's support for ore prices weakened. However, the market has raised its tone towards next year's economic growth. If we maintain our economic growth goals or place more emphasis on introducing stimulus policies, this sentiment will also drive the market higher today. Considering the subsequent replenishment of raw materials and strong expectations, the short-term market may focus on optimistic sentiment around news and consumption, while mining prices fluctuate widely.
