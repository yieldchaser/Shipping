## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 14/02/2023
DCE iron ore futures rose by 0.41 % today, the main contract closed 856.5.The traders' willingness to ship is more.The steel mills are active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is better.PBF at Shandong port deal 860-869 yuan/mt, increase 4-14 yuan/ mt.PBF at Tangshan port deal 880 yuan/mt,increase 5-17 yuan /mt. The favorable policies of real estate infrastructure construction in various regions have increased, making the market still have some optimistic expectations for iron ore demand. However, the downstream startup is relatively slow, the increase of molten iron output of blast furnace is limited, and the port inventory is accumulated, limiting the upward height of iron ore prices.

## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 14/02/2023
DCE iron ore futures rose by 0.41 % today, the main contract closed 856.5.The traders' willingness to ship is more.The steel mills are active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is better.PBF at Shandong port deal 860-869 yuan/mt, increase 4-14 yuan/ mt.PBF at Tangshan port deal 880 yuan/mt,increase 5-17 yuan /mt. The favorable policies of real estate infrastructure construction in various regions have increased, making the market still have some optimistic expectations for iron ore demand. However, the downstream startup is relatively slow, the increase of molten iron output of blast furnace is limited, and the port inventory is accumulated, limiting the upward height of iron ore prices. The current expectation and reality have not been falsified, and the market mentality will still be adjusted in the short term. Iron ore prices are expected to be dominated by shocks.
