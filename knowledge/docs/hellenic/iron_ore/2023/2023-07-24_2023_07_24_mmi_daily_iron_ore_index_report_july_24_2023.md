## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 24/07/2023
DCE iron ore futures continued to ﬂuctuate in a day by 0.06%.the main contract I2309 closed 843. some traders were activeto sell, but Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchasing enthusiasm is not high. total transactions remained insipid.PBF at Shandong port dealt 880 yuan/mt; increased 5 yuan/mt from last Friday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 900-903 yuan/mt; increased 0-3 yuan/mt from last Friday. The total global iron ore shipment volume of SMM is 29.65 million tons, a decrease of 1.8% compared to the previous month. Among them, 15.97 million tons were shipped from Australia, a decrease of 10.5% compared to the previous month; Brazil shipped 7.67 million tons, an increase of 9.2% month on month; The total amount of iron ore delivered to port by SMM China was 24.5723 million tons, an increase of 2.9% compared to the previous month. The arrival volume has slightly increased. On the demand side, aﬀected by Tangshan's environmental protection production restrictions, the number of blast furnace shutdowns in steel mills continues to increase. The supply increase and demand decrease have weakened the support for ore prices. However, as the end of the month approaches, market expectations for important meetings remain strong, and market sentiment remains strong.
