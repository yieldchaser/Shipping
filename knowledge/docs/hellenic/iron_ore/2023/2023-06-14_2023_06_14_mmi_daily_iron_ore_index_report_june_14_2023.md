## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 14/06/2023
DCE iron ore futures rose by 1.51% today, the main contract closed at 804.5. The traders' willingness to ship is general. The steel mills are activeto purchase. The overall trading sentiment of the market is general. PBF at Shandong port deal 855 yuan/mt,increase 12 yuan/mt. PBF at Tangshan port deal 866-870 yuan/mt,increase 2-14 yuan/mt. On the surface of the news, the National Development and Reform Commission held a regular policy briefing today to prohibit the demand for widespread producƟon and business suspension, and to adopt differentiated regulatory measures. The market still has optimistic expectations for other stimulus policies. SMM understands that the recent poor air in Hebei will lead to a round of ozone pollution in Qinhuangdao City, so the production of sintering machines in some steel enterprises is restricted, and it is expected that the demand for lump ores will still be supported. Considering the release of macro data tomorrow, market sentiment is still acceptable, and it is expected that short-term mining prices will continue to fluctuate.
