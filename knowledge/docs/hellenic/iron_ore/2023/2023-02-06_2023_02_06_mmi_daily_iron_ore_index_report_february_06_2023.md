## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 06/02/2023
DCE iron ore futures market falling trends,and start to rebounded in the afternoon. the main contract closed853.5 by 0.89%. some traders were negative to sell, Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchasing enthusiasm is not high. PBF at Shandong port dealt 853-865 yuan/mt; the same as yesterday; the same as last Friday; PBF at Tangshan port dealt 860 yuan/mt; the same as last Friday. According to SMM, the global iron ore shipments totaled 27.93 million tons, up 3.8% month-on-month. Australia's traffic volume remained stable, while Brazil's traffic volume recovered, rising to 5.05 million tons from 3.88 million tons last week. However, it is still at a low shipping level. At present, the demand side is relatively stable, but considering that most of the stock preparation before the festival is after the Lantern Festival, the purchase volume of steel mills will increase this week, supporting the ore price. However, considering that the demand for finished products has not yet recovered, and the pressure of steel mills has greatly aﬀected the enthusiasm of production, it is expected that the short-term ore price will still be dominated by volatile operation.
