## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 03/08/2023
DCE iron ore futures declined by 2.99 % today, the main contract closed at 810.5. The traders' willingnessto ship is general. The steel mills are not active to purchase. The overall trading sentiment of the market is less.PBF at Shandong port deal 860 yuan/mt,decrease 7 yuan/mt. PBF at Tangshan port deal 845 yuan/mt,decrease 22 yuan/mt. Yesterday's market news after the close of the market studied the impact of futures on the steel market, and the market discussed whether to cancel the night trading of iron ore. In addition, the recent news of crude steel pressure reduction has also had an impact on market sentiment, leading to a downward trend in futures trading. However, in the short term, no latest documents have been issued, and iron ore prices still depend on the intensity of production restrictions. In the face of uncertainty, the logic of iron ore prices mainly depends on fundamentals. Short term ore prices may experience a slight decline or rebound.
