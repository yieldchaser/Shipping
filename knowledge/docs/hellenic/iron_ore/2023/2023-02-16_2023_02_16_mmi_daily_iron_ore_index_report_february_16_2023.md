## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 16/02/2023
DCE iron ore futures rose by 1.79 % today, the main contract closed 880.The traders' willingness to ship is more. The steel mills are active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is better.PBF at Shandong port deal 882-893 yuan/mt, increase 2-18 yuan/ mt.PBF at Tangshan port deal 883 yuan/mt. At present, there are rumors on the news side, and the market sentiment ﬂuctuates slightly. However, the current fundamentals have not changed much, and the demand side supports the ore price. According to the inventory of ten major ports tracked by SMM, the total inventory of major varieties accumulated slightly on Thursday, and the demand for refined powder and lump ore improved to a certain extent. In recent months, the four major varieties have taken turns to go to the warehouse. The steel plant's profit is relatively general, and the purchase is more cautious. It mainly consumes the stock of raw materials in the plant, limiting the upward height of ore prices. Overall, iron ore prices will continue to ﬂuctuate.
