## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 16/08/2023
DCE iron ore futures rose by 0.82 % today, the main contract closed at 738.5. The traders' willingness to ship is general.The steel mills are active to purchase. The overall trading sentiment of the market is better. Today, there is news of steel mill level control in the market, which has been subdivided into specific production reductions and time periods. However, according to SMM research, no clear notice has been received yet. The profit margin at the end of the steel mill is acceptable, and high iron ore prices are supported by high-speed rail, and it is difficult to have negative feedback on the market. In the context of uncertain level control news, market senƟment is greatly affected by macro factors, with short-term ore prices mainly operating in a volatile trend.
