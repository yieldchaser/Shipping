## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 31/10/2023
DCE iron ore futures first fell and then rose. the main contract I2401 closed 898.5. an increaseof 0.34% throughout the day. some traders were negative to sell, Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 953-958 yuan/mt; increased 3 yuan/mt over yesterday. The manufacturing PMI in October 2023 was 49.5, a decrease of 0.7 percentage points compared to the previous month. The PMI of the steel industry was 45.6%, a decrease of 0.2 percentage points compared to the previous month. The steel industry is showing weak operation. Dragging the overall downward trend of the black series. However, there is still strong support for the fundamentals and macro aspects of iron ore due to the impact of high-speed iron ore and previous macroeconomic policies. With the fading of optimism, iron ore prices are expected to remain weak and volatile in the short term.
