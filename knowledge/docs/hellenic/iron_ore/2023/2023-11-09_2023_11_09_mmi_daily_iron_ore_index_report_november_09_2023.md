## Main
DCE iron ore futures rose by 1.79 % today, the main contract closed at 939. The steel mills are notactive to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is less. PBF at Shandong port deal 965-967 yuan/mt. PBF at Tangshan port deal 982 yuan/ mt.According to the inventory data of the ten ports tracked by SMM, there has been a significant accumulation of expensive products this week, resulting in a small accumulation of overall inventory. However, due to normal production of sintering machines, the demand for fines has also increased. In the face of poor profits in steel mills, high cost performance varieties are still favored. And this week, the number of blast furnace repairs in steel mills has increased, the fundamentals have weakened, and there is limited space above iron ore prices. Considering the consumption of macroeconomic policies, but with continuous market news, it is expected that mineral prices will struggle to decline smoothly, and may mainly fluctuate in the short term.
