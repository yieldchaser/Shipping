## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 18/08/2023
DCE iron ore futures rose by 2.94% today, the main contract closed at 771.5. The traders' willingness to ship is general. The steel mills are active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is less. PBF at Shandong port deal 852-859 yuan/mt,increase 1-2 yuan/mt. PBF at Tangshan port deal 864 yuan/mt. This week, the inventory of 35 major ports counted by SMM has slightly accumulated to 117 million tons. The high production of blast furnace molten iron at the steel plant end supports the demand for raw materials. Coupled with the approaching delivery month of the 09 contract, the basis difference between futures and cash has gradually narrowed, and there is some speculative demand from market traders, resulting in a continuous increase in port dredging volume to the highest level in nearly a year. As the delivery month approaches, there is a drive to repair the basis for near month contracts, and the flat control policy is expected to be fulfilled by far month contracts. However, the short-term rise in spot prices of iron ore ports is gradually lacking momentum, and it is necessary to pay attention to changes in the news in the future. It is expected that the main trend will be broad fluctuations in ore prices next week.
