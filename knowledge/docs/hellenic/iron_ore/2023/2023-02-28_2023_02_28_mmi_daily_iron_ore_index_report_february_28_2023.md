## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 28/02/2023
DCE iron ore futures weakly again，the main contract closed 888.5，an decrease of 0.78% throughout the day; some traderswere negative to sell, Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchasing enthusiasm is not high. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 891-895 yuan/mt; decreased 2-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 893-905 yuan/mt; decreased 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. Recently aﬀected by environmental restrictions in Hebei, iron ore demand has declined, and spot transactions are mainly in Shandong ports. In the short term, the iron ore fundamental contradiction is not big, the price is mainly aﬀected by the material demand and macro policy factors. The latter stage mainly focuses on the recovery of terminal demand, and it is expected that the mine construction will continue to run weakly and shockingly tomorrow.
