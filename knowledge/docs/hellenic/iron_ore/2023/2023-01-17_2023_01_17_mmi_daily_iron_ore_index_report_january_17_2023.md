## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 17/01/2023
DCE iron ore futures declined by 1.3 % today, the main contract closed 835.The traders' willingness to shipis less.The steel mills are wait and see .The overall trading sentiment of the market is worse.PBF at Shandong port deal 838 yuan/mt; PBF at Tangshan port dealt 840 yuan/mt. From the macro perspective, the GDP growth rate in 2022 was 3%, lower than the market expectation. However, considering that the market's vision has shifted to 2023, the impact on the market is relatively limited. Basically, according to SMM statistics, the impact of blast furnace overhaul on molten iron this week was 171.57 tons, a decrease of 134700 tons compared with last week's overhaul. The impact of blast furnace overhaul on molten iron will drop again next week. In the near future, the production of molten iron has slowly recovered, and the space for steel plant replenishment has again decreased. Due to the recent decline in ore prices, there is a demand for a small amount of procurement to reduce costs. However, in view of the logistics holidays before the festival, which may aﬀect transportation, in the short term, iron ore prices continue to ﬂuctuate.
