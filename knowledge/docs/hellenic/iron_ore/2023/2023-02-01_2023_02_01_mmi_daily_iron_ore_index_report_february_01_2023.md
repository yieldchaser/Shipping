## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 01/02/2023
DCE iron ore futures declined by 0.69% today, the main contract closed 867.The traders' willingnessto ship is more. The steel mills are active to purchase .The overall trading sentiment of the market is better.PBF at Shandong port deal 880-885 yuan/mt, increase 1-4 yuan/ mt.PBF at Tangshan port deal 880-885 yuan/mt,decrease 5-10yuan /mt. The Federal Reserve will announce its interest rate decision on Wednesday afternoon, US Eastern Time. The market is generally expected to raise interest rates by 25 basis points. The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve suggested that the suspension of interest rate increase might be in May, and the market's mood ﬂuctuated more. On the demand side, in addition to the replenishment of steel plants, the recovery of the daily average iron ore volume also supports the iron ore consumption. However, the downstream demand of the industrial chain is weak, and the supporting strength of the raw material price needs to be verified. It is expected that the ore price will remain volatile in the short term.
