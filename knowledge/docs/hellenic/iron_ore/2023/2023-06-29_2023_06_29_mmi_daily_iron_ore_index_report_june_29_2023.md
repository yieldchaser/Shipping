in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 29/06/2023

## Main
DCE iron ore futures tight by 0.85%. some traders were active to sell, steel mills purchased on demand, and purchasingenthusiasm have improved. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 875-880 yuan/mt; increased 0-5 yuan/ mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 885-900 yuan/mt; increased 0-5 yuan/mt over yesterday. SFF at Tangshan port dealt 740 yuan/mt; increased 8 yuan/mt over yesterday. Today, the apparent demand for steel has significantly decreased compared to the previous month, which is lower than market expectations. However, the overall inventory of steel, especially for building materials and hot coil inventory, remains relatively low year-on-year, leading to relatively optimistic market sentiment towards terminal demand; With the convening of important meetings in July, policy expectaƟons are strong. Iron ore is still operating with strong oscillaƟons. In the later stage, the production of molten iron has reached its peak periodically, and there is still room for the increase in iron ore supply. The ore price may fluctuate under pressure.
