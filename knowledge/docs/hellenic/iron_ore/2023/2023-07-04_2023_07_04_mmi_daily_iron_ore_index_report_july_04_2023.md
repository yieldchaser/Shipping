## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 04/07/2023
DCE iron ore futures declined by 0.06 % today, the main contract closed at 821.The traders' willingness to ship is less.The steel mills are not active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is general. PBF at Shandong port deal 865 yuan/mt. PBF at Tangshan port deal 885-892 yuan/mt. According to SMM's statistics on blast furnace maintenance, the impact of this week's maintenance on molten iron is 732000 tons, an increase of 89500 tons compared to last week's maintenance. It is expected that the impact of blast furnace maintenance will further increase next week. On the supply side, it is understood that there may be maintenance plans at Australian ports, which may affect certain shipments. The news today is relatively calm, and the market's discussion of production restrictions has calmed down. However, with the support of high-speed rail, iron ore prices may still face a dilemma, with high volatility being the main focus.
