## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 17/07/2023
DCE iron ore futures declined by 0.89% today, the main contract closed at 832.5. The traders' willingnessto ship is general.The steel mills are active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is normal.PBF at Tangshan port deal 872-873 yuan/mt,decrease 10-11yuan/mt. On the supply side, the total global shipment volume has rebounded, and the shipment volume from Australia and Brazil has increased. However, this week, the port volume slightly decreased by 7.8%. According to the demand side data tracked by SMM, the number of blast furnace maintenance has increased again this week, and the production of molten iron has dropped from a high level. The oﬀ-season demand downstream of the industrial chain is weak, and the profits of steel mills have narrowed. Considering that the market is often inﬂuenced by news and the market expecta- tions are highly volatile, it is expected that iron ore prices will mainly experience a correction in the short term.
