## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 27/04/2023
DCE iron ore futures tight by 0.56%. the main contract closed 713.5. Towards the end of the month,some traders' deliveries have led to an increase in speculative demand. The steel factory needs to replenish the stock just before the holiday, and the overall transaction atmosphere in the market is still good. PBF at Shandong port dealt 819-822 yuan/mt; increased 10-12 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 825-827 yuan/mt; increased 7-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. SSF at Tangshan port dealt 683 yuan/mt; increased 10 yuan/mt over yesterday. Today, there has been a slight improvement in the apparent demand for building materials, but the increase is limited. The market interpretation is that the holiday restocking is not as expected and is still relatively empty.
