## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 31/07/2023
DCE iron ore futures tight by 0.54%. the main contract I 2309 closed 841.5. some traders were active to sell,There are many inquiries from steel mills, but the wait-and-see sentiment increases as prices rise. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 866-875 yuan/mt; prices are mixed today from last Friday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 875-880 yuan/mt; the same as last Friday. There are three main reasons for the rebound in ore prices today: 1. Tangshan Environmental ProtecƟon Production Restriction has ended, the production of discontinued blast furnaces and sintering machines has resumed, and the production of molten iron has slightly increased. 2. PMI manufacturing data exceeded expectations. 3. Relevant departments have released news to promote consumption, boosting market confidence. During this period, news of crude steel pressure reduction interspersed, and iron ore prices fluctuated. In the later stage, mining prices are under pressure and difficult to rise. Or continue the volatile trend.
