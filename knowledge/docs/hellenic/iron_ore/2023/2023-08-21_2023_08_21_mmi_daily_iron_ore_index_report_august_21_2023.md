## Main
DCE iron ore futures continued to ﬂuctuate in a day, the main contract I2401 closed 777, an increase only 0.91% through- out the day;some traders were active to sell, Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, but purchasing enthusiasm has improved, purchased on demand. Tangshan port dealt 865-870 yuan/mt; increased 5yuan/ cycle, and iron ore supply continues to ease. In the short term, The trend of iron ore prices comes more from the short-term stimulus of macro policies and news, and it is expected that the mine price will continue to run strongly and shockingly tomorrow.
