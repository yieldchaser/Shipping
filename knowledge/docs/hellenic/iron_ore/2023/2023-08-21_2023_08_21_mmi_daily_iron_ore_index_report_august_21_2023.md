## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 21/08/2023
DCE iron ore futures continued to ﬂuctuate in a day, the main contract I2401 closed 777, an increase only 0.91% through- out the day;some traders were active to sell, Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, but purchasing enthusiasm has improved, purchased on demand. Today the overall market transaction atmosphere is acceptable. PBF at Shandong port dealt 865-870 yuan/mt; increased 5-12 yuan/mt from last Friday. Tangshan port dealt 865-870 yuan/mt; increased 5yuan/ cycle, and iron ore supply continues to ease. However, hot metal production is at a high level, and the fundamentals are still strong for iron ore prices. In the short term, The trend of iron ore prices comes more from the short-term stimulus of macro policies and news, and it is expected that the mine price will continue to run strongly and shockingly tomorrow.
