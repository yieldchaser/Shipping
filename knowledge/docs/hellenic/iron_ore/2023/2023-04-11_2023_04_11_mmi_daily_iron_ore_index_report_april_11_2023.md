## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 11/04/2023
DCE iron ore futures open lower and go higher, the main contract I 2309 closed 798.5, an increase of 1.53% throughout the day.some traders were active to sell, Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchasing enthusiasm is not high. Today the overall market transaction atmosphere is acceptable. PBF at Shandong port dealt 885-896 yuan/mt; prices are mixed today over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 897-900 yuan/mt; prices are mixed today over yesterday. Australia's main iron ore shipping port Hedland will clear anchorage ships tomorrow due to a severe cyclone and heavy rain, which is expected to reduce Australian shipments this week. Lead to more bullish sentiment in the iron ore market, combined with high iron ore demand to support iron ore prices, iron ore prices rose sharply in the afternoon. Taking into account the recent weak building materials and coke prices or the formation of a drag on the iron ore price, the limited room for the rise of the mine price, the mine price is expected to be mainly run in shock tomorrow.
