## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 25/04/2023
DCE iron ore futures fall today, the main contract closed 711, an decrease of 1.86% throughout the day;some traders were negative to sell, Some steel mills purchasing enthusiasm is not high. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 805-810 yuan/ mt; decreased 0-5 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 817-820 yuan/mt; decreased 5-8 yuan/mt over yesterday. Today, the main steel mills of Northwest United Steel have all reduced producƟon by 30%, and major steel mills have reported maintenance plans for their blast furnaces, leading to another decline in demand for iron ore. During the first quarter economic operation symposium of some steel enterprises held by the China Iron and Steel Industry Association, it called on major domestic steel mills to proactively reduce production, leading to a weakening market mentality. It is expected that the short-term iron ore prices may continue to fluctuate weakly.
