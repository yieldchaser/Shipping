## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 09/02/2023
DCE iron ore futures rose largely today，the main contract closed 863，an increase of 2.62% throughout the day. some traders were active to sell,Some steel procurement more cautious, the continuation of a small number of multi-frequency procurement strategy. Today the overall market transaction atmosphere is acceptable. PBF at Shandong port dealt 870-875 yuan/mt; increased 1 0-15 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 876-882 yuan/mt; increased 15-20 yuan/mt over yesterday. SSF at Tangshan port dealt 758 yuan/mt; Although the recent construction of the project is less than expected and the demand for steel is recovering slowly, from today's apparent demand, the overall apparent demand for steel has reached the level of the week before the Spring Festival. The market sentiment has improved, and the supply of iron ore is in the oﬀ-season of seasonal shipment, while the demand is gradually increasing, and the fundamentals of ore prices are relatively strong. It is still easy to rise but difficult to fall in the short term.
