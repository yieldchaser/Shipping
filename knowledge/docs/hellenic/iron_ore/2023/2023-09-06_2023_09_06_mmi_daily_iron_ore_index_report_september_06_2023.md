## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 06/09/2023
DCE iron ore futures rose by 0.12% today, the main contract closed at 853.5. The traders' willingness to ship is high.The steel mills are active to purchase. The overall trading sentiment of the market is more.PBF at Shandong port deal 915-917 yuan/mt,increase 5-7 yuan/ mt. PBF at Tangshan port deal 920 yuan/mt,increase 6 yuan/mt. According to the news, the real estate sector in first tier cities is gradually recovering, and the sentiment in this sector has improved, which also supports the sentiment in the black market. As of today, the operating rate of blast furnaces surveyed by SMM is 93.46%, an increase of 0.3% compared to the previous period. The daily average iron production of the sample blast furnace steel plant increased slightly by 9000 tons, and the demand side has strengthened its support for ore prices. In addition, some steel mills' profits have recently been restored, supporting the enthusiasm for construction. We need to pay attention to the demand for finished products, and it is expected that the short-term mining prices will be strong and volatile.
