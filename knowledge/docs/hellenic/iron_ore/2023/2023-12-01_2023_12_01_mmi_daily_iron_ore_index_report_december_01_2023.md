## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 01/12/2023

## Main
DCE iron ore futures rose today, the main contract I2401 closed 975.5. an increase of 1.99% throughout the day.Traders ship according to market conditions. Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 983-985 yuan/mt; the same as yesterday. PBF at Shandong port dealt 1000 yuan/mt; increased 5 yuan/mt over yesterday. As of December 1st, SMM has tracked a total of 112.08 million tons of inventory in 35 ports, a cumulative increase of 1.59 million tons from last week and a decrease of 18.37 million tons from the same period last year. The current port inventory is still at a low level. However, the global shipping volume has been increasing in the past two weeks, and it is expected that there will still be some room for further increase in the arrival volume at the port. The supply is becoming increasingly loose. However, based on the iron production tracked by SMM, it is expected that there will still be a decrease next week, and in the short term, there will be a weakening of ore price support. Considering the current policy regulatory pressure, it is expected that there is still a risk of a pullback in mining prices. However, considering the strong market expectations for macro policies, it is expected that the downward space is limited.
