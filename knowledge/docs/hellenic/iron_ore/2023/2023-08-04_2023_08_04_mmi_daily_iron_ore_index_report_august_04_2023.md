## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 04/08/2023
DCE iron ore futures declined by 0.75 % today, the main contract closed at 725.5. The traders' willingness toship is general. The steel mills are not active to purchase. The overall trading sentiment of the market is less.PBF at Shandong port deal 850-855 yuan/mt,increase 15 yuan/mt. At present, the basic situation of iron ore is mainly characterized by strong supply and weak demand, and the snail ore ratio continues to weaken. Steel mills' profits will be compressed, which may further affect the demand side of iron ore. Although inventory has accumulated, supply and demand conflicts may gradually accumulate in the later stages. Considering that macro policy expectations still exist, it is difficult for the iron ore futures market to experience a significant decline. In addition, the main contract moved to another month this week, and the activity of I2401 contract continued to rise. At present, the conservative view is to view the weak 01 contract, but it is necessary to pay attention to the implementaƟon of the coarse steel level control policy and changes in steel mill profits.
