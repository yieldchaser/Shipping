## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 12/06/2023
DCE iron ore futures declined by 1.81% today, the main contract closed at785.5.The traders' willingness to ship is general.The steel mills are not active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is general. PBF at Shandong port deal 833-835 yuan/mt,decrease 18-20 yuan/mt. PBF at Tangshan port deal 850-856 yuan/mt,decrease 13 yuan/mt.According to SMM shipping data, the total global iron ore shipment volume of SMM last week was 29.41 million tons, a decrease of 12.9% month on month, with shipments from Australia and Brazil both decreasing. Last week, the total amount of iron ore arriving in China was 21.6086 million tons, a decrease of 7.91% compared to the previous week. On the demand side, the production of molten iron is expected to stabilize and strengthen this week, with fundamentals providing some support for ore prices. Macroeconomic data this week, the Federal Reserve's interest rate meeting, and other factors may affect market sentiment. In the early stage, the rapid rise in mineral prices needs to be prevented from policy regulation, and it is expected that mineral prices will continue to fluctuate in the short term.
