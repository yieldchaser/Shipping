## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 15/08/2023
DCE iron ore futures rose by 1.58 % today, the main contract closed at 740.The traders' willingness to ship is general.The steel mills are active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is better.PBF at Shandong port deal 830-835yuan/mt,increase 2-7 yuan/mt. PBF at Tangshan port deal 840 yuan/mt. Today's MLF interest rate cut has released a positive signal, which helps to enhance market confidence. The market believes that this interest rate cut will continue to reduce the financing costs of the real economy and promote the recovery of the economy. But today's macro data release shows that real estate development investment has decreased both month on month and year on year, hedging some optimistic sentiment. Considering the short-term strong support of supply and demand for mining prices, there is no definitive document on the level control policy, and there is no change in the procurement pace of steel mills. It is expected that the short-term mining prices will be mainly strong and volatile.
