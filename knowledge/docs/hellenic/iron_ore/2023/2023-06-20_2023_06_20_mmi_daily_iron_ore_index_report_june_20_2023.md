## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 20/06/2023
DCE iron ore futures open higher and go lower. futures tight fall today. the main contract I2309 closed 806.5. an decrease of 0.92% throughout theday; some traders quotation is still stronger; steel mills purchased on demand; today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 850-855 yuan/mt; decreased 0-5 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 875-880b yuan/mt; decreased 0-5 yuan/mt over yesterday. Today, the People's Bank of China lowered the market quoted interest rate (LPR) for oneyear and five-year loans by 10 basis points to 3.55% and 4.20%, respectively. It is good for real estate demand, but the market mentality is still weak in the off-season of terminal demand, combined with the impact of recent rainstorm and festivals in many parts of the country. In addition, the increase in coke prices and the contraction of steel mill profits have also put some pressure on ore prices. In the short term, high-speed iron still has some support for ore prices, and the ore prices are in a dilemma of rising and falling, mainly due to short-term high volatility.
