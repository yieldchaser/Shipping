## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 25/07/2023
DCE iron ore futures rose by 1.36 % today, the main contract closed at 856.5.The traders' willingness to ship is general.The steel mills are not active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is normal.PBF at Shandong port deal 895 yuan/mt,increase 5 yuan/mt. PBF at Tangshan port deal 910 yuan/mt,increase 7-10 yuan/mt. Recently, there have been new developments regarding the reduction of crude steel. According to SMM research, some steel mills in East China have received verbal notifications requesting crude production not to exceed 2022, but specific documents have not been issued yet, which has aﬀected the market mentality to some extent. Fundamentally speaking, the impact of blast furnace maintenance this week on molten iron is 1.1702 million tons, an increase of 167500 tons compared to last week's maintenance. The production of molten iron has decreased, and the demand for iron ore has decreased. And the profit of the steel factory has shrunk, and the procurement frequency has not changed. The pattern of increasing supply and decreasing demand, combined with the oﬀ-season of finished products, is difficult to form strong support for mineral prices. However, considering the volatility of macro news, the market is still trading with bullish incentives, and driven by this sentiment, iron ore prices may still ﬂuctuate at high levels.
