## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 21/06/2023
DCE iron ore futures fall today by 0.99%. the main contract I2309 closed 797.5. some traders were negative to sell, and quotation is still stronger;Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchasing enthusiasm is not high. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 845-855 yuan/mt; decreased 0-5 yuan/mt over yesterday. As a whole, near the Dragon Boat Festival holiday, some steel mills still have stage replenishment demand, but the demand for building materials is poor, and most steel mills are weak in replenishment. Superimposed pressure on production is expected to gradually fall, and the sustainability of iron ore demand remains to be seen. In addition, the market senƟment for macro policies has weakened, and iron ore has continued to be weak and volatile in the near future.
