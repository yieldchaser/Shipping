## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 16/03/2023
DCE iron ore futures declined by 2.8% today, the main contract closed at 902.The traders' willingness to ship is more.The steel mills are more activeto purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is general. PBF at Shandong port deal 912-916 yuan/mt, decrease 12-16 yuan/ mt.PBF at Tangshan port deal 932-935 yuan/mt,decrease 15-17 yuan/mt. Overseas banking events triggered market panic, commodity prices fell and iron ore prices fell. Fundamentals According to the inventory data of the top ten ports tracked by SMM, the port inventory of the four major varieties decreased slightly to 93.8 million tons, only the pellet of the four major varieties accumulated slightly, and the iron ore supply and demand fundamentals changed little. At present, the main focus of the market is on whether the overseas economy has a better way to solve the crisis. It is expected that the iron ore price will fluctuate widely in the short term.
