## Main
in Weekly Vessel Valuations Report 11/01/2022
Image reference: 2022-01-11_weekly-vessel-valuations-report-january-11-2022_img1.png
Tanker: Suezmax values have firmed.
VLCC New Talisman (296,100 DWT, Sep 2009, Bohai Shipbuilding Heavy Industry Co) sold to Aeolos Management for USD 36.00 mil, VV value USD 34.67 mil.
Small Clean Tankers Northsea Alpha and North Sea Beta (8,600 DWT, May 2010, Kejin Shipyard) sold in an en bloc deal to Far Eastern buyers for USD 8.90 mil, VV value USD 10.92 mil.
Bulker: Modern Bulker values have firmed.
Panamax HH Pioneer (72,900 DWT, Oct 1997, Daedong) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 8.50 mil, VV value USD 9.41 mil - BWTS fitted.
Ultramax Ikan Senyur (61,500 DWT, Sep 2010, Shin Kasado Dock) sold to Far Eastern buyers for USD 22.00 mil, VV value USD 23.80 mil - BWTS Fitted.
Supramax Sparrow (53,500 DWT, Apr 2005, Imabari) sold for USD 13.50 mil, VV value USD 15.26 mil.
Handy Bulker Longshore (34,400 DWT, Jan 2010, SPP) sold to European buyers 15.70 mil, VV value USD 15.80 mil - BWTS Fitted.
Container: Container values have remained stable.
No Sales to report this week.
