Main
Bulkers: Strong pricing continued to be observed across vintage tonnage, leading to our values for older vessels firming.Softer pricing was evident in the sale of the Ehime Queen to Chinese buyers, resulting in a dip in our modern Capesize values. Capesize Ehime Queen (181,200 DWT, 2016, Imabari) sold to Chinese SS/DD Passed for USD 57.5 mil, VV Value USD 59.9 mil.
