## Summary
Main
Bulkers: Bulker secondhand activity remained busy this week, with transactions spanning Panamax to Newcastlemaxtonnage and Chinese buyers featuring prominently across multiple segments. Resale appetite also remained evident, with two 64,100 DWT Ultramax newbuilds from New Dayang Shipbuilding sold to Niovis Shipping, though soft pricing resulted in a dip to our Ultramax resale values. Capesizes RTM Carrier (205,500 DWT, 2012, Hanjin Subic) and RTM Zheng He (205,400 DWT, 2012, Hanjin Subic) sold to Chinese buyers, latter DD Passed, for USD 90 mil, VV Value USD 89 mil
Supramaxes Hull 1315 (64,100 DWT, 2027, New Dayang) and Hull 1316 (64,100 DWT, 2027, New Dayang) resold to Niovis Shipping for USD 73 mil, VV Value USD 73.6 mil
Supramax ASL Ixora (61,500 DWT, 2012, Shin Kasado Dock) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 19.6 mil, VV Value USD 19.5 mil
Handysize KS Grace (36,300 DWT, 2014, Shikoku Dockyard) sold to Greek buyers for USD 16.95 mil, VV Value USD 17.1 mil
Tankers: The secondhand tanker market is showing broad strength this week, with values firming across mid-size and older tonnage in particular.
