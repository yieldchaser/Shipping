Tankers: Despite the fluctuation in earnings this week, Tanker values held their firming trend across all segments. S&P activity has slowed down in the past number of weeks with only midsize/larger tonnage reported this week.

Containers: Second-hand values remain stable this week across all size segments. Handy container newbuilding values have picked up a little this week after a couple of new orders have been announced.
