## Main
in Weekly Vessel Valuations Report 21/12/2021
Image reference: 2021-12-21_weekly-vessel-valuations-report-december-21-2021-2_img1.png
Tanker: Mid-Age VLCC, Aframax and MR values have firmed.
VLCCs Hull 8052 and Hull 8053 (300,000 DWT, May 2022, Hyundai Samho Heavy Ind) sold to Greek buyers in an en bloc deal for USD 196.00 mil, VV value USD 197.18 mil - Resale, Scrubber Fitted.
LR2 Prosky (115,600 DWT, Jan 2019, Daehan) sold to Neda Maritime for USD 51.00 mil, VV value USD 51.54 mil - DD Due, BWTS Fitted.
Bulker: Panamax BC values have remained stable.
Ultramax Nord Columbia (60,400 DWT, Feb 2018, Oshima) sold to Singaporean buyers for USD 32.00 mil, VV value USD 34.53 mil - BWTS and Scrubber Fitted.
Supramaxes Pacific Bless and Pacific Crown (56,400 / 56,500 DWT, May - Sep 2012, Jiangsu Hantong Ship Heavy Industry) sold to Chinese buyers in an en bloc deal for USD 34.50 mil, VV value 34.64 mil - SS/DD Due.
Handy Bulker Wuhu (39,200 DWT, Mar 2014, Chengxi Shipyard) sold for USD 21.00 mil, VV value USD 19.97 mill - DD due.
Handy Bulker Bao Da (28,100 DWT, Apr 2001, Bohai Shipbuilding Heavy Industry Co) sold to Greek buyers for USD 6.50 mil, VV value USD 7.44 mil - DD Due.
Container: Modern Post Panamax values have firmed.
No sales to report this week.
