## Main
in Weekly Vessel Valuations Report 02/11/2021
Image reference: 2021-11-02_weekly-vessel-valuations-report-november-02-2021_img1.png
Tanker: Tanker values are stable.
VLCCs Seaways Liberty (301,000 DWT, Aug 2016, SWS), Seaways Triton and Seaways Cape Henry (300,900 DWT, Jan - Mar 2016, SWS), Seaways Hendricks and Seaways Diamond Head (300,800 DWT, May - Oct 2016, SWS) and Seaways Tybee (300,700 DWT, Oct 2015, SWS) sold to Ocean Yield ASA in an en bloc deal for USD 375.00 mil, VV en bloc value USD 387.78 mil - BBB.
Aframaxes Advantage Arrow and Advantage Avenue (115,800 DWT, Nov 2009 - Jun 2010, Samsung) sold to Bergshav AS in an en bloc deal for USD 52.00 mil, VV en bloc value USD 44.37 mil - Inc Charter.
Bulker: Smaller Bulkers values have softened.
Capesize MP The Hightower and MP The Kraft (209,200 DWT, Jan 2020, Jiangsu Yangzi Xinfu) sold in an en bloc deal for USD 130.00 mil, VV en bloc value USD 129.69 mil.
Capesize Cape Spring (180,100 DWT, Sep 2011, Qingdao Beihai Shipbuilding HI), sold for USD 30.10 mil, VV value USD 30.04 mil - SS/DD Passed.
Panamax Key Discovery (82,200 DWT, Nov 2010, Tadotsu Tsuneishi), sold to Greek buyers for USD 24.50 mil, VV value USD 24.94 mil.
Container: Panamax values have softened.
No transactions to report.
