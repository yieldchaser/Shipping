## Main
in Weekly Vessel Valuations Report 19/10/2021
Image reference: 2021-10-19_weekly-vessel-valuations-report-october-19-2021_img1.png
Tanker: Tanker values remained stable.
LR2s Navig8 Pride LHJ and Navig8 Providence (110,600 DWT, Aug 2018, New Times Shipbuilding) sold to Global Meridian Holdings in an en bloc deal for USD 96.00 mil, VV en bloc value USD 91.18 mil - Inc Charter.
MR2s Eco City Of Angels and Eco Los Angeles (50,300 DWT, Feb 2020, Hyundai Mipo) sold to Norden in an en bloc deal for USD 73.00 mil, VV en bloc value USD 70.85 mil - Inc Charter.
Bulker: Bulker values have firmed.
Capesize Cape Garland (178,400 DWT, Jan 2009, Mitsui Ichihara) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 32.25 mil, VV value USD 27.61 mil - DD Passed, BWTS fitted.
Capesize Bluemoon (170,000 DWT, Mar 2002, IHI) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 15.75 mil, VV value USD 13.97 mil - SS/DD Due.
Panamax Ibis Wind (82,900 DWT, Nov 2013, Sanoyas) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 28.50 mil, VV value USD 29.67 mil.
Panamax Lena B (81,900 DWT, Jan 2017, Tsuneishi Zhoushan) sold to Far Eastern buyers for USD 35.00 mil, VV value USD 34.91 mil - BWTS fitted.
Panamaxes Golden Endurer (79,500 DWT, Jan 2011, Jinhai Intelligent Manufacturing) and Golden Opportunity (75,500 DWT, Aug 2008, Jiangsu Rongsheng) sold to Greek buyers in an en bloc deal for USD 37.20 mil, VV value USD 36.88 mil - BWTS fitted.
Supramax Mariner (56,800 DWT, Jul 2009, Jiangsu Hantong Ship HI) sold to Asian buyers for USD 16.75 mil, VV value USD 15.32 mil - BWTS fitted.
Handy Bulker Xing Ning Hai (34,400 DWT, Jul 2015, Namura) sold for USD 23.00 mil, VV value USD 24.15 mil.
Container: Post Panamax values have firmed.
Handy Viking Merlin (1,740 TEU, Apr 2014, Guangzhou Wenchong) sold to CMA CGM for USD 33.00 mil, VV value USD 35.89 mil - DD passed.
