Tankers: Further softening of older tonnage Aframax and LR2 values as earnings continue to decline
No notable sales last week
Containers: Marginal gains for Panamax and larger segments across all ages. However, values softened for Sub Panamax and Handy's in response to recent sales and softer earnings
Sub Panamax Puerto Limon Express (2,592 TEU, Nov 2009, Hyundai HI Ulsan Shipyard) sold to an undisclosed buyer for USD 22.84 mil (SS/DD due), VV Value USD 24.4 mil
