## Main
in Weekly Vessel Valuations Report 02/04/2025
Image reference: 2025-04-02_weekly-vessel-valuations-report-april-01-2025_img1.png
Bulkers: Bulker Values have firmed this week, with Midage Handysizes seeing the most significate increase again this weekowing to sales of vessels such as Atlantic Brave and Nimble Nicky.
Capesize (Newcastlemax) Global Commande (208,000 DWT, Jun 2010, Universal) sold SS/DD Due to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 32 mil, VV Value USD 31.54 mil.
Panamax BC Nautilus (73,400 DWT, May 2001, Sumitomo) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 6.5 mil, VV Value USD 5.78mil
Supramax BC Lowlands Opal (5504000 DWT, Nov 2007, Oshima) to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 11.4 mil, VV Value USD 11.13 mil

Tankers: Tanker values have remained mostly stable over the last week with older tonnage Suezmax and Aframax values firming owing to recent deals
Suezmax Seacross (163,300 DWT, Jan 2006, Hyundai Samho HI) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 33 mil, VV Value USD 29.79 mil
Aframax P Yanbu (105,400 DWT, Jan 2011, Sumitomo) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 39 mil, VV Value USD 35.8 mil
Containers: Older Panamaxes are continuing up this week, smaller second-hand vessels nudging up too
