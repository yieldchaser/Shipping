## Main
in Weekly Vessel Valuations Report 16/10/2025
Image reference: 2025-10-16_weekly-vessel-valuations-report-october-14-2025_img1.png
Bulkers: VV Bulker values have mostly remained stable this week. With Capesizes and Supramaxesboth seeing an increase due to sales such as Mineral Shougang and Forever SW
Capesize BC (Newcastlemax) Mineral Shougang (206,400 DWT, Oct 2019, CMI Qingdao Shipyard) sold to Winning Shipping for USD 65.5 mil, VV Value USD 64.06 mil
Panamax BC Atheras (74,500 DWT, Sep 2006, Hudong Zhonghua) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 8.5 mil, VV Value USD 8.91 mil
Supramax BC Forever SW (58,500 DWT, May 2010, Tsuneishi Cebu) sold to unknown Greek buyers for USD 15.2 mil, VV Value USD 14.36 mil
Handy BC Eurosky (33,700 DWT, Aug 2011, Samjin Shipbuilding Industries) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 10.2 mil, VV Value USD 9.95 mil
Tankers: S&P activity has slowed this week and so the majority of VV Tanker values remain stable. The market seems cautious with buyers being selective and owners holding back.
VLCC Landbridge Horizon (308,400 DWT, Aug 2019, Dalian Shipbuilding Industry Corp) sold to Trafigura Beheer BV for USD 103 mil, VV Value USD 103.75 mil
Aframax Apsogos (115,400 DWT, Jul 2004, Samsung) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 22 mil, VV Value USD 22.94 mil
Containers: Midage and older Handysize and Sub Panamax values on the rise this week, alongside an increase in newbuilding values for the same. The rest of the sector holding steady.
Panamax Bharani (3,635 TEU, Jul 2010, Shanghai Shipyard) sold to Global Feeder Shipping for USD 30 mil Inc TC, VV Value USD 34 mil
Panamax Newnew Star (3,534 TEU, Oct 2007, Shanghai Shipyard) and Sub Panamax Xin Xin Tian 2 (2,401 TEU, Apr 2007, Naikai Setoda) sold en bloc to MSC for USD 55 mil, VV Value USD 50.3 mil
