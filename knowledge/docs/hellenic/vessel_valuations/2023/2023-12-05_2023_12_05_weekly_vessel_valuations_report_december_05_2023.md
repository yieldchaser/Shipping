## Main
Bulkers : Newer Capesize and Panamax continue to increase owing to high prices for vessels such as Aquavita Sol/Sea. Other vessels remain stable.
Spike in rates said to be causing a stall in sales as owners wait for S&P market momentum to build
Panamax BC Aquavita Sky and Aquavita Sea (81,500 DWT, Jan/Mar 2020, Jiangsu Hantong) sold in En Bloc deal to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 61.0 mil, VV Value USD 57.3 mil
Supramax BC NPS Mosa (53,600 DWT, Jun 2007, Iwagi Zosen) sold to unknown South Korean buyers for USD 11.60 mil, VV Value USD 12.11 mil
Handy BC Navios Lyra (34,700 DWT, Jun 2012, SPP) sold to unknown Middle Eastern buyers for USD 13.75 mil, VV Value USD 13.7 mil
Tankers : Tanker values remained stable last week with only notable change being slight softening of older VLCC tonnage
VLCC Harad (303,000 DWT, Oct 2001, Samsung) sold to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 30 mil (BWTS), VV Value USD 35.07 mil
MR2 (Chem/Product) Dee4 Mahogany & Dee4 Ilex (50,300 DWT, Sep & Jan 2022, Hyundai Mipo) sold to GNMTC in an en bloc deal for USD 108.00 mil(On Subs), VV Value USD 99.74 mil
MR2 (Chem/Product) King Gregory & Lady Malou (49,800 & 51,500 DWT, Nov 2012 & Jan 2013, Hyundai Mipo) sold to undisclosed buyers in an en bloc deal for USD 68.00 mil, VV Value USD 68.08 mil
Small Clean (Chem/Product) Loi Hope (9,000 DWT, Dec 2008, Kwang Sung) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 8.60 mil (SS/DD Passed), VV Value USD 9.25 mil
Containers : ULCV and New Panamax Resales firmed 0.9% and 1.7% respectively. Modern 5YO Feedermax gained 7.5% based on recent sales. Whilst Mid-age 10YO Post Panamax, Panamax, Sub Panamax and Handys lost c.0.8% on average
Panamax Northern Decency (4,173 TEU, Apr 2003, Hyundai HI) sold to Chinese buyer for USD 12.65 mil (BWTS), VV Value USD 11.03 mil
