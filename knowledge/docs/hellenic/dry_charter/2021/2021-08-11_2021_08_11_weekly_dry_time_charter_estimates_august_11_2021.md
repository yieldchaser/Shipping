## Summary
Main
Another stable week for the capesize period supported by short-term sentiment in the paper market and plenty of demand in the Pacific. The kamsarmax/panamax period rates moved up this week and despite many players being out of the market due to the summer season, there have been some short-term fixtures reported in the panamax sector. The average rate for one-year in the pacific moved up this week to $26,000/pdpr.

## Main
Another stable week for the capesize period supported by short-term sentiment in the paper market and plenty of demand in the Pacific.
The kamsarmax/panamax period rates moved up this week and despite many players being out of the market due to the summer season, there have been some short-term fixtures reported in the panamax sector. The average rate for one-year in the pacific moved up this week to $26,000/pdpr.
Despite the summer period being well under way, rates have remained stable this week and sentiment remains positive across both basins.
