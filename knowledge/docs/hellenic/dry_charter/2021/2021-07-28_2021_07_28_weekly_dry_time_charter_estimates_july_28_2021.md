## Summary
Main
The capesize period market has experienced an uptick this week thanks to increased iron ore cargoes out of Australia and weather-related delaysin the pacific. A slightly softer week for the kamsarmax/panamax market with little fresh demand to report aside from some activity in the Black Sea and also East Coast South America. The smaller sizes saw some support from port congestion due to the typhoon in the pacific and a strengthening coal market out of Indonesia.
