## Summary
Main
The capesize period market remained firm this week as port congestion in China lead to a tonnage deficit in the pacific. Despite a shorter week for many due to public holidays this week, the kamsarmax/panamax period market has remained stable. Demand in the Atlantic softened towards the end of last week with tonnage lists growing however, the market in the pacific remained firm plenty of activity out of southeast Asia and Indonesia.
