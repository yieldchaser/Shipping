## Summary
Main
Capesize TC rates continued to firm slightly this week. However we expect sentiment in the period market to dip in the coming weeks, following the pattern of the spot rates as the market winds down ahead of the Christmas period. A quiet week for the panamax/kamsarmax sector and rates moved sideways although the underlying sentiment remains positive.

## Main
Capesize TC rates continued to firm slightly this week. However we expect sentiment in the period market to dip in the coming weeks, following the pattern of the spot rates as the market winds down ahead of the Christmas period.
A quiet week for the panamax/kamsarmax sector and rates moved sideways although the underlying sentiment remains positive.
A stronger week for the smaller sizes, particularly the ultramax timecharter sector that is seeing a final push ahead of the upcoming festivities.
