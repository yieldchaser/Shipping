## Summary
Main
The capesize marker continued to come under pressure this week due to little activity out of Brazil and an oversupply of tonnage. Due to a lack of demand, the panamax/kamsarmax timecharter market has yet to find a floor and continued to fall this week. A mixed week for the smaller sizes with sentiment positive in Asia but certain areas including the Eastern Mediterranean lacked fresh enquiry.
