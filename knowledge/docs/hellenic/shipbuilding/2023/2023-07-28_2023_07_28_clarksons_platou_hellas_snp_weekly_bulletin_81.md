Recycling
Hit and Miss!
Certain vessels achieve a certain price sometimes in this market and quite clearly, this week portrays why each
sale can be individual, and not down to market conditions. It is reported a small container vessel of about 4,600
ldt has been committed basis delivery Bangladesh for region USD 622.00 per ldt, creating a huge spread across
all tonnage types. If this is indeed correct, then some reasoning unbeknown to many must be the answer as it
would seem this far exceeds current pricing sentiment. The only possible explanation is that for such a small
unit, a Letter of Credit can be opened from Bangladesh.
Why are we surprised? There have been reports of many units now sitting outside Chattogram (some for 4-6
weeks) waiting for L/C's to be opened and as such, several being diverted to India where they are offering
lower rates which clearly shows the difficulties recyclers are facing from their Banks.
