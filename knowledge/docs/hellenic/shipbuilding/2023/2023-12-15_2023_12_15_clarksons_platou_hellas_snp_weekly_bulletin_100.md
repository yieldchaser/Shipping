New Building
In gas carriers this week, Hyundai HI announced contracting three firm 98k CBM VLECs with an unnamed owner, reported to be MOL, with
the vessels all expected to have delivered by June 2027. Nantong CIMC SOE announced contracting a firm 12.5k CBM LNG Bunkering vessel
for Scale Gas. The vessel will have electric propulsion and is set to deliver within 2026. Dalian SB Offshore announced contracting one firm
plus one optional 7.5k CBM LCO2 Carrier for Schulte Group (for Northern Lights), with the vessel slated for delivery in mid-2026.
In MPPs, SAL Heavy Lift announced exercising their optional 14.6k dwt MPP at Wuhu Shipyard, with the vessel expected to deliver in June
2025.

Recycling
No Tidings!
It feels like there is a lack of ‘everything' at the moment as there is little activity to report, few sales to report, the shores of the Indian
sub-Continent starved of tonnage and few candidates being circulated for sale.
As such, we cannot provide any tidings of joy for the recycling industry, and we look set to tiptoe into the New Year.
We are seeing a slight increase in price indications from India and in addition, Turkey, as the lack of tonnage seems to be whetting the
recyclers appetite to try and draw some tonnage to their yards.
