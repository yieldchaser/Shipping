Recycling
Metronomic!
There is no change to the rhythmic pattern experienced over many weeks from the regular reporting of slow activity,
lack of tonnage, etc as the Eid Al-Adha festivities that are taking place this week have created even quieter market
conditions.
Despite the odd trickle of container units rippling into the market, which is evidenced by the sale reported below, this
lack of available tonnage certainly looks set to continue for the next few months whilst Owners focus their attention on
their summer holidays.
One positive news generates around the entry into force of the Hong Kong International Convention for the Safe and
Environmentally Sound Recycling of Ships (HKC). With Bangladesh, and thereafter, Liberia, ratifying the Convention, we
must now wait for 24 months under the IMO regulations (after the entry comes into force) that the HKC will become
maritime law. A truly momentous occasion!
Meantime, we wish all those currently celebrating this weeks festivities, a Happy Eid Al-Adha.
