New Building
In tankers this week, it was reported that CSSC Dalian contracted two firm 320k dwt VLCC's with clients of Dynacom
Tankers, with the vessels set to deliver in 4Q of 2026 and 1Q of 2027.
In dry bulk, Chengxi Shipyard announced ordering two firm 82k dwt Kamsarmaxes at Agricore Shipping, with the vessels
expected to deliver in 2026.
In the gas carrier market, NYK announced ordering one firm LPG dual fuel 86k CMB VLGC at Kawasaki HI, with the vessels
set to deliver in 2026. It brings their series to six at the yard. Hyundai Mipo announced contracts for two firm 22k CBM LCO2
Carriers with clients of Capital Maritime. The vessels will also be able to carry Ammonia and LPG and will deliver in 2026.
Finally, in containers, Samsung HI announced contracting sixteen firm methanol dual fuel 16,000 TEU containerships, with the
vessels slated to deliver throughout 2026 and 2027. Evergreen Marine also announced ordering eight firm methanol dual
fuel 16,000 TEU containerships at Nihon Shipyard, with the vessels also set to deliver throughout 2026 and 2027.

Recycling
Lack of any Revs!
With another lacklustre week in the ship recycling industry, at least the world of sport is this week throwing up many
different events: the Ashes, Formula 1 racing, the Women's Football World Cup and the British Golf Open Championship,
to entertain and divert the boredom away from the market place.
Price levels are reportedly falling further in India on the back of the monsoon season hampering the destination and
also, the weakened domestic steel market. Interestingly, there are also rumours of several vessels being re-routed from
Chattogram towards India due to ongoing Letter of Credit restrictions and once again, it would appear financing in
Bangladesh is difficult for tonnage over 10,000 ldt.
With Pakistan remaining absent from the bidding table, there really is a sense of concern during these summer months
as to where the available tonnage can be concluded to and perhaps, the shortage of tonnage at this current time is a
blessing in disguise.
