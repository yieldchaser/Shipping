## Main
Dry Cargo
Another 15-year-old Capesize has changed hands this week, highlighting the continued strong demandfor vessels of this vintage over the last couple of months. The mv Rosemary (abt 179k dwt, built 2010 Daewoo) was inviting offers earlier this month and, following healthy competition with more than ten bids received, has now been sold for around USD 28.5m.
In the geared segment, a couple of Supramax sales were concluded. The Japanese-controlled mv Atacama Queen (abt 5k dwt, built 2011 Imabari, OHBH) has been sold at USD 14.5m while the older mv Evriali (abt 53k dwt, built 2007 Iwagi) has changed hands at USD 10.3m, with dry dock due promptly in November.
Tanker
A comparatively quiet week on the tankers with no confirmed sales reported. However, market rumors indicate a 20-year-old Suezmax having been committed for a price in the mid USD 29 m's range.
It will be interesting to observe price levels in the coming weeks, particularly for crude carriers, as sentiment remains firm amid the ongoing strength in the chartering market.
Source: Clarkson Platou (Hellas) ltd
