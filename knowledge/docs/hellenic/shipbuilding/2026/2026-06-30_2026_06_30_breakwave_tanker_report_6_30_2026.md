• Our Long-term View - The tanker market has been recovering from a long period
of staggered rates as the growth in new vessel supply shrunk while oil demand

freight rates has led to significant new vessel ordering, with the orderbook now

supply/demand misbalance is small, we expect a meaningful negative balance to
develop longer term leading to a industry downcycle.
