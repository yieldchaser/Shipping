New Building
In Dry this week, Shanhaiguan have announced an order for 4 firm 37,000dwt lakes fitted handysize bulkers from existing client
Polish Steamship. The vessels are expected to deliver in 2025. U-Ming meanwhile have announced an order for a pair of
64,000 dwt Ultramax bulkers at New Dayang Shipyard, these are their 3rd and 4th in the series and will deliver in Nov 2024 and
April 2025 respectively. Finally, CITIC leasing are reported to have returned to Dalian for a further 5 x 65,000dwt bulkers all for
delivery in 2025, bringing their total number of vessels on order at the yard up to 15 vessels now.
In Gas, HMD have announced an order from an un-named European owner for a pair of LPG carriers. The vessels are
understood to be 45,000 cbm in size and the first of these is expected to deliver in 4Q 2024, with the second in 1Q 2025.
In Containers, Wuchang have announced an order with clients of Cosmoship for 2+2 x 1,180 TEU Container carriers. Both
vessels are expected to deliver in 2024.
Lastly, in the passenger carrier market, CMJL (Weihai) have announced an order for one 194 passenger RoPax placed by
China-Japan International which again is expected to deliver in 2024.
