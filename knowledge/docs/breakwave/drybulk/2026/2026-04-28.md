## Summary
The dry bulk shipping sector is experiencing a significant collapse in volatility to multi-year lows despite high absolute price levels, driven largely by equilibrium in the freight futures market. Breakwave maintains a cautious outlook for the second half of 2026, anticipating an economic slowdown in Asian markets due to impending energy shocks and cooling global demand. While short-term fundamentals are viewed negatively, the long-term outlook expects secular tightness and higher volatility due to geopolitical uncertainty and steady fleet growth.
